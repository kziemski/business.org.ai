## Industry Variations

Business capability development in retail focuses on operational excellence and innovation:

- **Omnichannel Retailers**: Build unified commerce capabilities, develop data analytics competencies, and create seamless customer experiences
- **Department Stores**: Enhance experiential retail capabilities, develop private label competencies, and build personal shopping services
- **Specialty Retailers**: Cultivate product expertise, develop customer relationship capabilities, and build trend forecasting competencies
- **Discount/Mass Retailers**: Optimize operational efficiency, enhance supply chain capabilities, and develop private brand competencies
- **Grocery/Supermarkets**: Build fresh food preparation capabilities, develop e-commerce fulfillment competencies, and enhance health and wellness offerings
- **Convenience Stores**: Develop foodservice capabilities, enhance mobile payment competencies, and build delivery partnerships

Key retail capability considerations include digital transformation initiatives, workforce skill development, and customer experience innovation.
