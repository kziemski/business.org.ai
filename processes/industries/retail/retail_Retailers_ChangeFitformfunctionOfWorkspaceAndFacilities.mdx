## Industry Variations

Asset management in retail involves significant real estate and store fixture investments:

- **Omnichannel Retailers**: Balance physical store investments with fulfillment infrastructure, optimize store portfolio for ship-from-store capabilities
- **Department Stores**: Manage anchor lease relationships, maintain flagship property investments, and coordinate capital improvements
- **Specialty Retailers**: Invest in branded store environments, manage mall-based lease portfolios, and deploy modular fixture systems
- **Discount/Mass Retailers**: Optimize standardized store formats, manage large parking facilities, and maintain efficient warehouse assets
- **Grocery/Supermarkets**: Maintain refrigeration and cold storage assets, manage fuel station infrastructure, and coordinate equipment lifecycle
- **Convenience Stores**: Oversee fuel dispensing equipment, maintain 24/7 facility operations, and manage high-turnover equipment

Key retail asset considerations include lease portfolio optimization, store remodel ROI analysis, and technology refresh cycles for POS equipment.
