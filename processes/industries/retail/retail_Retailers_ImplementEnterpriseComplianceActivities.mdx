## Industry Variations

Risk and compliance management in retail addresses unique operational exposures:

- **Omnichannel Retailers**: Manage cybersecurity across digital channels, comply with data privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA), and address fulfillment liability
- **Department Stores**: Coordinate vendor compliance, manage brand protection, and address slip-and-fall liability in large facilities
- **Specialty Retailers**: Comply with product safety regulations, manage import compliance, and address specialized industry requirements
- **Discount/Mass Retailers**: Implement loss prevention at scale, manage vendor compliance programs, and address supply chain risk
- **Grocery/Supermarkets**: Ensure food safety compliance, manage cold chain risk, and address product recall procedures
- **Convenience Stores**: Comply with fuel and tobacco regulations, manage age verification, and address robbery prevention

Key retail risk considerations include PCI-DSS compliance, organized retail crime prevention, and product liability management.
