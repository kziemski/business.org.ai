## Industry Variations

Product and merchandise development in retail varies significantly by format:

- **Omnichannel Retailers**: Develop products for both digital presentation and in-store experience, with unified product information management (PIM)
- **Department Stores**: Coordinate across multiple buyers and brand partnerships, manage private label development alongside national brands
- **Specialty Retailers**: Focus on curated assortments, trend-driven merchandise calendars, and exclusive product development
- **Discount/Mass Retailers**: Emphasize cost-optimized sourcing, bulk purchasing, and efficiency-driven product selection
- **Grocery/Supermarkets**: Manage perishable lifecycles, local sourcing relationships, and category management partnerships with CPG suppliers
- **Convenience Stores**: Optimize limited shelf space, focus on high-velocity items, and manage impulse purchase positioning

Key retail considerations include merchandise planning systems, open-to-buy management, and vendor collaboration through category management.
