## Industry Variations

Physical product delivery and fulfillment in retail involves specialized operations:

- **Omnichannel Retailers**: Implement ship-from-store, buy-online-pickup-in-store (BOPIS), curbside pickup, and same-day delivery capabilities
- **Department Stores**: Manage complex receiving operations, distributed inventory across sales floors, and white-glove delivery services
- **Specialty Retailers**: Focus on branded packaging, gift services, and specialized handling for unique product categories
- **Discount/Mass Retailers**: Optimize high-volume distribution centers, cross-docking operations, and efficient store replenishment
- **Grocery/Supermarkets**: Handle cold chain logistics, fresh product rotation (FIFO), and time-sensitive delivery windows
- **Convenience Stores**: Manage frequent small-lot deliveries, direct-store-delivery (DSD) relationships, and limited backroom operations

Key retail fulfillment considerations include inventory allocation algorithms, store clustering strategies, and last-mile delivery partnerships.
