Health insurance service delivery is distinguished by:
- **Claims Processing**: Medical coding (ICD-10, CPT), pre-authorization, and adjudication rules
- **Care Management**: Disease management, case management, and utilization review
- **Provider Relations**: Credentialing, contracting, and network management
- **Member Services**: Benefits interpretation, grievances, and appeals handling
