Health insurance sales and marketing differs through:
- **Open Enrollment Periods**: Annual enrollment windows for individual and Medicare products
- **Broker Relationships**: Heavy reliance on independent agents and brokers
- **Group vs Individual**: Different sales cycles for employer groups vs individual members
- **Compliance Requirements**: Marketing material approval and agent licensing requirements
