IT in health insurance must support:
- **Claims Systems**: Core administrative platforms for claims processing
- **EDI Transactions**: HIPAA-mandated electronic data interchange (837, 835, 270/271)
- **Interoperability**: Provider data exchange and patient access requirements
- **Security**: HIPAA security rule compliance and PHI protection
