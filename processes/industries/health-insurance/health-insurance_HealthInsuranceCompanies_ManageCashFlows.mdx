## Industry Variations

Financial management in health insurance involves:
- **Medical Loss Ratio**: Tracking and reporting MLR for ACA compliance
- **Reserve Management**: IBNR (Incurred But Not Reported) and claim reserves
- **Risk-Based Capital**: State-mandated capital adequacy requirements
- **Premium Accounting**: Earned vs unearned premium calculations
