## Industry Variations

Customer service in health insurance requires:
- **Clinical Expertise**: Understanding of medical terminology and procedures
- **Regulatory Compliance**: HIPAA privacy requirements and grievance procedures
- **Multi-Channel Support**: Phone, portal, mobile app, and provider-facing systems
- **Appeals Management**: Internal and external review processes for denied claims
