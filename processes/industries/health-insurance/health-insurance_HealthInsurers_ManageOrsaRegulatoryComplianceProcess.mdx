## Industry Variations

Risk and compliance in health insurance encompasses:
- **Regulatory Compliance**: State DOI examinations, CMS audits, and NAIC requirements
- **Fraud Prevention**: Special Investigations Units (SIU) and provider fraud detection
- **Medical Management**: Utilization review and medical necessity determinations
- **Enterprise Risk**: Underwriting risk, operational risk, and market risk
