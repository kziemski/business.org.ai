## Industry Variations

In health insurance, strategy development must account for:
- **Regulatory Landscape**: State and federal insurance regulations (ACA, ERISA, state mandates)
- **Risk Pool Management**: Balancing profitable and high-risk member populations
- **Network Strategy**: Provider network adequacy requirements and competitive positioning
- **Product Portfolio**: Medicare Advantage, Medicaid, individual, group, and specialty products
