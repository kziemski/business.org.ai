Health insurance product development is uniquely shaped by:

- **Benefit Design**: Essential health benefits, cost-sharing structures, and formulary management
- **Regulatory Approval**: State insurance department rate and form filings
- **Network Configuration**: In-network vs out-of-network benefit differentials
