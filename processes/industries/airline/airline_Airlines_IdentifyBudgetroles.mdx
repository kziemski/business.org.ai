# Identify budget/roles

## Overview

Creating a plan of financial outlay for the newly defined roles. Determine the amount of capital the organization is willing to invest in effectuating these new roles, how would the roles be financed, and what would comprise the ROI from these flows. Coordinate personnel responsible for change management and the finance division. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
