# Manage suppliers

## Overview

Collecting and analyzing new information in order to track and rate suppliers through a supplier information management system. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
