# Assign and support process ownership

## Overview

Assigning resources (employees) ownership of tasks. These include the responsibility of identifying, analyzing, and improving business processes in order to meet the goals and objectives such as increasing profits and performance, reducing costs, and accelerating schedules. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
