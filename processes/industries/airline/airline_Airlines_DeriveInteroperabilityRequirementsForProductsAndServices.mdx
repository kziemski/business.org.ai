# Derive interoperability requirements for products and services

## Overview

Determining the ability of products and services to work together, exchange and use information in a multi-vendor, multi-network, and multi-service environment. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
