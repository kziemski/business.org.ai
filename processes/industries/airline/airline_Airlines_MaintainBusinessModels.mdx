# Maintain business models

## Overview

Revising and updating business models to reflect the changes in the marketed services, product inventory, market behavior, available resources or accrued expenses. Determine how and when to modify the accepted business model in response to incoming feedback. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
