# Develop IT transformation plans

## Overview

Developing a robust plan to replace or upgrade an organization's information technology systems. Understanding the business need of IT transformation from current to an expected state for the business. Developing a strategic plan for IT operating model, governance, service delivery, and workforce transformation. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
