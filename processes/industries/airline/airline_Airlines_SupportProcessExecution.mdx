# Support process execution

## Overview

Assisting and executing the business processes. Use business process execution language (BEPL), which is a standard, executable language for specifying actions within the business processes with the use of web services. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
