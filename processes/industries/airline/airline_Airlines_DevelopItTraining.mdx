# Develop IT training

## Overview

Create and manage employee training programs by considering the need and availability of these programs. Manage all aspects related to the training programs. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
