# Evaluate new product/service inputs and requirements

## Overview

Assessing and reviewing the required inputs and necessary elements such as automation, technology, hardware installation, regulatory requirements, certifications, etc., for new products/services through defined process and analysis. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
