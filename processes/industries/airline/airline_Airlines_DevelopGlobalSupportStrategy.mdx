# Develop global support strategy

## Overview

Developing a plan to deploy support services and support functions throughout the organization globally. Arrange the organizations functional support areas to create efficiencies of scale in the delivery of support services, globally. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
