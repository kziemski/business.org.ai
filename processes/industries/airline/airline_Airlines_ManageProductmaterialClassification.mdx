# Manage product/material classification

## Overview

Controlling the details of the product and the input materials. Manage classification throughout the production process, and for future accessibility to new product development or product enrichment. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
