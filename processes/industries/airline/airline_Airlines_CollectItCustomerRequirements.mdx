# Collect IT customer requirements

## Overview

Identifying existing or potential IT gaps between the expected business performance levels and current business outcomes. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
