# Determine and document warranty policies

## Overview

Establishing warranty policies to assure customers that the company will guarantee its warranties that it issues. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
