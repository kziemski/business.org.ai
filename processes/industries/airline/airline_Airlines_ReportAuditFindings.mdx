# Report audit findings

## Overview

Reporting audit findings to management. Practice an internal audit with criteria for confirming a problem, a description of the situation, and the root cause of the problem. Make recommendations that resolve the issue. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
