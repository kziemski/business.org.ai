# Understand IT customer communities

## Overview

Interacting with IT customers to understand the IT needs through a collaborative community through involvement, connection, and informed communication. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
