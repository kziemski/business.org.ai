# Maintain chart of accounts

## Overview

Preparing trial balance account from general ledgers. List all accounts used in the general ledger. Alter accounts according to business requirements. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
