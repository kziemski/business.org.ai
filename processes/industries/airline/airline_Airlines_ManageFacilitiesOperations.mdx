# Manage facilities operations

## Overview

Managing all operational activities of the facility. Manage how each function/business unit works. Support the manufacturing facility to attain organizational goals. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
