# Conduct resource planning

## Overview

Analyzing workload needs in relation to asset maintenance and plan resources around those needs. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
