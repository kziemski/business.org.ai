# Understand resource requirements for each product/service and delivery channel/method

## Overview

Determining the production and distribution costs for each product or service, and each channel or method as factors in determining overall pricing. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
