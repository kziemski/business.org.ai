# Establish appropriate performance indicators (metrics)

## Overview

Designing key measures that analyze and interpret how effectively the business is achieving its objectives. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
