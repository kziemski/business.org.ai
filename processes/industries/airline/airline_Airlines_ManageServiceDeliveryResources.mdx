# Manage service delivery resources

## Overview

Understanding the demands on resources and creating a plan to enable the delivery of services via those resources. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
