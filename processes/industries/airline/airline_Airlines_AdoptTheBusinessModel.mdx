# Adopt the business model

## Overview

Consenting to a particular business model and formally accepting it to serve as the set of guiding principles in operating the company. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
