# Develop and maintain job descriptions

## Overview

Creating descriptions for job requisitions. Define the normal components of a job description, such as the overall position description with general areas of responsibility listed, essential functions of the job described with a couple of examples of each, required knowledge, skills, abilities, required education and experience, a description of the physical demands, and a description of the work environment. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
