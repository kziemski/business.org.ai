# Design for re-manufacturing

## Overview

This process enables Airlines to design for re-manufacturing within the Airline industry. Given the industry's focus on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround, this process must be executed with attention to FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA compliance requirements. Effective implementation ensures operational excellence and supports the organization's strategic objectives in a competitive marketplace.
