# Build and maintain IT strategic intelligence

## Overview

Building and maintaining intelligence towards changing organizational goals, supporting management, and operational or functional levels of the business. It is the ability to understand business trends that present threats or opportunities for IT in an organization. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
