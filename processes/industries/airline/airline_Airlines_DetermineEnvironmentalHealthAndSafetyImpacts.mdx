# Determine environmental health and safety impacts

## Overview

Determining the impact of EHS offering--and the procedures it employs to process them--on the environment at large, as well as the health and safety of employees. Evaluate the environmental impact of the organization's products, services, and operations. Conduct health, safety, and environmental audits. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
