# Define distribution strategy

## Overview

Determining distribution channels based on their fit with the products and services that are being offered by the airline. This also identifies the gaps in the existing strategy as determined by the distribution objectives. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
