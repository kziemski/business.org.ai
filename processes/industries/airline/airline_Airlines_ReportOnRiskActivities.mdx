# Report on risk activities

## Overview

Creating reports on risk activities, and communicating them to management. Prepare reports on the potential for adverse safety consequences. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
