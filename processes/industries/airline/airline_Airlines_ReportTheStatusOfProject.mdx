# Report the status of project

## Overview

Recording and documenting the current status and position of the project. Record and report items such as completed tasks, incomplete tasks, planned tasks, and problems faced. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
