# Define customer service experience

## Overview

Communicating to the customer service resources what is expected when engaging the customer. Relate service level expectations to the workforce. Ensure positive customer experience. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
