# Assess readiness for change

## Overview

Determining the level of preparedness of the conditions, attitudes, and resources at all levels in the organization needed for change to happen successfully. Define scope of the proposed change. Select the tools for a change readiness assessment. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
