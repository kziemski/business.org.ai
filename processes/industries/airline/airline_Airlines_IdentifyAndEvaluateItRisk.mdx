# Identify and evaluate IT risk

## Overview

Developing a timely and continuous process to identify and evaluate activities that might hinder IT operations or an IT project's goals. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
