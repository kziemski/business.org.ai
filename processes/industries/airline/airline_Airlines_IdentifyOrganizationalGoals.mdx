# Identify organizational goals

## Overview

Creating and developing strategic objectives that establishes a process to outline expected outcomes and guide employees' efforts. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
