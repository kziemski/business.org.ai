# Prepare AR reports

## Overview

Preparing reports that detail balances due or what to collect from customers at a certain point in time. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
