# Maintain IT asset records

## Overview

Maintaining the complete list of IT items or resources available with the organization with the details on date of purchase, licenses, deployment and SLAs. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
