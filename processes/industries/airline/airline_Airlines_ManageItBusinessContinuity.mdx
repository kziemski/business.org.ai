# Manage IT business continuity

## Overview

Integrating the disciplines of Emergency Response, Crisis Management, Disaster Recovery (technology continuity) and Business Continuity for IT. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
