# Commission equipment

## Overview

Commissioning and inducting any tools, implements, or systems of instruments required for the manufacturing process. Source any engineering solutions that are necessitated for the manufacturing process. Place orders or contract agents for sourcing such equipment. Install and inaugurate the equipment. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
