# Manage employer branding

## Overview

Creating, maintaining and communicating company's reputation and values to keep current employees and attract potential hires. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
