# Review contract and agreed terms

## Overview

Meeting with the customer, partner, and/or supplier to review the terms of the solutions contract and agree on the terms set forth. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
