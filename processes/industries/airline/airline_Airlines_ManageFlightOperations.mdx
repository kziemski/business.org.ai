# Manage flight operations

## Overview

Managing all aspects of flight operations. Flight operations encompass the assignment and routing of the aircraft, the management of  gates, and the closing out of the flight. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
