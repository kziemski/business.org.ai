# Define future state

## Overview

Determining the state or position that the organization wants to be in after the implementation of the change. Gather necessary information about the processes, structures, and cultures. Define resource requirements. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
