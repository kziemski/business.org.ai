# Define future IT services

## Overview

Defining the expected demand and usage of information technology services to meet organization's future business goals. Gather necessary information about the processes, resource requirements, and structures pertaining to planned business growth. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
