# Create and maintain IT service delivery model

## Overview

Design and maintaining an IT service delivery model that defines the processes and procedures needed to deliver the IT services and solutions. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
