# Design and implement KM capabilities

## Overview

Creating knowledge bases and other repositories to preserve and develop company expertise, and to train new employees. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
