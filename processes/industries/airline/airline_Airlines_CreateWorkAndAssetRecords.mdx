# Create work and asset records

## Overview

Implementing records to include all construction work that has been performed. Include all new or modified construction, and any construction or regulatory issues that might have occurred. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
