# Define preventive quality activities

## Overview

Identifying gaps in customer requirements and determining whether the gap will be mitigated through preventive Quality activities or deemed as acceptable risk. The goal of any preventive quality activities is to create provisions to prevent, control, or reduce the risk of not meeting the CtQCs. In addition, any standard methodology that will be used to design or conduct preventive Quality activities are defined and documented. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
