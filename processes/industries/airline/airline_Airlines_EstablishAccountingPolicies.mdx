# Establish accounting policies

## Overview

Establishing policies and procedures to prepare financial statements, including methods, measurement systems, and procedures for providing disclosures. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
