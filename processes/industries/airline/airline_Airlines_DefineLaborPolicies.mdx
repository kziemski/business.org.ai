# Define labor policies

## Overview

Outlining labor policies for resources and ensuring that those policies meet the needs of the organization, the customer, and government regulations. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
