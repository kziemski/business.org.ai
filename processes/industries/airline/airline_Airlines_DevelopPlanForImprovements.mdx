# Develop plan for improvements

## Overview

Devising a course of action to be taken to improve under-performing channels and to promote or expand channels that surpass expectations. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
