# Prioritize opportunities consistent with capabilities and overall business strategy

## Overview

Creating an index of market opportunities, and arrange them in order of preference. Prioritize based on the opportunities' adherence to the overall business strategy. Correlate with the competencies and capacities that the organization, as a whole, processes. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
