# Develop and train employees

## Overview

Creating a link between employee and organizational development needs. Conduct and manage employee training programs by considering the need and availability of these programs. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
