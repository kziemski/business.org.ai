# Assess connection to other initiatives

## Overview

Correlating the change initiative with the other initiatives. Create an alignment between the goals and objectives of the change process and that of the other initiatives. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
