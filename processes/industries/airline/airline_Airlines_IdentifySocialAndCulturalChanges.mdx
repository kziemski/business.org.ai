# Identify social and cultural changes

## Overview

Distinguishing changes in societal makeup, as well as the cultural composite. Isolate shifts in the societal composition and distribution, as well as the value systems and attributes that bind the organization together. Analyze well-regarded publications--and gather the perspective of public intellectuals and opinion leaders--on relevant issues. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
