# Develop IT service/solution "sunset" plans

## Overview

Developing plans to retire IT service/solution resources when the service/solution is no longer feasible. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
