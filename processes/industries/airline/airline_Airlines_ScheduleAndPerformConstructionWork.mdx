# Schedule and perform construction work

## Overview

Arranging a timetable for which to perform construction work. Schedule resources to contract assets for new or replacement assets. Reschedule or redesign assets if needed. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
