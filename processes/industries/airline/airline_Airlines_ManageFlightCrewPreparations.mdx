# Manage flight crew preparations

## Overview

Scheduling crew for flight assignments. The crew provide bids for choosing their duty /off-duty period and these bids are factored in to create crew rosters.  Resource and reserve requirements are determined, and bid services / technology / other enablers are provided to submit bids. Thereafter, the crew bid process, which includes receiving bids for current and vacation schedules, is managed, crew accommodations booked and assignment plans produced. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
