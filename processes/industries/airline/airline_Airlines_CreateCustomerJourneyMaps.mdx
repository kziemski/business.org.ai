# Create customer journey maps

## Overview

Creating a story of the customer's experience: from initial contact, through the process of engagement and into a long-term relationship. The goal is to teach organization about the customer. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
