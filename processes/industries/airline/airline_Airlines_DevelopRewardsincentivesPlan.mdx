# Develop rewards/incentives plan

## Overview

Creating and designing the plan for rewarding the employees exhibiting the desired behavior. Specify rewards in recognition of service, effort, or achievement regarding the change, including bonuses, compensation, stock options, profit sharing, vacations, and flexible time. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
