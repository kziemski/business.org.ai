# Build investor relationships

## Overview

Creating a strategic management responsibility for integrating finance, communication, marketing, and securities law compliance. Allow the most effective two-way communication among the organization, the financial community, and other constituencies. Enlist the investor relations function to provide market intelligence to corporate management. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
