# Determine target segments

## Overview

Identifying the targeted segment of customers. Deduce those particular customer segments that are to be targeted from among the market segments. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
