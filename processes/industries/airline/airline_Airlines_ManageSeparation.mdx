# Manage separation

## Overview

Managing the process of employee separation, including resignations, discharges, and layoffs. Inform the employee of the termination. Complete paperwork for continuation of benefits. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
