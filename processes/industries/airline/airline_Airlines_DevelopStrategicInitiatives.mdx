## Overview

Developing strategic projects that help fulfill long-term goals. Develop time-bound projects that are discretionary in nature and lie beyond the scope of the organization's routine operations. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
