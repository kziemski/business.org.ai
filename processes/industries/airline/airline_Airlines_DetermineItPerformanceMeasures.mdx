# Determine IT performance measures

## Overview

Determining measures for evaluating the performance of IT services in the organization. Establish key performance indicators, including the IT services performance index. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
