# Identify measurement methods

## Overview

Using tools to measure quality. Use tools such as quality improvement oversight system tool, performance measurement tools, consumer information models/guides, and validation tools to effectively determine the quality levels. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
