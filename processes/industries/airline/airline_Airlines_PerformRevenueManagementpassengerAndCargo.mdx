# Perform revenue management (passenger and cargo)

## Overview

Maximizing revenue realization from sales of available inventory. Revenue management is a critical function in an airline and is performed by a specialist team. This is not a finance function. This is accomplished with dedicated systems and processes.  These include monitoring and managing inventory and customer demand, developing and managing pricing and maximizing yield.  Its outcome is used as an input to the Develop and manage sales plans (passenger and cargo) [10105] group of processes. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
