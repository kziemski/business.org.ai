# Determine change enablers

## Overview

Identifying the person(s) or thing(s)responsible for making the change possible. Consider factors such as interdependence of efforts, reward to the integrators, sharing of power and responsibility, and employee understanding of why change is essential. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
