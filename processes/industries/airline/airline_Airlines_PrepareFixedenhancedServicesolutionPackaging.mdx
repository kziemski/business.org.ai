# Prepare fixed/enhanced service/solution packaging

## Overview

Developing packaging for fixed/enhanced service/solution based on the standalone or bundled offerings to be used by the organization. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
