# Develop and execute other marketing campaigns/programs

## Overview

Designing and executing additional marketing campaigns, besides advertising, including undercover marketing, relationship marketing, sponsorships, viral marketing, social media marketing, thought-leadership, and content marketing. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
