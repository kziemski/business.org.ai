# Manage expatriates

## Overview

Managing foreign resources. Manage employees who are sent to live abroad for a defined time period, as well as non-native employees. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
