# Evaluate the effectiveness of the employee on-boarding program

## Overview

Assessing the performance and effectiveness of employee on-boarding program. Examine the performance of on-boarding program through feedback and reviews from the new employees. Create web and written forms. Obtain information through face-to-face discussions. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
