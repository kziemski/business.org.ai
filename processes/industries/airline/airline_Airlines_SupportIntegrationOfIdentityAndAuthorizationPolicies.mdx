# Support integration of identity and authorization policies

## Overview

Create and implement policies that integrate authorization policies with authorized profiles of users meant to access network resources. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
