# Identify IT customer transformation needs

## Overview

Identifying changing needs of staff dependent on information technology based on continuous improvement to deliver results according to organizational goals. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
