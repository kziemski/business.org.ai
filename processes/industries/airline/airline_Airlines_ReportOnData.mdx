# Report on data

## Overview

Summarizing and documenting the results of data analysis. Create graphs and visualizations to illustrate numerical findings to make them more accessible to readers. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
