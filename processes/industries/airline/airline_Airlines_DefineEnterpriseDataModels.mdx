# Define enterprise data models

## Overview

Define different ways of representation, usage, and identification of data with independent or interdependent sources across the organization. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
