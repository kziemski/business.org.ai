# Define portfolio governance

## Overview

Outlining the administration of business portfolio of the organization. Create and manage the rules and regulations regarding the business processes in order to identify, select, prioritize, and monitor portfolio components. Include a set of metrics to indicate the health and progress of the portfolio in the most vital area. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
