# Triage IT service delivery incidents

## Overview

Sorting the incidents of IT service delivery in certain order so that the services could be delivered based on the criticality. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
