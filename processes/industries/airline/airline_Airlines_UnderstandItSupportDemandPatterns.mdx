# Understand IT support demand patterns

## Overview

Evaluate criticality catered by the IT support and expectations to resolve raised or identified issues. Determine the usual requests received for IT support for each area of IT operations. Ensure resolution to every identified or reported issue within specified SLAs. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
