# Define service delivery process

## Overview

Defanging policies and procedures that focus on meeting the needs and expectations of the customer within the working parameters of the organization. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
