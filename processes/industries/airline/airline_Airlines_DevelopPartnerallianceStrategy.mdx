# Develop partner/alliance strategy

## Overview

Defining direction and plan objectives for partnering with other companies to deliver product/services. Focus on creating a vision and strategic objectives and culminate in creating measures for the strategic alliance or partnership. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
