# Perform construction performance management

## Overview

Managing the construction process to ensure that activates are on task, on budget, and are being performed with safety and quality in mind. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
