# Implement promotional pricing programs

## Overview

Managing schemes that offer lower pricing for a limited time as a promotional and sales incentive when launching a new product or a service. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
