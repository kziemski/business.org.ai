# Determine and evaluate IT regulatory and audit requirements

## Overview

Determining and evaluating IT regulatory and audit requirements. Train employees on regulatory and audit requirements. Records for the appropriate regulatory and audit agencies must be maintained and the new product process must be approved by the appropriate regulatory body before it is published to the organization. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
