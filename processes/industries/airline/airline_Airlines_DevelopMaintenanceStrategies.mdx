# Develop maintenance strategies

## Overview

Creating goals and agendas to better realize the success of the maintenance policies that have been put into place. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
