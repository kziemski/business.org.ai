# Maintain relationships with regulators as appropriate

## Overview

Developing and preserving relationships with the regulators, without compromising the legal basis of the relationship. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
