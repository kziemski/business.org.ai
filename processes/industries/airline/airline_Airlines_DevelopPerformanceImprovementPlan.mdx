## Overview

Using performance indicators to report, analyze, and create a detailed performance improvement plan to bridge the performance gaps. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
