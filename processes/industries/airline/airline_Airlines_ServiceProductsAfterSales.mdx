# Service products after sales

## Overview

Assigning post-sales policies and paying claims on purchased products.  This is a process that is an administrative function focused on creating rules (claim codes). This group ensures that claims are valid and are processed quickly.  As well as to quickly determine responsibility for claim settlement. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
