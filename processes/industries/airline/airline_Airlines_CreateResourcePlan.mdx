# Create resource plan

## Overview

Creating a plan to ensure that all resources are available to carry out services required for the customer. This can include physical resources and personnel. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
