## Overview

Identifying and choosing improvement opportunities. Create and manage improvement projects. Perform the improvement activities continuously and routinely. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
