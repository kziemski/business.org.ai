# Capture customer needs and wants

## Overview

Identifying and collecting customers' wants and needs of a product and/or services from a marketing perspective. Identify which consumer needs are important and whether needs are being met by current products/services. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
