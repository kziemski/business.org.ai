# Prepare statements for board review

## Overview

Preparing a draft of financial statements for the board to review before they are sent to the auditor. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
