# Define customer service requirements across the enterprise

## Overview

Defining a set of behaviors, skills, and policies needed to provide customer service effectively across the enterprise. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
