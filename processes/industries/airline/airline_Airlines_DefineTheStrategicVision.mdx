# Define the strategic vision

## Overview

Developing goals to define organizations vision. Define and document ideas, direction, and activities which enable the organization to reach these goals. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
