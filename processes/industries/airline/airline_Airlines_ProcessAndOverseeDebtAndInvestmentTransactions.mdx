# Process and oversee debt and investment transactions

## Overview

Tracking loans taken and money invested in different options. Arrange and supervise loans from banks and individuals and investments in different available and profitable options. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
