# Develop and manage IT resilience and risk

## Overview

Develop and include the processes required to rapidly adapt and respond to any internal or external opportunity, demand, disruption, or threat to IT. Develop a more dynamic, strategic, and integrated approach to managing risk and compliance obligations. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
