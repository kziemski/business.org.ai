# Analyze data

## Overview

Conducting data analysis. Choose statistical algorithms that best reveal patterns and trends in the data. Compare with hypotheses and time series forecasts. Determine error rates and significant outliers. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
