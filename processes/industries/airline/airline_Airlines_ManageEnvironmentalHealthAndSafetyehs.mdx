# Manage environmental health and safety (EHS)

## Overview

Determining the impacts of environmental health and safety. Create and implement the EHS program. Train and educate employees of the EHS function. Oversee and manage the EHS program. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
