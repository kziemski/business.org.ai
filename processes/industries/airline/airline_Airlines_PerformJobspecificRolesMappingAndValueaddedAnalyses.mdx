# Perform job-specific roles mapping and value-added analyses

## Overview

Appraising job-specific roles within the organizational chart and their hierarchical architecture. Analyze a map of work-related roles within the organizational structure. Examine the value added by the positions associated with jobs to be performed and how they stack up within the organization's operations. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
