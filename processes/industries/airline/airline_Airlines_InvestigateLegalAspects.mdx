# Investigate legal aspects

## Overview

Examining regulatory and legislative frameworks. Obligate the organization to remediate any damages through compensations, fines, and any other remedial efforts necessitated to correct the situations. Analyze local environmental laws, binding international covenants, etc. in order to examine legal accuracy about the rules and procedures. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
