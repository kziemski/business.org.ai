# Develop transformation roadmap

## Overview

Creating a blueprint for execution of IT transformation from the existing state to the planned organizational structure based on the value proposition and projected business growth. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
