# Plan and acquire assets

## Overview

Planning, acquiring, and managing facilities, workspaces, and supporting assets. Acquire, configure, and manage facilities and workspaces, to include supporting equipment and materials. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
