# Investigate fraudulent claims

## Overview

Reviewing and assessing claims that contain deliberately incorrect information or that have been submitted with the goal to deceive the system. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
