# Define and implement IT service management

## Overview

Defining and implementing activities involved in designing, creating, delivering, supporting, and managing the lifecycle of information technology services within an organization. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
