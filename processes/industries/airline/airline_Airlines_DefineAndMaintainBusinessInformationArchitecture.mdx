# Define and maintain business information architecture

## Overview

Creating strategies to manage the organization's information and content. Outline the architecture for information collection and communication. Administer information resources, data management and content. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
