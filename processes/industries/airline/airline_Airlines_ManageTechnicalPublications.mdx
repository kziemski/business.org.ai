# Manage technical publications

## Overview

Documentation of changes/maintenance provided is required by law. Airline is obliged to publish these documents to regulator periodically. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
