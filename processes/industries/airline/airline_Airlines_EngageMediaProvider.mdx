# Engage media provider

## Overview

Enrolling the services of certain media providers, who employ marketing channels that are appropriate for target audience. Analyze vendors over several criteria, including channels appropriate for these populations, price points, turnaround time, reliability, quality of deliverables, etc. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
