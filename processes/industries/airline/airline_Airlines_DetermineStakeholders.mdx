# Determine stakeholders

## Overview

Identifying and communicating with shareholders affected by the change. Consider internal and external stakeholders that will be affected by the change. Determine the amount of influence the change will have on them. Ensure the involvement of these stakeholders in the change process by effectively communicating with them. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
