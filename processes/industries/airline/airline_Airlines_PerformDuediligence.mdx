# Perform due-diligence

## Overview

Auditing the status quo of the probabilities, before formalizing any restructuring of the organization with another entity. Systematically investigate all entities discerned to be of interest in Identify restructuring opportunities [16793], to verify all tangible and substantial facts. Consider engaging specialist professional services including legal, accounting, and consulting help. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
