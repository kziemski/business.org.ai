# Manage infrastructure configuration

## Overview

Identifying and tracking individual configuration items, documenting functional capabilities and interdependencies of IT infrastructure. Determining the gaps and needs in order to enhance existing infrastructure configuration. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
