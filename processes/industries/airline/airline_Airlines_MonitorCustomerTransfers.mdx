# Monitor customer transfers

## Overview

Overseeing passenger flight transfers.  Manage transfer flight information which includes managing date/flight information display systems, a transit check-in service, and the management of transfers to / from non-interline partners, including check-in of interline passengers and baggage. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
