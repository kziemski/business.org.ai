# Manage workforce scheduling

## Overview

Organizing the workforce so that all positions are covered for all shifts with the necessary skilled resources in place. Have a system in place to backfill positions while an employee is on leave. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
