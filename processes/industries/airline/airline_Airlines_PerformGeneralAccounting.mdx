# Perform general accounting

## Overview

Applying basic principles, concepts, and accounting practices in recording and preparing final accounts, and using accounting information in management. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
