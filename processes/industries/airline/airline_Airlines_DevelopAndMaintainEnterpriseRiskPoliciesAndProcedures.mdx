# Develop and maintain enterprise risk policies and procedures

## Overview

Establishing and maintaining the policies and procedures for managing risk. Create rules and regulations for enterprise risk dealing with hazardous, financial, operational, and strategic risks. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
