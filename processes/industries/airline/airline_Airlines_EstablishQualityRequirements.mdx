# Establish quality requirements

## Overview

Determining essential activities, processes, and attributes for securing enterprise quality. Outline critical characteristics for quality. Outline activities encouraging the preservation of quality. Create quality controls. Confirm capabilities in accordance with quality requirements. Finalize the plan for quality maintenance. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
