# Realign and refresh project management strategy and approaches

## Overview

Reorganizing and stimulating the approach and strategy for managing business projects. Make improvements based on the project scope and on findings from Evaluate the impact of project management (strategy and projects) on measures and outcomes [11131]. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
