# Relocate material and tools

## Overview

Relocating the tools and raw materials. Shift raw or finished material and machines of company from one place to another place according to changes in business requirements. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
