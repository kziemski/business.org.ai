# Assess customer experience across touchpoints

## Overview

Evaluating customer experiences, expectations, and suggestions in both liked and disliked areas of the product or services. Analyze all modes of communication, human and physical interactions, or customers experience during the relationship lifecycle with your organization. Evaluate the gaps/further development/alterations to the existing product/service to attain better customer response. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
