# Publish accounting policies

## Overview

Creating a written copy of agreed-upon procedures for preparing financial statements, and making them available to the public. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
