# Develop IT strategic alignment

## Overview

Developing the process of aligning the organization's business divisions and staff members with the organization's planned objectives for IT. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
