# Develop and maintain service/solution architectures

## Overview

Creating and maintaining a services and solutions architecture over a network that can be revised as needed or even eliminated in case of inefficiencies. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
