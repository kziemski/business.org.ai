# Manage cockpit operations

## Overview

Managing cockpit activities and coordinating operations between cockpit and air traffic control.  Develop a flight plan and submit to air traffic control (ATC). Perform pre-flight checks and prepare for departure, as well as the actual take-off.  While in flight, the weather is monitored on a continuous basis.  Prepare for arrival at the destination. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
