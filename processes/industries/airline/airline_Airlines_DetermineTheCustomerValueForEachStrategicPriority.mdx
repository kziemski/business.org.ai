# Determine the customer value for each strategic priority

## Overview

Analyzing the value preposition; the value the customer gets from a product/services for each of your Identify strategic priorities [19975]. Customer value is the satisfaction a consumer feels after making a purchase for goods or services relative to what he/she must give up to receive them. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
