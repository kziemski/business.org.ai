# Evaluate acquisition options

## Overview

Appraising entities identified as being suitable for acquisition, taking into account the restructuring opportunities in the internal and external context. Verify the appropriateness and viability of the short-listed options. Ensure these entities pertain to the state-of-affairs in the market, as well as fit with the resources and capabilities of the organization. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
