# Measure customer satisfaction with customer problems, requests, and inquiries handling

## Overview

Calculating satisfaction levels of customers by effectively evaluating the process of handling requests/inquiries of customers. Use it to explore new ideas and opportunities for enhanced customer requests/inquiries handling and resolution process.
