# Identify published best practices

## Overview

Realizing those practices and procedures that are the most effective to the success of the business and making that information available. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
