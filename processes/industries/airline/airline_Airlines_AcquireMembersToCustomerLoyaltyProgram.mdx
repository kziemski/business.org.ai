# Acquire members to customer loyalty program

## Overview

Convincing customers to register their personal information with the company and be assigned a unique identifier that they use when making purchases. The identifier makes it easier for the company to track customer purchases. Customers are rewarded by various incentives that encourage repeat business [20007]. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
