# Create and maintain IT security policies, standards, and procedures

## Overview

Develop and maintain an architecture for securing and ensuring the privacy of data flows throughout the organization. Create, test, evaluate, and implement IT security policies to ensure the safe use of IT services and solutions. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
