# Develop consensus forecast

## Overview

Arriving at a consensus over the forecasted levels of demand for services by analyzing baseline forecasts and customer input. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
