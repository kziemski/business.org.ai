# Develop training plan

## Overview

Creating a detailed summary of all the actions relevant to teaching a person a particular skill or type of behavior. Determine who will deliver the training. Determine when and where the apprentice or trainee needs to go to receive the structured component of the training. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
