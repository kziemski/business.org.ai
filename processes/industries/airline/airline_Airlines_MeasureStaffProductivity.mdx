# Measure staff productivity

## Overview

Evaluating the productivity of employees. Measure activities performed to determine where automation could increase productivity. Identify differences between the productivity of employee groups. Evaluate the quality of work. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
