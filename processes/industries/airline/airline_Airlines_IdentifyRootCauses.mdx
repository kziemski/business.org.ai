# Identify root cause(s)

## Overview

Recognizing the reasons that have triggered the nonconformance events or activities. Perform a root-cause analysis through documentary evidence and interviews. Involve the appropriate individuals. Leverage cause analysis and risk assessment. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
