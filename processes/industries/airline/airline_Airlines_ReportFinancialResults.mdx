# Report financial results

## Overview

Reporting financial results to management, and releasing results to the public. Report financial statements, including the income statement, balance sheet, and statement of cash flows. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
