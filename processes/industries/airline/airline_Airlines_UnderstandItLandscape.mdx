# Understand IT landscape

## Overview

Understanding the complete logical structure and working of the organization's IT landscape. Assess the configuration of hardware and software (IT Assets) across the organization that supports overall business operations. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
