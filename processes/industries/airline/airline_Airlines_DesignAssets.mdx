# Design assets

## Overview

Designing assets to meet organizational needs as well as ensuring that the asset adheres to all national, regional, and local construction codes. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
