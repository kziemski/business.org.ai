# Balance operational workloads across available infrastructure components

## Overview

Balancing workloads of all the processes and services that are provisioned to their internal or external clients, across available components of IT infrastructure. No component should be over or under utilized with the workflow of the IT operations. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
