# Obtain resources

## Overview

Gathering resources needed to complete all construction work. Verify that all resources have the proper training and skills to perform the work. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
