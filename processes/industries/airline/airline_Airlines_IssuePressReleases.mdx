# Issue press releases

## Overview

Issuing press releases to carefully selected media in distribution channels such as the web, newspapers, and social media. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
