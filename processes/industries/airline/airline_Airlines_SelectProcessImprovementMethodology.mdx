# Select process improvement methodology

## Overview

Assessing and choosing methodologies to identify, analyze, and improve existing processes within an organization to meet new goals and objective. Assess the various methodologies available such as process mapping, statistical process control, and simulation. Choose the most appropriate and effective methodology. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
