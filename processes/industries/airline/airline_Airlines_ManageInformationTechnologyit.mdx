# Manage Information Technology (IT)

## Overview

Managing process groups relevant to the business of information technology within an organization. The process groups include "Develop and manage IT customer relationships", "Develop and manage IT business strategy", " Develop and manage IT resilience and risk", " Manage information", " Develop and manage services/solutions", "Deploy services/solutions", and " Create and manage support services/solutions". Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
