# Assess and analyze impact of each option

## Overview

Scoping and probing to study the impact of strategic options for fulfilling the organization's objectives. Estimate a measure of the impact effectuated by each set of strategic decisions, which comprise Define strategic options [10047]. Closely examine the consequences of each option. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
