# Forecast IT service demand

## Overview

Forecasting demand for IT services using current business growth, research, and customer feedback. Refine these forecasts, inspect the approach used in creating forecasts, and determine its accuracy. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
