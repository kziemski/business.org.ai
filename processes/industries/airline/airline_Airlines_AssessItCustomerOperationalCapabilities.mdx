# Assess IT customer operational capabilities

## Overview

Evaluate the ability of the group of staff dependent on information technology, to align resources and critical processes according to organizational vision being able to deliver effectively and efficiently. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
