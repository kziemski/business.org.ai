# Develop content strategy

## Overview

Planning, development, and management of content-written or in other media. Getting the right content to the right user at the right time through strategic planning of content creation, delivery, and governance. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
