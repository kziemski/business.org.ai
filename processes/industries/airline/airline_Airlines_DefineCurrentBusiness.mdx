# Define current business

## Overview

Defining the status quo relating to the de facto core of what the business is. Reflect over the fundamental essence of what the business accomplishes and the manner in which it operates. Look beyond the obvious solution capabilities to delineate capacities that form the basis of the business engine. Involve senior executives and management personnel and possibly professional services providers. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
