# Establish the enterprise risk framework and policies

## Overview

Creating an agenda for the rules and regulations of enterprise risk that deal with hazardous, financial, operational, and strategic risks. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
