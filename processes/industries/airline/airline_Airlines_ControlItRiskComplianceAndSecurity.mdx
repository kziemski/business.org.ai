# Control IT risk, compliance, and security

## Overview

Ensure effective control in overall IT risk management, formulate and execute guidelines in-line with regulatory bodies, and manage organizational security throughout the business operations. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
