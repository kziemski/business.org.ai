# Manage flow of returned products

## Overview

Tracking and taking care of the products that have been internally returned either because of their deficiency or in cases of incorrect delivery. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
