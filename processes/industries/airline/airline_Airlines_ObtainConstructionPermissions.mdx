# Obtain construction permissions

## Overview

Gathering the required permits for construction from the proper jurisdiction. This may include inspections during and after construction to verify that the new asset meets all national, regional, and local codes. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
