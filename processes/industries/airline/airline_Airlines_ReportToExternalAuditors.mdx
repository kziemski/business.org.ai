# Report to external auditors

## Overview

Reporting to external auditors. This process requires the organization to report to external auditors about the regulations for any critical data that the organization is holding. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
