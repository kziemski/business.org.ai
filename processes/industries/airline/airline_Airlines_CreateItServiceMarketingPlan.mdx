# Create IT service marketing plan

## Overview

Creating a marketing strategy for IT offerings to customers. Plan processes for making budgets; identifying and developing media; and managing marketing content and promotional activities. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
