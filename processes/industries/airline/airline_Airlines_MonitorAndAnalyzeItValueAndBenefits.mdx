# Monitor and analyze IT value and benefits

## Overview

Examining and analyzing the value and benefits of IT service management to ensure benefits outweigh incurred costs. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
