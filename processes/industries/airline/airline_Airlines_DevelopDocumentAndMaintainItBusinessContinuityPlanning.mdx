# Develop, document, and maintain IT business continuity planning

## Overview

Develop, document, and maintain plans to ensure uninterrupted operations of critical IT services. Determine resources such as specialized personnel, equipment, support infrastructure, legal and financial aspects. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
