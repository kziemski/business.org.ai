## Overview

Establishing and managing services for providing support to users of IT services and solutions. Define the plethora of services by which the organization assists users of computers, software products, or other information technology products. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
