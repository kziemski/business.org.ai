# Evaluate IT continuity

## Overview

Evaluating IT business needs and IT's ability to recover from internal or external threat exposure. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
