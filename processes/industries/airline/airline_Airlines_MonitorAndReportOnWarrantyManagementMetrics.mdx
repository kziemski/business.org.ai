# Monitor and report on warranty management metrics

## Overview

Comparing warranties by using applicable metrics to see how they are handled and resolved. Develop and submit reports that summarize significant conclusions. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
