# Manage gates

## Overview

Managing crew based on gate needs. Review the flight schedule and aircraft types and determine a schedule based upon an understanding of constraints. Define ground crew assignment plan and assign crew based upon the available gates.  Determine the management of gates (by zone or by gate), the available gates, and review the crew schedule. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
