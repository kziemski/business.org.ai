# Define IT service management strategy

## Overview

Defining perspective, position, plans, and patterns needed to execute designing, delivering, managing, and improving the way information technology is used within an organization. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
