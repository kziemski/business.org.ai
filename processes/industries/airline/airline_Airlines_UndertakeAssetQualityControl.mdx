# Undertake asset quality control

## Overview

Implementing a checks and balances system to verify that the maintenance was performed correctly. Rework when errors are found. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
