# Define the quality strategy

## Overview

Outlining the strategy for managing enterprise quality. Define and formalize quality techniques and standards. Assign responsibilities for achieving the required quality levels. Standardize the quality maintenance procedure, tools and techniques, recording and reporting, the timing of quality maintenance activities, and the roles and responsibilities for the quality management team. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
