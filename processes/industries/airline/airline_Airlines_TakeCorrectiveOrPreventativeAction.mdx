# Take corrective or preventative action

## Overview

Pursuing corrective and preventative activities to eliminate the cause of a detected nonconformity. Define the nonconformity. Communicate and assign responsibility. Identify the appropriate corrective and preventive action. Implement and monitor for reoccurrence. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround.
