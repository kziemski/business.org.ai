# Manage relations with quasi-government bodies

## Overview

Managing relations with quasi-governmental organizations, corporations, businesses, or any other agency that is treated by national laws and principles to be under the supervision of the government but also distinct and self-directed from the government. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
