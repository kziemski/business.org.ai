# Develop property strategy and long term vision

## Overview

Strategizing a long-term vision for managing properties. Prepare strategies and a long-term vision for managing purchased/retained properties. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
