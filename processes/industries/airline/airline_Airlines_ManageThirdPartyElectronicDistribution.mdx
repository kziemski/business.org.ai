# Manage third party electronic distribution

## Overview

Managing relationships with wholesale distributors, consolidators and other distributors (cruise lines etc.). An example of a third party electronic distribution systems, or Global Distribution System (GDS) is Amadeaus, an electronic distribution network used by multiple airlines to increases their distribution reach.
