# Manage third party electronic distribution

## Overview

Managing relationships with wholesale distributors, consolidators and other distributors (cruise lines etc.). An example of a third party electronic distribution systems, or Global Distribution System (GDS) is Amadeaus, an electronic distribution network used by multiple airlines to increases their distribution reach. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
