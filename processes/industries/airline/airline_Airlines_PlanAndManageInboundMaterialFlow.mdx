# Plan and manage inbound material flow

## Overview

Creating and executing a strategy for all the internal activities related to the flow/transfer of inbound materials. This process includes planning inbound material receipts, managing inbound material flow, monitoring inbound delivery performance, managing the flow of returned products, controlling the quality of returned parts, and salvaging or repairing returned products. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
