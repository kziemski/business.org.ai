# Establish accountability for change management

## Overview

Identifying and assigning the people accountable for effective change management. Hold managers accountable for ensuring change happens systematically and rigorously. Ensure that certain behaviors are rewarded or reprimanded accordingly. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
