# Produce and distribute employee annual tax statements

## Overview

Providing tax deductions statements created by certified chartered accountants to every employee for their reference or refunds. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
