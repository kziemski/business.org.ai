# Design and manage product data, design, and bill of materials

## Overview

Designing the BOM-Bill of material, manufacturing BOM and Service BOMA bill of materials list for all the raw materials and components/parts used in the producing end product. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
