# Design and implement control activities

## Overview

Defining and executing policies, procedures, techniques, and mechanisms and actions taken to minimize risk. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
