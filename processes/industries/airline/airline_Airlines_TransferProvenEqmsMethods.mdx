# Transfer proven EQMS methods

## Overview

Recording and transferring the best practices and proven methods associated with enterprise quality management systems (EQMS) that can be leveraged in improving the organization's framework. Record proven methodologies and approaches with the objective of communicating them for upgrading, refining, and enhancing the organization's systems. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
