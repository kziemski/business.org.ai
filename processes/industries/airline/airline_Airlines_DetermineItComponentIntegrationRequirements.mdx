# Determine IT component integration requirements

## Overview

Determining the requirements to integrate IT components such as hardware, software, database, telecommunication, and network. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
