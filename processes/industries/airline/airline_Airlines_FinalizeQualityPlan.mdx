# Finalize quality plan

## Overview

Establishing how the critical-to-quality characteristics will be achieved, controlled, ensured, and managed throughout the lifecycle of a product/service. Address quality requirements and critical-to-quality characteristics. Conduct a preventive quality assessment. Describe how to verify the product/service, verification criteria, and response to nonconformance. Keep records to demonstrate conformity. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
