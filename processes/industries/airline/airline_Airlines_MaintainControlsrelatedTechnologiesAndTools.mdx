# Maintain controls-related technologies and tools

## Overview

Managing technologies and tools related to the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data in order to ensure the security of the organization's information. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
