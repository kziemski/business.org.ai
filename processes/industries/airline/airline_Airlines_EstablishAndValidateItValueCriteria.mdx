# Establish and validate IT value criteria

## Overview

Create and certify the standards to determine the value of the investments, projects, and activities of IT function for the overall business objectives. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
