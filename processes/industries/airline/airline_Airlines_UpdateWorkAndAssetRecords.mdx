# Update work and asset records

## Overview

Modifying existing maintenance records to include all new work that has been performed, what assets were serviced, and any issues that might have arisen. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
