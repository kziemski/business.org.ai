# Provide baggage services

## Overview

Provisioning all aspects of baggage handling for passengers. The airline provides outbound baggage services (receiving baggage, conducting baggage security checks, sorting and transporting baggage to the aircraft); transfer baggage services and inbound baggage services(unloading baggage and transferring to baggage claim); baggage recovery services and the management of baggage handling operations, which covers the completion and transport of freight and documentation. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
