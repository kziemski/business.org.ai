# Manage EHS costs and benefits

## Overview

Administering the costs and benefits of EHS management program. Evaluate program costs to ensure that the benefits of the program always outweigh its costs. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
