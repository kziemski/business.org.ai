# Define the business concept and long-term vision

## Overview

Creating a conceptual framework of the organization's business activity and strategic vision with long-term applicability. Scout the organization's internal capabilities, as well as the customer's needs and desires, to identify a fit that can be used to advance a conceptual structure of the organization's business activity. Conduct analysis in light of relevant externalities and large-scale shifts in the market landscape. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
