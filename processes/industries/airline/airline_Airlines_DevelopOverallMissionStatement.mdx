# Develop overall mission statement

## Overview

Establishing an overarching, compact statement that concisely underscores the mission of the organization. Define and communicate a clear and succinct mission statement, which encapsulates how the organization aims to proceed in order to Establish a strategic vision [10020]. Solicit critical inputs from senior management and strategy executives, and collaborate with marketing or personnel from allied functions. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
