# Perform abandonment

## Overview

Abandoning assets. Manage the act of deliberately and permanently giving up, granting, leaving, or surrendering property or buildings and allowing others to use the intellectual property without protest. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
