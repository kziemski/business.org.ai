# Analyze systems and technology

## Overview

Analyzing the capabilities of technology and process automation systems deployed within the organization in order to direct future associated processes. Conduct a broad-based survey to examine various aspects associated with such systems and technologies, with the objective of identifying key facets that are of interest. Investigate the intended purpose, purpose served, utility, longevity, remaining service-life, repair or service requirements, etc. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
