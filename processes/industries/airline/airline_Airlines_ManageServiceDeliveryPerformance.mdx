# Manage service delivery performance

## Overview

Conducting and implementing performance measures to ensure successful delivery of service to the customer. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
