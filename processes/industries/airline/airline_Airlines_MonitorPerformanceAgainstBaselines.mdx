# Monitor performance against baselines

## Overview

Overseeing the progress of activities to ensure they are on-course and on-schedule in meeting the objectives and performance targets against Establish baselines for business value drivers [19983]. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
