# Plan IT infrastructure change

## Overview

Identify the gaps and needs of existing IT infrastructure. Plan and develop strategies to upgrade/replace existing IT infrastructure. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
