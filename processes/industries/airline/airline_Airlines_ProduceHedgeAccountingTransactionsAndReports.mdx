# Produce hedge accounting transactions and reports

## Overview

Preparing and documenting accounts and records of all hedging investment transactions to reduce risks due to change in markets. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
