# Document trade

## Overview

Documenting and recording the trade processes while making transactions, noting the description, quality, number, transportation medium, indemnity, and inspection. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
