# Manage Financial Resources

## Overview

Overseeing key back-office processes for organizations. This category includes process groups related to planning and management accounting, revenue accounting, general accounting and reporting, fixed-asset project accounting, payroll, accounts payable and expense reimbursements, treasury operations, internal controls, tax management, international funds/consolidation, and global trade services. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
