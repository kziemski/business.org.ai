# Perform corrective asset maintenance and repairs

## Overview

Repairing or correcting faults that occur with an asset. This could be a break or other repairable damage. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
