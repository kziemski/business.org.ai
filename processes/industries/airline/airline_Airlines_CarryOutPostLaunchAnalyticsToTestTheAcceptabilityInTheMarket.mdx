# Carry out post launch analytics to test the acceptability in the market

## Overview

Measuring the performance of marketing once the product/services are launched. This broadly covers measuring user engagement and product's/service's performance in the market. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
