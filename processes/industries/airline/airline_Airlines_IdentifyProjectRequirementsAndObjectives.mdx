# Identify project requirements and objectives

## Overview

Recognizing and defining what the project is ultimately supposed to do. Specify the capabilities, features, or attributes of the project's deliverables, as well as any kind of formal documentation. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
