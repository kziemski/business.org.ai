# Scope processes

## Overview

Defining the extent and limits of business processes. Define the range and diversity of all the set of activities and tasks that, once completed, will accomplish an organizational goal. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
