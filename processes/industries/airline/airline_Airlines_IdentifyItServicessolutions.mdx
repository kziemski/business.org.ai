# Identify IT services/solutions

## Overview

Identifying processes and supporting procedures that are performed by an organization to design, plan, deliver, operate, and control information technology services/solutions offered to customers. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
