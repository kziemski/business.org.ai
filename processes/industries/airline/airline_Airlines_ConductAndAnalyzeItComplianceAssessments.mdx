# Conduct and analyze IT compliance assessments

## Overview

Evaluate and analyze the IT environment for the compliance of industry regulations and government legislation. Ensure that IT capability and resources meet the set standards. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
