# Oversee and coordinate enterprise risk management activities

## Overview

Coordinating to plan, organize, lead, and control the activities of an organization in order to minimize the special effects of risk on capital and earnings. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
