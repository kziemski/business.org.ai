# Design for manufacturing

## Overview

Carrying out the steps necessary to appropriately manufacture correct parts.  This includes designing application, product hardware, mold, casting, mechanical, and electrical aspects of the product. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
