# Automate and control plant

## Overview

Creating and applying technology to monitor and control the production and delivery of products and services. Automation involves a broad range of technologies. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
