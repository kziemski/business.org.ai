# Create strategic and tactical sales plans by customer

## Overview

Establishing long term customer sales plans to assess current sales and to determine future sales objectives, strategies for achieving the goals, and available resources. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
