# Design and plan launch of project

## Overview

Creating a plan specifying when to initiate the project, and introducing it to the target audience. Clearly define the project team, objectives, timelines, and milestone. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
