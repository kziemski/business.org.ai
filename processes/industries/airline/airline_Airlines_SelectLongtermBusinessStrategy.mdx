# Select long-term business strategy

## Overview

Developing a strategy for the achievement of business goals over the distant future. Adopt one of the strategic options for realizing its mission over the long term. Enlist senior management executives, comprising strategy and/or business unit personnel. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
