# Analyze business unit strategies

## Overview

Assessing the performance of a business unit against set organizational goals which are based on pre-defined metrics collected through various business unit operations. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
