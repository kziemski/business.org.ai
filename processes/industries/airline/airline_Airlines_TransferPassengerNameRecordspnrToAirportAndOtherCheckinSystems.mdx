# Transfer Passenger Name Records (PNR) to airport and other check-in systems

## Overview

PNR (Passenger Name record) is a record in the booking system after a booking is completed and the ownership is transferred to the airline from the booking agent. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
