# Prioritize IT projects

## Overview

Listing the IT projects in the order of most important to the least. Determining which of many IT projects are most important or critical to business operations. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
