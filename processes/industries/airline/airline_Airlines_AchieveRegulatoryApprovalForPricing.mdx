# Achieve regulatory approval for pricing

## Overview

Obtaining internal price approvals and governmental approvals that are required for licensed products and for products that can be reimbursed by the government. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
