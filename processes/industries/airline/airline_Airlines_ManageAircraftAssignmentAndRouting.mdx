# Manage aircraft assignment and routing

## Overview

Overseeing all aspects of assigning and routing aircraft. This entails assigning aircraft tail numbers to flight legs, which are origin-destination pairs with time/date of arrival/departure; evaluating maintenance requirements - e.g. whether and when a plane will be ready and available to fly, whether the maintenance department approved use of the aircraft for the flight - and turning over the assignment schedule / mapping of flights with tail numbers to SOC - systems operation control - the airline's control room. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
