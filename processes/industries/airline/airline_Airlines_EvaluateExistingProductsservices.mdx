# Evaluate existing products/services

## Overview

Examining the brands owned and products offered in the market. Determine the relative position of the existing products/brands in the marketplace. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
