# Create IT security, privacy, and data protection risk governance

## Overview

Defining and managing organization's approach to governing IT security and ensuring the privacy of data flows throughout the organization. Establish and manage tools to support the governance process in order to avoid misuse of information and breach of organizational privacy. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
