# Identify implications for key operating model business elements that require change

## Overview

Determine impacts of elements such as staffing, skills, training, new markets, technology, or policies within operating model which needs change. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
