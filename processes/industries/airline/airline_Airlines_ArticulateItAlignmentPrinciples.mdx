# Articulate IT alignment principles

## Overview

Systematic approach to clearly communicate and operate the usage of information technology as it relates to business objectives. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
