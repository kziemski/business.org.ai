# Share knowledge of specific risks across other parts of the organization

## Overview

Sharing information about risks and resilience strategies of business operations across the organization so that prospective risks can be avoided. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
