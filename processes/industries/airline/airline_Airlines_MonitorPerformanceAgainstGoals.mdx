# Monitor performance against goals

## Overview

Defining methodology and frequency of assessment for measuring and monitoring performance of various functions/processes/activities against standard set goals. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
