# Evaluate de-merger options

## Overview

Evaluating departments and subsidiaries within the organization, and/or previously merged entities, to assess the appropriateness of a de-merger, taking account of the fit between these entities as well as any relevant externalities. Examine the pertinence and soundness of a formalized dissociation. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
