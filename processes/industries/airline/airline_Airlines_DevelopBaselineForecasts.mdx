# Develop baseline forecasts

## Overview

Identifying the demand anticipated for the organization's services. Estimate future demand for services using historical data, analysis of the market environment, and external data. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
