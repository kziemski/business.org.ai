# Create organizational design

## Overview

Formulating a design for the organization's resources that allow it to meet its objectives. Develop a new framework for molding the organization's various processes into a coherent and seamless whole. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
