# Assess SLA compliance

## Overview

Gather data from each service target defined in an SLA for a time segment or review period to evaluate an overall performance percentage. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
