# Release resources

## Overview

Discharging leveraged resources from service delivery commitments upon completion. Returning resources to the resource pool. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
