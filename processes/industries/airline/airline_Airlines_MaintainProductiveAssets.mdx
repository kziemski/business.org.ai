# Maintain productive assets

## Overview

Preserving productive assets through the planning, managing, and performance of preventative, routine, and critical maintenance work. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
