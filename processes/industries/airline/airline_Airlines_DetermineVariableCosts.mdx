# Determine variable costs

## Overview

Calculating the variable costs of production. Approximate those costs that depend on the volume of products/services produced by the organization. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
