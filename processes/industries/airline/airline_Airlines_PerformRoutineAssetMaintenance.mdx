# Perform routine asset maintenance

## Overview

Carrying out required maintenance to continue upkeep of equipment or assets. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
