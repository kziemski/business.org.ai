# Communicate with shareholders

## Overview

Practicing regular, transparent communication with shareholders through annual shareholders' meetings, quarterly earnings calls, shareholders letters, one-on-one emails or calls, etc. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
