# Develop business cases

## Overview

Create a business case with value proposition indicating current situation, proposed solution, financial analysis, and measurable benefits to the IT customers. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
