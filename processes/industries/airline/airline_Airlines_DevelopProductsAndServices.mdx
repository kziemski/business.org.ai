# Develop products and services

## Overview

Developing new products/services from scratch, including all activities associated with the design, prototyping, evaluation, and market testing of these planned offerings. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
