# Create and maintain quality partnerships

## Overview

Establishing and maintaining partnerships with third-party sources to achieve quality excellence. Source and evaluate third-party sources both public and private to ensure that the most effective and efficient partnerships are formed. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
