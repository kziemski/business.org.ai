# Manage programs

## Overview

Establishing, implementing, and managing business programs. Successfully handle related projects that together constitute a program. Establish the program structure and approach. Coordinate with stakeholders and partners. Execute the program. Assess and report the performance of the program. Coordinate and prioritize resources across projects. Manage links between the projects and the overall costs and risks of the program. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
