# Identify design/development performance indicators

## Overview

Identifying performance parameters. Determine the parameters to measure performance of the design and development of the product/service concepts into prototypes. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
