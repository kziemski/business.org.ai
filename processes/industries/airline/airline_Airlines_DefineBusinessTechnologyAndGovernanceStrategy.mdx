# Define business technology and governance strategy

## Overview

Defining the need of technology in business and systematic implementation of IT investments. It comprises of assessing competitive technology components to ensure structural analysis, development, usage and security of technology for efficient business operations. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
