# Derive regulatory compliance requirements

## Overview

Meeting regulatory requirements set forth by such directives as RoHS, WEEE, ELV, and REACH. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
