# Conduct health and safety and environmental audits

## Overview

Conducting an inspection to verify that the organization adequately complies with the environmental, health, and safety standards. Audit procedures and records regarding responsibility for the environment, health, and safety. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
