# Identify core EQMS processes, controls, and metrics

## Overview

Recognizing and implementing the processes, controls, and metrics for maintenance of EQMS. Define the role of EQMS in failure mode and effects analysis, complaint handling, and advanced product quality planning. Establish the role of EQMS in evaluating metrics such as cost of quality, overall equipment effectiveness, on-time and complete shipments, percentage of products in compliance, and new product introductions. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
