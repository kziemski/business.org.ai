# Negotiate and establish service level agreements

## Overview

Establish a service level agreement, which is a negotiated agreement designed to create a common understanding about services, priorities, and responsibilities. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
