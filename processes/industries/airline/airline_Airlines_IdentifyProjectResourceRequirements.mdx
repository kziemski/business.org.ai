# Identify project resource requirements

## Overview

Identifying the prerequisites of business projects. Identify the people with appropriate and applicable skills and competencies. Locate resources such as capital, facilities, equipment, material, and information required to accomplish the objectives of a specific project. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
