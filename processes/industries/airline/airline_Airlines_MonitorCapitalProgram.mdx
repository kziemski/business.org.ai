# Monitor capital program

## Overview

Monitoring plans on capital projects. Capital projects can be purchasing buildings, lands, etc. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
