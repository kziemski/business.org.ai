# Set up and maintain service delivery governance and management system

## Overview

Providing a system for which to manage customer needs and a structure for which to facilitate service delivery to fulfill those needs. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
