# Monitor and map current and emerging technologies

## Overview

Monitoring and evaluating existing and forthcoming technologies to meet the current and future growth plans for business operations. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
