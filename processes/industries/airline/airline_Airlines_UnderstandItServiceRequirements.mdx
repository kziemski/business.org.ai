# Understand IT service requirements

## Overview

Understand requirements related to information technology services considering enterprise-level effects and understand potential achievements in the business environment. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
