# Solicit feedback from customer on service delivery satisfaction

## Overview

Engaging the customer post delivery to gauge the effectiveness of services rendered in order to improve on key delivery functions going forward. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
