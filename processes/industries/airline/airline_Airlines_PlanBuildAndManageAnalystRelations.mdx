# Plan, build, and manage analyst relations

## Overview

Creating and maintaining long-term relations with analysts. Involve analysts in strategy and product decisions in order to elicit valuable information. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
