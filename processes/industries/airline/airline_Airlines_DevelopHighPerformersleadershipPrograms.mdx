# Develop high performers/leadership programs

## Overview

Creating a program that incorporates incentives and compensation put forth by the organization to recognize high performing workers and excellence in leadership. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
