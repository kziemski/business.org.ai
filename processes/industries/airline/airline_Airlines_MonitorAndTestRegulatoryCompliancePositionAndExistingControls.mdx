# Monitor and test regulatory compliance position and existing controls

## Overview

Monitoring, appraising, and evaluating the compliance position of the organization in order to fine-tune for effective remediation. Track efforts for handling regulatory and compliance requirements necessitated by law. Test the robustness of internal frameworks, procedures, and approaches for dealing with these requirements, in order to clearly identify any necessary changes. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
