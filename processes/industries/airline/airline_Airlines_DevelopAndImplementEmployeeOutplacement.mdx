# Develop and implement employee outplacement

## Overview

Helping former employees transition to new jobs or to re-orient themselves in the job market. Deliver help through one-on-one sessions or in a group format. Provide guidance in career evaluation, resume writing, interview preparation, developing networks, and job searching. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
