# Manage project scope

## Overview

Determining and documenting a list of specific project goals, deliverables, tasks, costs, and deadlines. Use the scope statement to explain the boundaries of the project. Assign responsibilities for team members. Set up procedures for verifying and approving the completed tasks. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
