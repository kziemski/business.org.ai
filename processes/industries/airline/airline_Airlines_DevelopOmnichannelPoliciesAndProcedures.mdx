# Develop omni-channel policies and procedures

## Overview

Determining the detailed policies and procedures that each of the channels needs to follow in order to conform to the organizational marketing strategy and to provide seamless customer service experience. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
