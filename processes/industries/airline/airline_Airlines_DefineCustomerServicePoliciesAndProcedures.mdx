# Define customer service policies and procedures

## Overview

Outlining the framework of policies and methods for developing customer service strategy. Establish the rules and regulations that serve as a guideline for the customer service strategy. Take into account customer needs and behavior. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
