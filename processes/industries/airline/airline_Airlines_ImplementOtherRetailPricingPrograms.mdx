# Implement other retail pricing programs

## Overview

Determining the optimum consumer pricing for each product or service at the point of sale, based on production and distribution costs and estimated sales volume. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
