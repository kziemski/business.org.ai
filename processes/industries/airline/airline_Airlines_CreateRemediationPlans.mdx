# Create remediation plans

## Overview

Creating plans for remediation efforts. Make a plan to address a case of environmental adulteration. Identify and treat the adulteration so that the area will become operational again. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
