# Establish  data ownership and stewardship responsibilities

## Overview

Establishing entities responsible for data accuracy, integrity, and timeliness that can authorize or deny access to certain data. Develop data utilizing governance processes to ensure fitness of data elements. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
