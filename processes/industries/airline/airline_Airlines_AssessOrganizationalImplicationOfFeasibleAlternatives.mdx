# Assess organizational implication of feasible alternatives

## Overview

Probing the repercussions of all practicable organizational design options. Analyze the significance and impact of workable organizational structure options. Closely examine the long-term impact of these frameworks over the functioning of the organization. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
