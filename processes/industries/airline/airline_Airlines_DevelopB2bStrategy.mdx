# Develop B2B strategy

## Overview

Defining a long term plan of action and roadmap to achieve business to business objectives and goals. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
