# Validate warranty claim

## Overview

Ensuring that the claim falls within the parameters of the warranty in question. After validation is made, the claim must be investigated. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
