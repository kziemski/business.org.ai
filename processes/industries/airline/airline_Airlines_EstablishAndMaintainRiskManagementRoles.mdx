# Establish and maintain risk management roles

## Overview

Determine and maintain roles that are specialized in each risk areas and coordinating all risk management activities for IT function with due escalation structure. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
