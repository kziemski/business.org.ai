# Design the change

## Overview

Developing plans for change management, training, communication, and rewards/incentives. Establish metrics for measuring the change adoption. Clarify new roles for employees. Identify budgets. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
