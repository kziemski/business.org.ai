# Develop IT compliance, risk, and security strategy

## Overview

Ensuring that the organization effectively manages risk. Develop rules and standards for robust IT operations, manage risk, and adopt measures to protect integrity, confidentiality, and security of IT assets. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
