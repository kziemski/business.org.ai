# Manage air crew qualifications

## Overview

Qualifying pilots and flight attendants.  Airline crew (cabin crew and flight crew (pilots)) are required by law to keep their qualifications current, which entails periodic training and re-training. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
