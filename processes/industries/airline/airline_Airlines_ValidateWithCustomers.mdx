# Validate with customers

## Overview

Creating a process to validate the sales process and the assumptions that underpin the business model. Understand if the products/services have a repeatable, scalable business model around that product. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
