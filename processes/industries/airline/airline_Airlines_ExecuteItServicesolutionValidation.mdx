# Execute IT service/solution validation

## Overview

Validating that the proposed IT service/solution is feasible and provides the needed services for the customer. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
