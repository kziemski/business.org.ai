# Process trade preferences

## Overview

Preparing global trade under preference, which allows the organization to import/export products at a lower or nil rate of customs duty and/or levy charge. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
