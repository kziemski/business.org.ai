# Test continuous business operations

## Overview

Assessing ongoing activities within the organization that are not intended to stop except for in an emergency. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
