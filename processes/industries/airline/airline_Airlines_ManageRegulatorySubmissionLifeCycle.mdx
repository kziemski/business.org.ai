# Manage regulatory submission life cycle

## Overview

Determine and follow the timely input and update of regulatory information by assessing reforms, regulatory policies, and guidelines. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
