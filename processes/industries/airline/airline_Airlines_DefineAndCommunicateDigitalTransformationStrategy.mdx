# Define and communicate digital transformation strategy

## Overview

Defining the integration of digital technology into business operations and service delivery, and convey the strategy to different segments of business. It is always backed by continuous improvement followed by periodic review and change per requirement of the business. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
