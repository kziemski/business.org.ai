# Measure cost effectiveness

## Overview

Measuring the cost effectiveness of the organization's processes. Track the return on investing in marketing campaigns, new equipment, and process redefinition. Measure the cost per employee or cost per cycle for a given process, function, or business unit. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
