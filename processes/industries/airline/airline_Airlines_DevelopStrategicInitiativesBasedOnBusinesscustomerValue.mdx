# Develop strategic initiatives based on business/customer value

## Overview

Creating a statement of the organization's direction based on what is considered "value" to the customer or business. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
