# Control exports and imports

## Overview

Overseeing and directing the flow of trade to/from the organization in order to ensure financial gains. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
