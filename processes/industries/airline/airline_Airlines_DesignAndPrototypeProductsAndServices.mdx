# Design and prototype products and services

## Overview

Sketching and standardizing product and service based on the market. Analyze the data market competitiveness and innovation attained for the product and service development. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
