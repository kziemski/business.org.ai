# Define and manage skills taxonomy

## Overview

Analyzing the skills needed to perform services to be delivered. Classify and organize these skills requirements. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
