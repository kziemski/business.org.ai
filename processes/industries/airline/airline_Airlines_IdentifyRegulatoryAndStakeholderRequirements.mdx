# Identify regulatory and stakeholder requirements

## Overview

Determining any protocols or standards to comply with, set by regulatory agencies or the organization's stakeholders. Closely examine all standards and matters of compliance relating to the environment, health, and safety. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
