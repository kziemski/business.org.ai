# Develop training program

## Overview

Identifying skills, knowledge, and attributes that need enhancement in order to perform a job. Develop the appropriate training programs. These programs can be computer-based, classroom, or on-the-job training, etc. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
