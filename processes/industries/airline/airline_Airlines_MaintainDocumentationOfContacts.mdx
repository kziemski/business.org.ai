# Maintain documentation of contacts

## Overview

Keeping a repository of documents that contain information about the network of partners, prospects and customers. Keep records up-to-date with routine reviews and modify as needed. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
