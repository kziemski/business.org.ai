# Execute projects

## Overview

Implementing the business projects of the organization. Evaluate the impact of project management. Record and report the status of the project. Manage the project scope. Promote and sustain activities and involvement. Realign and revamp the project management strategy and approach. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
