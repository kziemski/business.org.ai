# Develop new branding

## Overview

Creating branding collaterals and campaigns that carve a significant and differentiated presence for the organization's offerings. Create new collaterals, which include names, designs, and symbols, for their products/services. Ensure collaterals reflect the unique value proposition of the respective offerings through a consistent theme. Create advertising and promotion campaigns. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
