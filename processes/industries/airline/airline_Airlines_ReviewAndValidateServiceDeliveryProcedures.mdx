# Review and validate service delivery procedures

## Overview

Revisioning service delivery procedures that fall short of performance parameters. Realign procedures with specified expectations in order to provide successful service delivery. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
