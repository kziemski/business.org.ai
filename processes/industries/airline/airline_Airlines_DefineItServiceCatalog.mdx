# Define IT service catalog

## Overview

Create and design an organized and curated collection of all IT-related services that can be performed by, for, or within the organization. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
