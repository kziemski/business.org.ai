# Review and report program performance

## Overview

Evaluating and documenting the performance of business programs. Evaluate the performance of the programs. Create reports on the basis of the analysis. Use performance indicators and metrics such as desired/achieved goals, completion dates, issues and defects, and cost effectiveness. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
