# Develop project measures and indicators

## Overview

Developing procedures and indictors to assess performance of business projects. Design and develop metrics and indicators--such as cost, schedule, resources, risk, and quality--that exhibit the performance of the business projects. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
