# Plan and manage IT continuity

## Overview

Planning and managing IT's ability to recover from exposure to internal and external threats. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
