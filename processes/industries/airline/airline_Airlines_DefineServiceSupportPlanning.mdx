# Define service support planning

## Overview

Develop strategies and methodologies to provide service support. Examine service levels, support complexity, stakeholder requirements to offer service support. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
