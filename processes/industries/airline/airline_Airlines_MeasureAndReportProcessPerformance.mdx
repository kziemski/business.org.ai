# Measure and report process performance

## Overview

Defining and using performance indicators to consider the financial perspective, customer perspective, internal process perspective, and learning perspective of the organization. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
