# Develop sales partner/alliance relationships

## Overview

Cultivating an alliance of partners by identifying, analyzing, negotiating, and managing partnerships with other economic agents. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
