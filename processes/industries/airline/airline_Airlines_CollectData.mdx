# Collect data

## Overview

Gathering and harvesting structured and unstructured data from disparate sources. Clean and pre-process data. Remove duplicates. Convert to a uniform format to make records comparable. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
