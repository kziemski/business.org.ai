# Measure and benchmark

## Overview

Creating and administering a strategy to manage organizational performance. Benchmark the performance of the internal processes, as well as the organization as a whole. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
