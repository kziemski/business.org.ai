# Design facility

## Overview

Preparing and analyzing different designs for a facility in order to finalize which design will be the most suitable option. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
