# Manage airline websites

## Overview

Developing content updates and populating the website with relevant sale and fare information.  Managing external content from service providers and other sources is another key activity to ensure that the site is relevant, accurate and up to date. Within the Airline industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on flight operations, passenger experience, and maintenance turnaround. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, TSA, DOT, IATA standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
