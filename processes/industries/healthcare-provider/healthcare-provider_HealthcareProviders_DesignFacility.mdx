Healthcare facilities management must comply with healthcare-specific building codes, infection control requirements, and life safety standards. This includes maintaining specialized HVAC systems, medical gas systems, and emergency power systems. Facilities must meet The Joint Commission Environment of Care standards and CMS physical environment requirements.
