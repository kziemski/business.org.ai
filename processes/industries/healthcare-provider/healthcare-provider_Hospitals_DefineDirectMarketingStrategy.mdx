## Industry Variations

Healthcare marketing must comply with advertising regulations, HIPAA restrictions on patient information use, and ethical guidelines from medical associations. Marketing strategies focus on community health education, service line promotion, and physician referral development while maintaining patient trust and organizational reputation.
