## Industry Variations

Healthcare compliance involves navigating a complex regulatory landscape including HIPAA, the Affordable Care Act, Stark Law, Anti-Kickback Statute, EMTALA, and state-specific healthcare regulations. Healthcare providers must also maintain accreditation with bodies like The Joint Commission, CMS Conditions of Participation, and specialty-specific certification requirements.
