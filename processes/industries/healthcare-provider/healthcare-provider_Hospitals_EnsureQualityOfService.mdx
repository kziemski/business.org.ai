Quality management in healthcare is driven by patient safety imperatives and regulatory requirements. This process incorporates clinical quality measures (CQMs), patient satisfaction scores (HCAHPS), and value-based purchasing metrics. Healthcare providers utilize root cause analysis, sentinel event reporting, and continuous quality improvement methodologies specific to clinical settings.
