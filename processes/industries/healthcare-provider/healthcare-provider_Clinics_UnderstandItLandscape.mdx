## Industry Variations

Healthcare IT processes must prioritize HIPAA compliance, patient data security, and interoperability between clinical systems. This includes EHR/EMR integration, health information exchange (HIE) connectivity, and support for clinical decision support systems. IT must ensure 99.99% uptime for critical patient care systems and maintain audit trails for all PHI access.
