## Industry Variations

Permit application processing in city government is a core public service with significant regulatory implications:

- **Multi-Department Review**: Building permits typically require concurrent review by building, fire, zoning, engineering, and sometimes environmental departments
- **State Building Codes**: Cities enforce state-adopted building codes (IBC, IRC) with local amendments, requiring inspectors with specific certifications
- **Zoning Compliance**: Permit review includes verification of zoning compliance, setbacks, lot coverage, and conditional use permit requirements
- **Public Notice Requirements**: Certain permits trigger public notice, neighbor notification, and hearing requirements before approval
- **Expedited Processing**: Cities often offer fee-based expedited review for time-sensitive projects while maintaining standard service levels for routine applications
