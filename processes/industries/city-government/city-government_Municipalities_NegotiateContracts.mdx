## Industry Variations

Contract negotiation in municipal government primarily involves labor agreements with public employee unions:

- **Collective Bargaining**: Cities negotiate Memoranda of Understanding (MOUs) with multiple bargaining units representing police, fire, general employees, and management associations
- **Meet and Confer**: Non-represented employees may have "meet and confer" rights requiring good faith consultation on working conditions
- **Public Disclosure**: Negotiation updates and final agreements require public disclosure and council approval in open session
- **Impasse Procedures**: State law provides mediation, fact-finding, and arbitration procedures when negotiations reach impasse
- **Cost Analysis**: Negotiated salary and benefit changes require multi-year fiscal impact analysis for budget planning and council approval
