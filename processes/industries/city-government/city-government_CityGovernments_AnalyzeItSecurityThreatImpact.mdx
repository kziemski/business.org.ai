## Industry Variations

IT security threat analysis in city government involves unique considerations due to public sector responsibilities:

- **Critical Infrastructure**: Cities operate essential services (water, power grid controls, traffic systems, emergency dispatch) that are high-value targets requiring specialized threat modeling
- **Public Data Protection**: Threat analysis must consider exposure of citizen personal information, tax records, utility accounts, and law enforcement data
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Cities must comply with CJIS (Criminal Justice Information Services), HIPAA for health department data, and state data breach notification laws
- **Limited Resources**: Security analysis must prioritize threats given typically constrained IT budgets compared to private sector organizations
- **Inter-Agency Coordination**: Threats often require coordination with state/federal cybersecurity agencies, regional fusion centers, and law enforcement partners
