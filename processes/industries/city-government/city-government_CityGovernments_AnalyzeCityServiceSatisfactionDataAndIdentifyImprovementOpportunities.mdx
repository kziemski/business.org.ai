## Industry Variations

Citizen service satisfaction analysis in city government has unique characteristics:

- **Democratic Accountability**: Satisfaction data directly informs elected officials and may influence policy decisions, budget priorities, and re-election outcomes
- **Equity Analysis**: Cities must analyze service satisfaction across demographic groups, neighborhoods, and income levels to ensure equitable service delivery
- **Multi-Channel Feedback**: Data comes from 311 systems, town halls, social media, surveys, and council meetings rather than traditional customer feedback channels
- **Service Breadth**: Analysis covers diverse services from trash collection to parks maintenance to permit processing, each with different satisfaction drivers
- **Performance Benchmarking**: Cities often compare satisfaction metrics with peer cities through ICMA or National Citizen Survey benchmarking programs
