## Industry Variations

Emergency call routing in municipal 911 centers has unique operational requirements:

- **NENA Standards**: Call routing follows National Emergency Number Association (NENA) standards for call handling, transfer protocols, and interoperability
- **CAD Integration**: Computer-Aided Dispatch (CAD) systems automatically route calls based on incident type, location, and available unit status
- **Multi-Jurisdictional**: Calls may need routing to county sheriff, state police, federal agencies, or neighboring PSAPs based on jurisdiction and incident type
- **NG911 Transition**: Municipalities are transitioning to Next Generation 911 supporting text, images, and video requiring updated routing capabilities
- **Performance Metrics**: Call routing performance is measured against NFPA standards for answer times (10 seconds) and dispatch times (60-90 seconds)
