## Industry Variations

City government payroll processing involves unique public sector complexities:

- **Multiple Bargaining Units**: Cities often process payroll for numerous unions (police, fire, AFSCME, professional staff) each with different pay scales, overtime rules, and benefits
- **Public Pension Systems**: Payroll must calculate and remit contributions to state/local retirement systems with complex tier structures and vesting rules
- **Specialty Pay**: Public safety payroll includes specialty pay (hazard, bilingual, shift differential, certification pay) governed by MOUs and civil service rules
- **Transparent Reporting**: Employee compensation is public information, requiring accurate payroll data for public salary databases and CAFR reporting
- **FLSA Compliance**: Special FLSA rules apply to police and firefighters regarding overtime calculation and compensatory time
