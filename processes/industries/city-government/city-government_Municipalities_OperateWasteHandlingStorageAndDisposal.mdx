## Industry Variations

Municipal waste operations have unique regulatory and service delivery requirements:

- **Flow Control**: Cities may have flow control ordinances directing waste to designated transfer stations or landfills to support municipal solid waste systems
- **Enterprise Fund**: Many cities operate solid waste as an enterprise fund with user fees covering operational costs rather than general fund subsidies
- **Recycling Mandates**: State recycling and diversion mandates (e.g., California AB 939) require cities to achieve specific landfill diversion rates
- **Franchise Agreements**: Some cities franchise collection to private haulers while maintaining rate regulation and service level standards
- **Hazardous Waste**: Municipalities must provide household hazardous waste collection events and e-waste disposal compliant with EPA regulations
