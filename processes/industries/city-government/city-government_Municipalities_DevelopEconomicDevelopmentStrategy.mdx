## Industry Variations

Economic development strategy in municipal government focuses on growing the local tax base and creating jobs:

- **Business Attraction**: Cities market to prospective employers through economic development corporations, highlighting workforce, infrastructure, and quality of life
- **Incentive Programs**: Cities offer tax abatements, TIF districts, enterprise zones, and other incentives to attract business investment
- **Small Business Support**: Many cities provide small business assistance through incubators, technical assistance, and microloan programs
- **Workforce Development**: Economic development strategies coordinate with community colleges and workforce boards on skills training aligned with employer needs
- **Redevelopment**: Brownfield remediation and downtown revitalization programs aim to reactivate underutilized properties and attract private investment
