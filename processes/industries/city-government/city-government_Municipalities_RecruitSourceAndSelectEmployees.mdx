## Industry Variations

Municipal recruitment and selection follows civil service rules distinct from private sector hiring:

- **Civil Service Examinations**: Most positions require competitive examinations (written, oral, practical) with ranked eligibility lists from which hiring must occur
- **Veterans' Preference**: Federal and state laws require preference points for veterans in scoring and hiring decisions
- **Residency Requirements**: Some cities maintain residency requirements for public safety or all positions, affecting candidate pools
- **Background Investigations**: Extensive background checks are required for police, fire, and positions with financial or security responsibilities
- **Lateral Programs**: Police and fire departments often recruit experienced officers from other agencies through lateral entry programs with modified requirements
