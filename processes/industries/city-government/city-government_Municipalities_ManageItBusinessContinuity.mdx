## Industry Variations

IT business continuity in municipal government must ensure essential public services remain operational:

- **Essential Services Priority**: Continuity planning prioritizes 911 dispatch, police/fire CAD systems, utility SCADA, and emergency notification systems
- **EOC Integration**: IT continuity plans integrate with Emergency Operations Center (EOC) activation for coordinated disaster response
- **Public Safety Networks**: FirstNet and other public safety networks require specialized continuity planning separate from general government IT
- **Mutual Aid for IT**: Some cities have mutual aid agreements for IT disaster recovery, sharing backup facilities and technical resources
- **Cloud Migration**: Cities increasingly leverage cloud services for continuity, though public records requirements may limit cloud options
