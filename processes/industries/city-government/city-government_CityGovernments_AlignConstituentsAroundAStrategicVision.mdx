## Industry Variations

Building constituent alignment around strategic vision is fundamentally different in city government:

- **Electoral Mandate**: Strategic vision must reflect campaign commitments of elected officials while building consensus across political factions on the council
- **Public Participation**: State laws often require formal public participation processes, community meetings, and comment periods for comprehensive plan adoption
- **Diverse Stakeholders**: Cities must align residents, businesses, neighborhood associations, advocacy groups, unions, and regional partners with often competing interests
- **Long-Term Horizon**: Strategic visions (comprehensive plans, sustainability plans) often span 10-20 years, outlasting individual administrations
- **Transparency Requirements**: Strategic planning documents, meeting materials, and stakeholder input become public records subject to open meetings and records laws
