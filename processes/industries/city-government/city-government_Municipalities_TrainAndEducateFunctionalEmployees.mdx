## Industry Variations

Employee training in municipal government involves specialized requirements for public service roles:

- **POST/Fire Academy**: Police and firefighters require academy training meeting state POST (Peace Officer Standards and Training) or fire service certification requirements
- **Continuing Education**: Many positions require ongoing CEU credits for professional certifications (engineers, planners, building officials, EMTs)
- **Mandatory Training**: All employees typically receive mandatory training on harassment prevention, ethics, safety, and emergency procedures
- **Leadership Development**: Cities invest in supervisory and management training to develop internal candidates for civil service promotional exams
- **E-Learning Platforms**: Municipalities increasingly use learning management systems to deliver training cost-effectively across dispersed worksites
