## Industry Variations

Fire services support for public events in municipal government involves unique public safety considerations:

- **Life Safety Standby**: Fire department provides standby services for large gatherings, including engine companies, EMS units, and fire prevention personnel based on crowd size
- **Permit Coordination**: Fire marshals review and approve special event permits for pyrotechnics, temporary structures, cooking operations, and crowd capacity
- **Mutual Aid**: Large events may require automatic or mutual aid agreements with neighboring jurisdictions to maintain baseline emergency response capability
- **Community Outreach**: Fire departments use public events for education (fire safety demos, smoke detector programs) and recruitment opportunities
- **Cost Recovery**: Municipalities may charge event organizers for fire standby services above baseline staffing levels through special event fee schedules
