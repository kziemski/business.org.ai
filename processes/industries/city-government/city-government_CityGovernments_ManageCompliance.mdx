## Industry Variations

Compliance management in city government spans a broad range of regulatory requirements:

- **Grant Compliance**: Cities receiving federal grants must comply with Uniform Guidance (2 CFR 200), maintaining documentation, cost allocation, and single audit requirements
- **State Mandates**: Cities must track and report compliance with state-mandated programs, often with unfunded mandate tracking for cost recovery advocacy
- **Consent Decrees**: Some cities operate under DOJ consent decrees for police reform, ADA compliance, or environmental issues requiring monitored compliance programs
- **Procurement Compliance**: Public contracting requires compliance with competitive bidding thresholds, minority business goals, and prevailing wage requirements
- **Environmental Regulations**: Cities must comply with EPA stormwater permits (MS4), Clean Air Act requirements, and environmental justice considerations
