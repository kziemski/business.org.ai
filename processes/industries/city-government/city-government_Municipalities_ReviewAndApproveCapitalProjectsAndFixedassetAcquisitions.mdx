## Industry Variations

Capital project approval in municipal government follows structured public investment processes:

- **CIP Development**: Cities develop 5-10 year Capital Improvement Programs (CIP) prioritizing infrastructure investments across departments
- **Council Approval**: Individual projects and contracts above threshold amounts require city council approval, often with public hearings
- **Funding Source Analysis**: Project approval requires identifying funding sources (general obligation bonds, revenue bonds, grants, impact fees, special assessments)
- **CEQA/NEPA Review**: Major capital projects may require environmental impact review under state or federal environmental law
- **Asset Condition Assessment**: Project prioritization often relies on infrastructure condition assessments (pavement condition, pipe condition ratings) for data-driven decisions
