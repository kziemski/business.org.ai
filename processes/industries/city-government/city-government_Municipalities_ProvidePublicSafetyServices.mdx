## Industry Variations

Public safety service delivery is a core municipal function with distinctive characteristics:

- **24/7 Operations**: Police, fire, and EMS maintain continuous operations requiring complex shift scheduling, overtime management, and minimum staffing requirements
- **Response Time Standards**: Municipalities establish response time goals (e.g., 4-minute fire response, 8-minute EMS) that drive station locations and staffing levels
- **Accreditation Standards**: Departments may pursue CALEA (law enforcement) or ISO (fire) accreditation requiring documented policies and performance standards
- **Interlocal Agreements**: Automatic and mutual aid agreements with neighboring jurisdictions extend response capabilities and require coordination protocols
- **Community Policing**: Modern policing emphasizes community engagement, problem-oriented policing, and collaborative approaches beyond traditional enforcement
