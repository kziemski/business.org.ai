## Industry Variations

Permit code verification in municipal government is a foundational regulatory function:

- **Code Adoption**: Municipalities adopt state building codes (IBC, NEC, UPC) often with local amendments addressing regional conditions like seismic, wind, or flood zones
- **Plan Review**: Verification requires licensed plan examiners to review construction documents against multiple code disciplines (structural, electrical, plumbing, mechanical, fire, accessibility)
- **Inter-Department Routing**: Complex projects route through multiple review disciplines (building, fire, public works, environmental) with consolidated comment tracking
- **Electronic Plan Review**: Many cities have transitioned to electronic plan review systems enabling remote review, digital markup, and faster turnaround times
- **State Certification**: Building officials and plan reviewers often require ICC certification and state licensing to perform code verification duties
