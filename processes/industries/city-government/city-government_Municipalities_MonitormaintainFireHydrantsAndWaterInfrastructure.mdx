## Industry Variations

Fire hydrant and water infrastructure maintenance involves coordination between multiple city functions:

- **ISO Ratings**: Hydrant flow testing and maintenance directly impacts ISO fire suppression ratings, affecting property insurance rates citywide
- **Fire Department Coordination**: Annual hydrant testing is often conducted by fire departments who report maintenance needs to water utilities
- **GIS Mapping**: Municipalities maintain GIS databases of hydrant locations, flow rates, and maintenance history for emergency response planning
- **Color Coding**: NFPA standards recommend color-coding hydrants by flow capacity (blue/green/orange/red) to guide firefighting operations
- **Flushing Programs**: Routine hydrant flushing maintains water quality but requires coordination with traffic, public notice, and water conservation during droughts
