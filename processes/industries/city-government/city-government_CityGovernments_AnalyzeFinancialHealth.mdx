## Industry Variations

Financial health analysis for city governments differs fundamentally from private sector financial analysis:

- **GASB Standards**: Cities follow Governmental Accounting Standards Board (GASB) rather than GAAP, using different financial statements and metrics
- **Credit Rating Focus**: Financial health directly impacts municipal bond ratings and borrowing costs, requiring attention to debt ratios and reserve levels
- **Fund Balance Analysis**: Cities monitor fund balance levels against policy targets (typically 15-25% of expenditures) rather than profitability metrics
- **Revenue Diversity**: Analysis examines concentration risk across tax sources and sensitivity to economic cycles
- **Unfunded Liabilities**: Pension and other post-employment benefit (OPEB) liabilities require specialized actuarial analysis and long-term planning
