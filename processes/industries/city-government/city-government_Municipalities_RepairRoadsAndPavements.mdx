## Industry Variations

Road and pavement repair in municipal government involves specific public works practices:

- **Pavement Management Systems**: Cities use computerized PMS to rate road conditions (PCI scores), prioritize repairs, and forecast maintenance budgets over 10-20 year cycles
- **Utility Coordination**: Repairs must coordinate with underground utilities (water, sewer, gas, telecom) and may require moratorium periods after utility cuts
- **Prevailing Wage**: Public works contracts require Davis-Bacon or state prevailing wage rates, affecting project costs and contractor selection
- **ADA Compliance**: Repairs to sidewalks and curb ramps must meet current ADA/PROWAG accessibility standards, often upgrading entire blocks
- **Seasonal Constraints**: Northern climate cities have limited paving seasons and must manage pothole repairs during winter freeze-thaw cycles
