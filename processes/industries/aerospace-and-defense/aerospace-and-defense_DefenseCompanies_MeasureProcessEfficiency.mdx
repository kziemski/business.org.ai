# Measure process efficiency

## Overview

Evaluating the efficiency of the organization's processes. Adapt evaluation to the activities and business components being measured. (Unlike process Measure staff productivity [11078], process Measures outcomes [11076] [e.g., how much of X is produced within X time period].) Measure a manufacturing process, for example, according to number of units produced per FTE or number of "high quality" units produced. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
