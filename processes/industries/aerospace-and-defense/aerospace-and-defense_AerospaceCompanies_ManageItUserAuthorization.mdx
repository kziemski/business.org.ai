# Manage IT user authorization

## Overview

Managing the process of authorizing IT users to access applications, systems, IT components, or networks by associating user rights. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
