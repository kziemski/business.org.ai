# Establish storage management constraints

## Overview

Determining potential constraints for physical storage and retrieval of components or products in a storage facility within a certain timeframe. Consider factors such as the building shape, height, capacity, door locations, lift equipment, automation, etc. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
