# Report to external auditors

## Overview

Reporting to external auditors. This process requires the organization to report to external auditors about the regulations for any critical data that the organization is holding. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
