# Create customer journey maps

## Overview

Creating a story of the customer's experience: from initial contact, through the process of engagement and into a long-term relationship. The goal is to teach organization about the customer. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
