# Manage engineering change orders

## Overview

Coordinating the implementation of requests for component changes, equipment repairs, and the optimization of production/delivery processes. Oversee Request an engineering change [11418] in the production and/or delivery operations. Coordinate with the concerned divisions that will implement these changes. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
