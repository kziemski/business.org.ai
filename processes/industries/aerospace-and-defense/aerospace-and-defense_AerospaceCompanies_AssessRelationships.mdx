# Assess relationships

## Overview

Ascertaining how the business entity relates to all levels of government. Identify areas that needs further growth and resources to foster those relationships. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
