# Develop strategic initiatives based on business/customer value

## Overview

Creating a statement of the organization's direction based on what is considered "value" to the customer or business. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
