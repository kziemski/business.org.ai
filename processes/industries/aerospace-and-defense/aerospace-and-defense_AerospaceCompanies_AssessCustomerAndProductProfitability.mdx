# Assess customer and product profitability

## Overview

Studying product demand and targeted customer preferences. Study customers' demands or preferences after deducting the cost of delivering the final product. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
