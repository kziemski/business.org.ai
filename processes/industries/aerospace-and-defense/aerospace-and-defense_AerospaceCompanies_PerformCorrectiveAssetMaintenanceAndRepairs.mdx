# Perform corrective asset maintenance and repairs

## Overview

Repairing or correcting faults that occur with an asset. This could be a break or other repairable damage. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
