# Manage requirements for partners

## Overview

Associating with partners to simplify and increase the efficiency of the process. Take care of the specific inventory requirements for all the partners. Ensure the timely dispatch and receiving of the inventory. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
