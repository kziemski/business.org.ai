# Identify strategic priorities

## Overview

Creating a statement of the organization's direction to guide decision making around the allocation of resources. Provide a focus on the organization's overarching goals to ensure coherent and considered action. Strategic objectives are ranked by their importance in achieving the strategic goals. All subsequent operational or tactical planning and resource allocation is based on strategic priorities Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
