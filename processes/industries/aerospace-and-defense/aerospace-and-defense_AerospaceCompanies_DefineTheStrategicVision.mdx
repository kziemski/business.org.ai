# Define the strategic vision

## Overview

Developing goals to define organizations vision. Define and document ideas, direction, and activities which enable the organization to reach these goals. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
