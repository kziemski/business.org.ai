# Ensure that each business unit/function follows the enterprise risk reporting process

## Overview

Checking the reporting process of each business unit's/function's options and activities to improve opportunities and lessen threats. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
