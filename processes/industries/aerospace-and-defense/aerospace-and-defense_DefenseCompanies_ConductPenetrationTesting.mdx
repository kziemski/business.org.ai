# Conduct penetration testing

## Overview

Conduct penetration testing (pen test) through an authorized stimulated attack to identify security weakness in an IT environment by evaluating the system or network with various harmful techniques. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
