# Design the relationships between organizational units

## Overview

Fleshing out the connections and dependencies among the various units of the organization. Delineate the relationship among business units or process frameworks within the organization, in terms of activities, synergies, and shared resources and responsibilities. Formalize relationships among business units so that any mutual coherence is clearly understood and can be attended to. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
