# Manage product/material classification

## Overview

Controlling the details of the product and the input materials. Manage classification throughout the production process, and for future accessibility to new product development or product enrichment. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
