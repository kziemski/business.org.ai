# Develop IT compliance, risk, and security strategy

## Overview

Ensuring that the organization effectively manages risk. Develop rules and standards for robust IT operations, manage risk, and adopt measures to protect integrity, confidentiality, and security of IT assets. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
