# Verify effectiveness of inventory control and quality

## Overview

Analyzing issues related to quality as perceived by customers. Follow parts trends to identify issues with FIFO consumption of parts, the delivery of documented parts, defective or non-working parts, and the compliancy of parts that are configured for the aircraft. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
