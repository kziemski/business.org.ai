# Set up and maintain service delivery governance and management system

## Overview

Providing a system for which to manage customer needs and a structure for which to facilitate service delivery to fulfill those needs. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
