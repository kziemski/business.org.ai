# Confirm delivery according to contract terms

## Overview

Confirming that the organization has satisfied all terms of the delivery contract set forth in collaboration between the organization and customer. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
