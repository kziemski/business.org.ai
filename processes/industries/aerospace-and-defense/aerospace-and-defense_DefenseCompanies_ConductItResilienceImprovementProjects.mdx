# Conduct IT resilience improvement projects

## Overview

Conducting projects to improve the strategy and process for rapidly adapting to any threat in IT. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
