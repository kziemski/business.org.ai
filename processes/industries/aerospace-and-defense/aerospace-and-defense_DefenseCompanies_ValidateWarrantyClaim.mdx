# Validate warranty claim

## Overview

Ensuring that the claim falls within the parameters of the warranty in question. After validation is made, the claim must be investigated. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
