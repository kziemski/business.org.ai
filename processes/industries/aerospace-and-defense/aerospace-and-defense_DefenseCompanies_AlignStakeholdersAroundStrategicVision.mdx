# Align stakeholders around strategic vision

## Overview

Orienting those entities, associated with the organization that have a direct bearing on its operations and output, in a way that advances its strategic vision. Map all stakeholders in strategic configurations, within the architectural layout of the marketplace, and position the organization relative to them. (This exercise is undertaken by senior strategy personnel, drawing upon the process Define a business concept and long-term vision [17040].) Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
