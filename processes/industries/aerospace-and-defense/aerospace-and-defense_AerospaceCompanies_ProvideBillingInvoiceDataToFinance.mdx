# Provide billing / invoice data to finance

## Overview

Invoicing customer when repaired part is returned to sender. The customer is invoiced using Invoice customer [10743].  The invoice is created using the materials and labor data, and any warranty dependent costs that have been identified and possibly excluded. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
