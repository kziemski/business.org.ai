# Communicate quality specifications

## Overview

Communicating the desired quality specifications to the manufacturing units, as well as the distribution centers, to avoid any misunderstanding or misinterpretation. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.

## Industry-Specific Considerations

- Compliance with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 requirements must be integrated into every step of this process
- Long product lifecycles (20-30 years) directly impacts how this process is designed and executed
- Delivery operations require coordination with specialized Aerospace and Defense supply chain partners

## Key Metrics

- **Schedule Performance Index (SPI)**: Ratio of earned value to planned value, indicating schedule adherence
- **Quality Escape Rate**: Number of defects discovered after process completion per total units
- **First Pass Yield (FPY)**: Percentage of units passing quality inspection on first attempt, critical for process efficiency
