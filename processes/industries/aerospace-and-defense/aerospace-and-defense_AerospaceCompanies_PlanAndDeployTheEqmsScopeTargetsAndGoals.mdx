# Plan and deploy the EQMS scope, targets, and goals

## Overview

Establishing and effectively deploying the scope, targets, and goals of EQMS. Define the role of EQMS through nonconformance/corrective and preventive action, compliance/audit management, risk management, failure mode and effects analysis, and statistical process control. Implement EQMS into operational activities. Define the goals and objectives that are to be achieved by the EQMS. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
