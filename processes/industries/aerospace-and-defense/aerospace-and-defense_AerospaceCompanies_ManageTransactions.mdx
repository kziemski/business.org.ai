# Manage transactions

## Overview

Managing any transfer of funds in the course of conducting cross-border trades or investments, including conversion across currencies. Find the most suitable alternative for making payments, while saving taxes and avoiding any unwarranted regulation, with the objective of protecting capital. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
