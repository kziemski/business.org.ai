# Identify and review customer touchpoints

## Overview

Creating methods to gauge customer experiences, expectations, and suggestions. Review both liked and disliked areas of product/services to be delivered. Evaluate touchpoints based on the nature of product/service in the market e.g., billboards, web sites, direct mail, service calls, etc. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
