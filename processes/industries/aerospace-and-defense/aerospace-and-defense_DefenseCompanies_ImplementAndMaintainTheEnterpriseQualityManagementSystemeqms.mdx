# Implement and maintain the enterprise quality management system (EQMS)

## Overview

Establishing and administering the software that manages content and business processes for quality and compliance across the value chain. Define the quality strategy. Plan and deploy the EQMS scope, targets, and goals. Identify core process controls and metric. Develop EQMS governance. Assess the performance of EQMS. Encourage improvements in EQMS. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
