# Develop IT support service sourcing strategy

## Overview

Developing a strategy for sourcing resources to support users of IT services and solutions. Establish sources that will make use of e-mail, live support software online, or a tool where users can log a call or incident in order to retrieve IT support. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
