# Determine IT service/solution development

## Overview

Determining the development of IT service/solution. Analyze the pros and cons of IT service/solution and it's methods on the basis of their cost effectiveness and development value. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
