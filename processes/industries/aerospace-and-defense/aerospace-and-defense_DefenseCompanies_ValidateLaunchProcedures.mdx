# Validate launch procedures

## Overview

Verifying the measures/processes/techniques through systems and tools involved in the introduction of products and services. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
