# Evaluate existing products/services

## Overview

Examining the brands owned and products offered in the market. Determine the relative position of the existing products/brands in the marketplace. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
