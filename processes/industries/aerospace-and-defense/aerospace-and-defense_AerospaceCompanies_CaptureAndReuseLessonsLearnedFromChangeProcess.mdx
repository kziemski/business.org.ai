# Capture and reuse lessons learned from change process

## Overview

Documenting and standardizing insights gleaned and the knowledge acquired from studying the change process already implemented. Create case studies/best practices guides from the process of implementing change. Include experienced personnel. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
