# Conduct performance assessments

## Overview

Measuring, researching, and recording the performance of people, processes, mechanisms, or other areas of the business that the organization wants to benchmark or track. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
