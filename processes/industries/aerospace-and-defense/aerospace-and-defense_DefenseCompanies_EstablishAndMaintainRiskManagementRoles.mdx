# Establish and maintain risk management roles

## Overview

Determine and maintain roles that are specialized in each risk areas and coordinating all risk management activities for IT function with due escalation structure. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
