# Understand IT customer communities

## Overview

Interacting with IT customers to understand the IT needs through a collaborative community through involvement, connection, and informed communication. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
