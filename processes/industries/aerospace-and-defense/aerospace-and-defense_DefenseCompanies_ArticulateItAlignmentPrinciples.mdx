# Articulate IT alignment principles

## Overview

Systematic approach to clearly communicate and operate the usage of information technology as it relates to business objectives. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
