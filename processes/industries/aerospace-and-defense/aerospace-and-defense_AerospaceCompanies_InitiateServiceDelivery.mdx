# Initiate service delivery

## Overview

Collaborating with the customer to understand service needs. Review, understand, and modify the delivery scope with the organization needs of the customer in mind. Confirm readiness and identify, select, and assign resources. Plan for service delivery. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
