# Manage labor relations

## Overview

Managing labor relations, the collective bargaining process, and the relationships between the labor and management. Take care of employee grievances. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
