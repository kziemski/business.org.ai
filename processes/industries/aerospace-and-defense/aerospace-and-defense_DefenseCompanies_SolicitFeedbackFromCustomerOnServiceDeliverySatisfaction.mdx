# Solicit feedback from customer on service delivery satisfaction

## Overview

Engaging the customer post delivery to gauge the effectiveness of services rendered in order to improve on key delivery functions going forward. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
