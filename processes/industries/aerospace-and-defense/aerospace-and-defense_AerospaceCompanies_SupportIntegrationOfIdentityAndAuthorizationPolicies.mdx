# Support integration of identity and authorization policies

## Overview

Create and implement policies that integrate authorization policies with authorized profiles of users meant to access network resources. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
