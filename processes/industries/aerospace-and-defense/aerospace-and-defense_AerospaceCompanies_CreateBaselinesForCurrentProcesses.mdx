# Create baselines for current processes

## Overview

Establishing baselines that provide standards for assessing performance levels and allow for a relational benchmarking of current processes. Undertake a survey of archival performance records, conducted in conjunction by the management and the operations personnel. Take into account the organization's internal objectives, particularly for process improvement and enhancement. Understand industry best practices. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
