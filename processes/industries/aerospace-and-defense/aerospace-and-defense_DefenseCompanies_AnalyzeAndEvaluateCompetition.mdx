# Analyze and evaluate competition

## Overview

Assessing the competitive forces in the marketplace that could potentially affect the organization. Analyze various aspects of business competition including competing firms. Aggregate competitive intelligence, create benchmarks to juxtapose processes and performance metrics, and inject crucial information about the competition into management models to synthesize insights. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
