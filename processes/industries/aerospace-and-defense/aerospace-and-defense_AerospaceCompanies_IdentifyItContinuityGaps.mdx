# Identify IT continuity gaps

## Overview

Identifying the limitations of the IT organization's ability to remediate disruptions in IT services. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
