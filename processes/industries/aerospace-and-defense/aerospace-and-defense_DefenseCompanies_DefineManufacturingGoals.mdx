# Define manufacturing goals

## Overview

Creating quantifiable strategic objectives for each manufacturing segment in conjunction with sales projections. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.

## Industry-Specific Considerations

- Compliance with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 requirements must be integrated into every step of this process
- Long product lifecycles (20-30 years) directly impacts how this process is designed and executed
- Delivery operations require coordination with specialized Aerospace and Defense supply chain partners
- Aerospace and Defense organizations must balance government contracting and classified programs with operational efficiency

## Key Metrics

- **Cost Performance Index (CPI)**: Ratio of earned value to actual cost, indicating budget efficiency
- **Schedule Performance Index (SPI)**: Ratio of earned value to planned value, indicating schedule adherence
