# Communicate strategic initiatives to business units and stakeholders

## Overview

Establishing procedures for communications within the organization which creates the road map for successful understanding of strategic initiatives for both business units and stakeholders (internal and external). Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
