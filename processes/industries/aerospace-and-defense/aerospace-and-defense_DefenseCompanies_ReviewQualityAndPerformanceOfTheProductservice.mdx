# Review quality and performance of the product/service

## Overview

Identifying the quality and performance of the product/service delivered to customers. Analyze data from the customer feedback, audits, measures of customer satisfaction (such as product quality complaints and recalls), and organizational policies on delivery. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.

## Industry-Specific Considerations

- Compliance with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 requirements must be integrated into every step of this process
- Long product lifecycles (20-30 years) directly impacts how this process is designed and executed
- Product and service development must account for precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management requirements
- Aerospace and Defense organizations must balance government contracting and classified programs with operational efficiency

## Key Metrics

- **First Pass Yield (FPY)**: Percentage of units passing quality inspection on first attempt, critical for process efficiency
- **On-Time Delivery (OTD)**: Percentage of deliverables completed within committed timeframes
- **Cost Performance Index (CPI)**: Ratio of earned value to actual cost, indicating budget efficiency
