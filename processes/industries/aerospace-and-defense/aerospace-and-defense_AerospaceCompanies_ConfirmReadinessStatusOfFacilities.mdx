# Confirm readiness status of facilities

## Overview

Confirming final assembly line readiness.  Parts and components are built,  then shipped to be assembled in one facility called the final assembly line. Ensure that all elements involved in the generation of the product are complete, tested and ready for operation before the start of production. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
