# Enhance/Modify existing KM approaches

## Overview

Leveraging KM evaluations and identified gap to enhance existing approaches. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
