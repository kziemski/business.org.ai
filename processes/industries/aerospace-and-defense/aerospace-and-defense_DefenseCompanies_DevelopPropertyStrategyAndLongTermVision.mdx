# Develop property strategy and long term vision

## Overview

Strategizing a long-term vision for managing properties. Prepare strategies and a long-term vision for managing purchased/retained properties. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
