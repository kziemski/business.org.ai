# Manage IT customer relationships

## Overview

Managing the IT relationship with its customers by systematically coordinating interactions over multiple touch points on a regular basis. Coordinate the IT's efforts to reach out to its customers, which include emails, social-media interactions, newsletters, and direct conversations. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
