# Monitor international rates

## Overview

Forecasting and monitoring changes in foreign currency value or interest rates around the world that play an important role in the organization. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
