# Assess SLA compliance

## Overview

Gather data from each service target defined in an SLA for a time segment or review period to evaluate an overall performance percentage. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
