# Identify project resource requirements

## Overview

Identifying the prerequisites of business projects. Identify the people with appropriate and applicable skills and competencies. Locate resources such as capital, facilities, equipment, material, and information required to accomplish the objectives of a specific project. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
