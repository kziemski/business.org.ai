# Manage portfolio, program, and project

## Overview

Managing investments, holdings, products, businesses, and brands, along with the related projects that together constitute a program. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
