# Identify requirements for product/service design/development partners

## Overview

Determining essential elements for collaborators involved in blueprint/development of product/service. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
