# Test against quality plan

## Overview

Examining the quality of organizational processes. Conduct tests. Collect information and data. Record the results of these tests. Determine the dispositions of the test results. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
