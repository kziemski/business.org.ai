# Triage IT service delivery incidents

## Overview

Sorting the incidents of IT service delivery in certain order so that the services could be delivered based on the criticality. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
