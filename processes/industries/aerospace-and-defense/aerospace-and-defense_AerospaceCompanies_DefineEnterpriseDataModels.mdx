# Define enterprise data models

## Overview

Define different ways of representation, usage, and identification of data with independent or interdependent sources across the organization. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
