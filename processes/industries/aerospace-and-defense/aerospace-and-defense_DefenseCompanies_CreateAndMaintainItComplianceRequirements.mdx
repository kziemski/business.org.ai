# Create and maintain IT compliance requirements

## Overview

Develop and maintain IT compliance standards. Maintaining requirements set forth by such directives as GRCP, PMI RMP, CGRC, CGEIT, CRMA. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
