# Control quality of returned parts

## Overview

Implement a checks and balances system to verify that returned parts meet acceptable quality standards to determine appropriate disposition activity. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
