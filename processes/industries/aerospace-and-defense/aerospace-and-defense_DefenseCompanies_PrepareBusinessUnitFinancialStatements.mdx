# Prepare business unit financial statements

## Overview

Making reports of subsidiaries units to show profits generated from them. Prepare financial statements (balance sheets, income statements, cash flow statements and statements of shareholders' equity) for a single unit of a business. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
