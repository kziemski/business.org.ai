# Match resource demand with capacity, skills, and capabilities

## Overview

Matching demand with skills and capability. Enlisting suppliers and partners to help with demand when needed. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
