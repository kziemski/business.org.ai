# Translate customer service requirements into logistics requirements

## Overview

Determining the requirements for managing the flow of things between the point of origin and the point of consumption by assessing the service requirements of the customers. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
