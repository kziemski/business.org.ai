# Research technologies to innovate IT services and solutions

## Overview

Systematically investigating and studying materials and sources relevant to the IT function. Reach meaningful insights and conclusions in the form of new ideas and innovation for delivering IT services and solutions. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management.
