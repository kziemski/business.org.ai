# Conduct service delivery/project review and evaluate success

## Overview

Reviewing the entire service delivery process to evaluate the success of the project from beginning to end. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
