## Overview

Developing service/solution along with creating a strategy that provides a base for delivering service/solution aligned with overall business needs. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management.
