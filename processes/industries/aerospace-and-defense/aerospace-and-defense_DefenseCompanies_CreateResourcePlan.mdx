# Create resource plan

## Overview

Creating a plan to ensure that all resources are available to carry out services required for the customer. This can include physical resources and personnel. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
