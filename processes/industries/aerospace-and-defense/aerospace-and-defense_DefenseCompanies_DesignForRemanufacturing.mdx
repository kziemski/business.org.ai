## Overview

This process enables DefenseCompanies to design for re-manufacturing within the Aerospace and Defense industry. Given the industry's focus on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management, this process must be executed with attention to FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 compliance requirements. Effective implementation ensures operational excellence and supports the organization's strategic objectives in a competitive marketplace.
