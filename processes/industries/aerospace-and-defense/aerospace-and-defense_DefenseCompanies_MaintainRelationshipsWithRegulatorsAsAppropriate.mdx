# Maintain relationships with regulators as appropriate

## Overview

Developing and preserving relationships with the regulators, without compromising the legal basis of the relationship. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
