# Define process steps for controls (or integration points)

## Overview

Establishing the steps for developing quality controls. Conduct Alpha testing. Have the product team conduct rework. Send the product/service for Beta testing, and carry on rework as needed. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
