# Establish baseline metrics

## Overview

Establishing baselines that provide standards for assessing performance. Create metrics and KPI's for various functions/processes/activities based on organizational goals. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
