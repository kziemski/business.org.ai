# Develop transformation roadmap

## Overview

Creating a blueprint for execution of IT transformation from the existing state to the planned organizational structure based on the value proposition and projected business growth. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
