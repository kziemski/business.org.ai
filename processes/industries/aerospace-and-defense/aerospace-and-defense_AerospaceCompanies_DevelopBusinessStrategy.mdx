# Develop business strategy

## Overview

Developing an organization's mission statement, strategy, and business design. Create a concise statement that clearly articulates the mission of the organization, outlining practicable targets to Establish a strategic vision [10020]. Delineate strategic options by matching these alternatives with the organization's internal capabilities. Create an organizational design, and identify goals by developing strategies at the functional and process levels. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
