# Manage IT requirements

## Overview

Managing the IT requirements for business objectives. Identify the requirements of hardware and software equipment to store, retrieve, transmit, and manipulate data related to business operations. Consider factors such as functional, design, growth phases, and delivery schedule while managing IT requirements. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
