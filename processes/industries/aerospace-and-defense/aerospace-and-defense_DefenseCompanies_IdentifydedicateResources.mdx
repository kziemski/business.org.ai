# Identify/Dedicate resources

## Overview

Identifying and dedicating the resources for managing remediation efforts. Discern the resources needed for remediation efforts. Dispense with resources in a sound and well-reasoned manner. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
