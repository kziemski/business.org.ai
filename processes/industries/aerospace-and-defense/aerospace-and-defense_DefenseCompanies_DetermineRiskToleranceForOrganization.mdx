# Determine risk tolerance for organization

## Overview

Recognizing the organization's tolerance for risk, given risk-return trade-offs for one or more anticipated and predictable consequences. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
