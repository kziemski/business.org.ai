# Manage service delivery development and direction

## Overview

Providing guidance of resources to ensure that the development and direction of service delivery is in line with customer needs. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
