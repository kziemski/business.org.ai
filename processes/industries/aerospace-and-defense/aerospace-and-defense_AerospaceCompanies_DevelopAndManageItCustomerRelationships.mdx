# Develop and manage IT customer relationships

## Overview

Creating and administering relationships with IT customers. Understanding customer needs including high-level business requirements for IT transformation. Plan for and communicate IT services along with establishing IT service levels, providing transformation guidance, and performance analysis that foster IT customer relationships. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
