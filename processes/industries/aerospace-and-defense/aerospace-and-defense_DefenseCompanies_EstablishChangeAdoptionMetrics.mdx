# Establish change adoption metrics

## Overview

Establishing a system or standard of measurement for measuring the adoption of the change. Consider activities such as the number of people who have adopted the change, how quickly have they adopted, number of unique adopters, and adopters by teams/divisions. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
