# Manage system and process integration

## Overview

Ensuring that customer required work from different business units, partners, and/or processes and systems they need to access are consistent and fully integrated. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
