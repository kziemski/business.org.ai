# Agree on warranty responsibilities with suppliers

## Overview

Negotiating with manufacturers and dealers the obligations of each party in upholding warranties. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
