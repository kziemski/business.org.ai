# Respond to IT information security and network breaches

## Overview

Address any form of unauthorized network breach such as unauthorized access or usage of data, applications, services, networks, and/or devices. Identify the root cause and take corrective measures to resolve the breach. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
