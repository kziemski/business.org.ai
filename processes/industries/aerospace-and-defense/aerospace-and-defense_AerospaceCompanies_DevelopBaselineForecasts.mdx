# Develop baseline forecasts

## Overview

Identifying the demand anticipated for the organization's services. Estimate future demand for services using historical data, analysis of the market environment, and external data. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
