# Review and monitor IT physical environment security controls

## Overview

Identifying and examining security controls for physical environment of information technology such as business facilities, equipment, and resources. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
