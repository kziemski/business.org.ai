# Manage IT user identity and authorization

## Overview

The process of identifying, authenticating, and authorizing IT users to have access to applications, systems, IT components, or networks by associating user rights and restrictions with established identities. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
