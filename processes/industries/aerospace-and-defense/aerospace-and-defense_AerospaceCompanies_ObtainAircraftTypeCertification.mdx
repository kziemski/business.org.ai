# Obtain aircraft type certification

## Overview

Obtaining aircraft regulatory certification in country of origin.  Obtain regulatory authority in the country where first-time aircraft builds will take place. Ensure the design work that has been completed complies with the regulatory requirements for the aircraft type being built. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
