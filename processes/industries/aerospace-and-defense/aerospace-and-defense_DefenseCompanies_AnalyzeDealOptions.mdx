# Analyze deal options

## Overview

Examining various options shortlisted for assimilating new entities into the organization or dissociating from it. Undertake a piecemeal and comprehensive consideration of each option identified for acquisition, merger, de-merger, and divestment. Consider intangible and non-material aspects of the entities involved and synergic aspects. Consider the assistance of specialist professional services. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
