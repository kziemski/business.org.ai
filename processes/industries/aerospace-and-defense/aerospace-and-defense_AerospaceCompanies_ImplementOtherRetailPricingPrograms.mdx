# Implement other retail pricing programs

## Overview

Determining the optimum consumer pricing for each product or service at the point of sale, based on production and distribution costs and estimated sales volume. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
