# Determine and document warranty policies

## Overview

Establishing warranty policies to assure customers that the company will guarantee its warranties that it issues. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
