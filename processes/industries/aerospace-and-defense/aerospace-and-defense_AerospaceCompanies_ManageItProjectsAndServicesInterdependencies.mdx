# Manage IT projects and services interdependencies

## Overview

Manage capabilities required for the successful delivery of information technology projects, which, by extension, affect the success of the overall IT services. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
