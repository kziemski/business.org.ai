# Manage customer meetings/workshops

## Overview

Arranging and leading meetings, seminars, workshops and training events with customers to educate them about current offerings, best practices and technological advances to provide more value to customers and to increase their attrition and loyalty. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
