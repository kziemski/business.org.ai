# Develop business models

## Overview

Creating an economic model that describes the goals of an organization and the business processes needed to achieve those goals. This involves information gathering, securing necessary approvals and authorizations, integrating with preexisting models, including the general business concept [10002] and business strategy [10015], and formally accepting the model as the basis for organization's day-to-day operations. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
