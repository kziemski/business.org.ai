# Manage enterprise quality

## Overview

Managing organizational attributes that are closely associated with the quality of output. Determine the quality requirements. Evaluate the correspondence between the quality performance and requirements. Manage non-conformance activities. Ensure implementation and maintenance of the enterprise quality management system. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
