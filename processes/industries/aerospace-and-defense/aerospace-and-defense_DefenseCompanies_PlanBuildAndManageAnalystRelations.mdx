# Plan, build, and manage analyst relations

## Overview

Creating and maintaining long-term relations with analysts. Involve analysts in strategy and product decisions in order to elicit valuable information. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
