# Establish and validate IT value criteria

## Overview

Create and certify the standards to determine the value of the investments, projects, and activities of IT function for the overall business objectives. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
