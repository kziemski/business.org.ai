# Derive security requirements for products and services

## Overview

Implementing security requirements through authentication and encryption of CE device data stream.  Utilize security measures such as cryptographic protocols and hardware security (smart cards). Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
