# Develop and maintain service/solution architectures

## Overview

Creating and maintaining a services and solutions architecture over a network that can be revised as needed or even eliminated in case of inefficiencies. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
