# Define IT change/release standards

## Overview

Establishing guidelines for the changed/released IT services and solutions to meet business objectives with optimum utilization. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
