# Compile aircraft component change and configuration management data

## Overview

Documenting  components that have been changed.  Record the new serial number, the modification standard of the part being installed, number of cycles remaining on the new part, service bulletins or modifications that need to be made to the component that have not yet been made, and when it is in plan for those changes to be made. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
