# Develop merger/demerger/acquisition/exit strategy

## Overview

Defining a strategy for corporate development. Include providing a framework for evaluating merger and acquisition candidates; and planning for a value creation through merging/demerging with a company, acquiring a company, or exiting from an already merged/acquired company. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
