# Manage returns; manage reverse logistics

## Overview

Managing the process of a customer taking previously purchased merchandise back to the retailer. Remit a refund in the original form of payment, exchange for another item (identical or different), or a store credit. Perform reverse logistics and the repair/refurbishment. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
