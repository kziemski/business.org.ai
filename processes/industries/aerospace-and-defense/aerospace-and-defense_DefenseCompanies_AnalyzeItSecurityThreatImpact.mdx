# Analyze IT security threat impact

## Overview

Analyzing the impact of threats to critical IT assets across different departments and functions in the organization in terms of quantifiable results. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
