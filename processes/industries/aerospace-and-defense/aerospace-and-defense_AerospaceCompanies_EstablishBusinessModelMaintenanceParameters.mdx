# Establish business model maintenance parameters

## Overview

Determining the timeline, procedures and responsibilities for reviewing the business model and for updating it to best serve the organization. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
