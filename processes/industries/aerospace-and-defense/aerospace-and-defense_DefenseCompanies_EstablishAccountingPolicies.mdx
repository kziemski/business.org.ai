# Establish accounting policies

## Overview

Establishing policies and procedures to prepare financial statements, including methods, measurement systems, and procedures for providing disclosures. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
