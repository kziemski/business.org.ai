# Refine business unit strategies in support of company strategy

## Overview

Evaluating existing business unit strategy based on the company's strategy and eliminate unwanted/unnecessary resources/elements and re-consider necessary resources to meet the overall company's strategy. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
