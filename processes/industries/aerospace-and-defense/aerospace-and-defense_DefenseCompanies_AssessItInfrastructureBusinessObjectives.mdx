# Assess IT infrastructure business objectives

## Overview

Assessing the goals and objectives of IT infrastructure and how it contributes to the overall business objectives. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
