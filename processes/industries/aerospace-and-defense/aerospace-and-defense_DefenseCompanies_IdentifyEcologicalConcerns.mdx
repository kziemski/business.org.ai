# Identify ecological concerns

## Overview

Identifying changes in ecological ecosystems that can be directly or indirectly detrimental to the organization. Analyze ecological factors within the immediate ecosystem for near to middle-term impact. Analyze the ecology, at large, to get a sense of long-term shifts and concerns. Gather analyses from research publications. Speak to subject matter experts. Engage with advocacy groups, lawyers, journalists, and the active populations. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
