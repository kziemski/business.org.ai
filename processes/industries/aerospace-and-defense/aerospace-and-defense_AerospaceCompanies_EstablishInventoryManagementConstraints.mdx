# Establish inventory management constraints

## Overview

Determining any problems that might be faced while managing inventory. Identify problems and possible issues in managing the warehousing of the raw materials, spares, and other items of inventory. Take stock of inventory needs, and determine the exact quantity of the inventory needed in the near future. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
