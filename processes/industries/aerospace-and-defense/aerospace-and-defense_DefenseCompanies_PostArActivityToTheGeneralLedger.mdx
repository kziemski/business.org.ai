# Post AR activity to the general ledger

## Overview

Preparing the general ledger for account receivables from journals. Place all journal entries related to accounts receivables in the general ledger accounts of a business. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
