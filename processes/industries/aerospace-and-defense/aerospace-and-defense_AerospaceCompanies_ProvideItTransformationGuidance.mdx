# Provide IT transformation guidance

## Overview

Understanding the necessity of IT transformation for the business. Collect and analyze customer requirements. Identify opportunities and prioritize outcomes. Develop and support business case for transformation. Develop transformation plan and roadmap. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
