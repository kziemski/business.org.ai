# Maintain chart of accounts

## Overview

Preparing trial balance account from general ledgers. List all accounts used in the general ledger. Alter accounts according to business requirements. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
