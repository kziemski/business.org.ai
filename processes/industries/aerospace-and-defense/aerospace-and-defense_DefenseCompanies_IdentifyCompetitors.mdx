# Identify competitors

## Overview

Identifying your competitors, their service and/or product. Evaluating competitors strategies to determine their strengths and weaknesses relative to those of your own product or service. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
