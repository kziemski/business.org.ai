# Evaluate breadth and depth of organizational structure

## Overview

Evaluating the structural makeup of the organization, including pertinent features of and associated synergies among constituent elements. Examine the organization's architectural framework, paying close attention to the individual elements, the relations among them, and the conjoint and co-acting forces therein. Analyze functions and processes, as well as work-related positions. Scrutinize various relevant characteristics associated with each of them, depending upon the scale, scope, and type of organization. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
