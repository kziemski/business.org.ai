# Monitor and control portfolio

## Overview

Overseeing and administering the business portfolio of the organization. Monitor all activities related to investments, holdings, products, businesses, and brands by effectively monitoring and supervising these activities. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
