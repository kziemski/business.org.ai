# Develop role analysis and activity diagrams for key processes

## Overview

Creating an understanding of the fit between job roles and organizational processes in order to properly place personnel. Deconstruct key processes into constituent activities, and examine job-related roles. Take cues from Develop role activity diagrams to assess hand-off activity [10051]. Map appropriate positions against these important processes, which in turn expedite the deployment of staff members. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
