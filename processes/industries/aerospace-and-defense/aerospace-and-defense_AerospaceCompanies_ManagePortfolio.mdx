# Manage portfolio

## Overview

Managing the business portfolio of the organization, including investments, holdings, products, businesses, and brands. Establish a portfolio strategy. Define portfolio governance. Monitor and control the portfolio. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
