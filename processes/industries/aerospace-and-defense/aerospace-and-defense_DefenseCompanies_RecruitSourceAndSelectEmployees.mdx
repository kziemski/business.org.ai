# Recruit, source, and select employees

## Overview

Determining and handling employee requirements. Recruit or source the candidates as per the requirements. Screen and select the most appropriate candidates. Take care of the newly hired and re-hired employees. Maintain records of information for all applicants. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
