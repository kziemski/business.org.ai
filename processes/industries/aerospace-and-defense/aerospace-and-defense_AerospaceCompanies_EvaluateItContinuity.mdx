# Evaluate IT continuity

## Overview

Evaluating IT business needs and IT's ability to recover from internal or external threat exposure. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
