# Analyze business unit strategies

## Overview

Assessing the performance of a business unit against set organizational goals which are based on pre-defined metrics collected through various business unit operations. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
