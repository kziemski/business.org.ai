# Determine business value for each strategic priority

## Overview

Establishing a standard measure of value to determine the business worth for each of the Identify strategic priorities [19975]. List the effectiveness and utility for every important strategic element based on the benefit it adds to the business. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
