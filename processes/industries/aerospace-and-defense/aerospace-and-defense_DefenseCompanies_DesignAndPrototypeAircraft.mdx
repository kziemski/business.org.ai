# Design and prototype aircraft

## Overview

Creating and evaluating new products/services, with the objective of readying them for production. Undertake the design and development of new products/services. Allocate resources to these projects, create specifications, ensure compliance, conduct prototyping, test, and conduct on-boarding of supply-chain stakeholders. Enlist R&D staff, with the involvement of functional heads, in this iterative process. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
