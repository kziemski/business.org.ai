# Compile and communicate internal and regulatory compliance reports

## Overview

Submitting compliance reports to regulatory agencies. These reports can be made to environmental, securities, or human resources agencies as stipulated by the local governing body. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
