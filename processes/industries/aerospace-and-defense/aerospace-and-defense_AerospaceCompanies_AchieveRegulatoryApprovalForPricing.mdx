# Achieve regulatory approval for pricing

## Overview

Obtaining internal price approvals and governmental approvals that are required for licensed products and for products that can be reimbursed by the government. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
