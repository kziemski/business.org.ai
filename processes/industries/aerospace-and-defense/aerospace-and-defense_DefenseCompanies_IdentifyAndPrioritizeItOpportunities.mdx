# Identify and prioritize IT opportunities

## Overview

Identifying IT opportunities on the basis of collection and analysis of IT customer requirements, then prioritize the identified IT opportunities on the basis of their importance. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
