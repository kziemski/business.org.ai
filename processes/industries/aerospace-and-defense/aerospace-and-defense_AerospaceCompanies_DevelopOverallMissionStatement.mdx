# Develop overall mission statement

## Overview

Establishing an overarching, compact statement that concisely underscores the mission of the organization. Define and communicate a clear and succinct mission statement, which encapsulates how the organization aims to proceed in order to Establish a strategic vision [10020]. Solicit critical inputs from senior management and strategy executives, and collaborate with marketing or personnel from allied functions. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
