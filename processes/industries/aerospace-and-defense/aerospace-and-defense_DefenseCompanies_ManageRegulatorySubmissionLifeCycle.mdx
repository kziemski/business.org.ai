# Manage regulatory submission life cycle

## Overview

Determine and follow the timely input and update of regulatory information by assessing reforms, regulatory policies, and guidelines. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
