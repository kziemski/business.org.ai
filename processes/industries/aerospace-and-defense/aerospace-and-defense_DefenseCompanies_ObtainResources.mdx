# Obtain resources

## Overview

Gathering resources needed to complete all construction work. Verify that all resources have the proper training and skills to perform the work. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
