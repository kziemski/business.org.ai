# Develop change management plans

## Overview

Creating a detailed structure summary for the purposes of managing the change. Demonstrate the reasons for the change. Define the type and scope of change. Form a change management team. Create a communication plan. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
