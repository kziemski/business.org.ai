# Negotiate and establish service level agreements

## Overview

Establish a service level agreement, which is a negotiated agreement designed to create a common understanding about services, priorities, and responsibilities. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
