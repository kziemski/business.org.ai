# Develop and manage human resources (HR) planning, policies, and strategies

## Overview

Creating strategies for the HR function. Create and implement strategies for managing the work force. Supervise and enhance the strategies, plans, and policies supporting the HR function. Developing models for managing competency levels of the HR of the organization. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
