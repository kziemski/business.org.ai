# Develop plan for improvements

## Overview

Devising a course of action to be taken to improve under-performing channels and to promote or expand channels that surpass expectations. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
