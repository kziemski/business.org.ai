# Identify business value drivers

## Overview

Determining key indicators or factors responsible for driving business value. Key business value drivers comprise of operational, financial, and sustainability drivers. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
