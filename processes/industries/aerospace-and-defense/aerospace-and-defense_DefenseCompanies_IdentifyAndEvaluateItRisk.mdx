# Identify and evaluate IT risk

## Overview

Developing a timely and continuous process to identify and evaluate activities that might hinder IT operations or an IT project's goals. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
