# Perform repair provider selection and interaction

## Overview

Selecting a supplier base from an existing population of suppliers of parts and services. This can involve integration of information from a variety of external sources such as Dun and Bradstreet, past supplier performance measures, etc. to develop a network of preferred suppliers. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
