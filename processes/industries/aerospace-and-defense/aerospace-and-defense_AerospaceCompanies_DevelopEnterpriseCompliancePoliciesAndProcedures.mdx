# Develop enterprise compliance policies and procedures

## Overview

Creating a standardized approach to ethics and compliance. Have a programmatic approach for compliance that focuses on the definite risks the organization faces. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
