# Monitor the regulatory environment for changing or emerging regulations

## Overview

Analyzing and overseeing the regulatory environment in order to spot any changing or emerging regulations. This process element calls upon the organization to monitor the regulatory environment for any new statutes, policies, and enactments issued by the respective government authorities or those which have been updated. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
