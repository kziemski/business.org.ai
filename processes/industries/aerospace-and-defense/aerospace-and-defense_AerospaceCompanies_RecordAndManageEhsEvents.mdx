# Record and manage EHS events

## Overview

Recording and managing all events and activities associated with complying with environmental, health and safety standards. Create event calendars. Assign funds. Educate employees. Conduct events. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
