# Investigate fraudulent claims

## Overview

Reviewing and assessing claims that contain deliberately incorrect information or that have been submitted with the goal to deceive the system. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
