# Define and execute business information lifecycle planning and control

## Overview

Develop and implement strategies to plan and manage the flow of an information system's data from creation and initial storage to the time when it becomes obsolete and deleted. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
