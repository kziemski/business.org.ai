# Document repair testing

## Overview

Confirming and documenting repair testing.  Once a component repair is complete, fully document the testing standards required, confirm testing has been carried out, and record the results. Document who did what, and where and when testing occurred. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
