# Implement promotional pricing programs

## Overview

Managing schemes that offer lower pricing for a limited time as a promotional and sales incentive when launching a new product or a service. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
