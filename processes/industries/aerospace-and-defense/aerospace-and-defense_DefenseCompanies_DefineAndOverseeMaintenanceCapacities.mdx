# Define and oversee maintenance capacities

## Overview

Managing the potential facilities capacities that are available for planning maintenance services. An example will include a hanger which can have one bay which can accommodate 1 B747 or B777, or 2 B737s or 2 A320s. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
