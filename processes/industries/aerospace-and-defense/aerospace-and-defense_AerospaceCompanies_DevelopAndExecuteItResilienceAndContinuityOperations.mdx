# Develop and execute IT resilience and continuity operations

## Overview

Create and execute a process to rapidly adapt and respond to any internal or external opportunity, demand, disruption, or threat in IT. Maintain continuous IT operations to protect employees, assets, and overall brand equity. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
