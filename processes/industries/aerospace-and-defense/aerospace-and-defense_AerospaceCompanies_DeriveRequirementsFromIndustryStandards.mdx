# Derive requirements from industry standards

## Overview

Complying with consumer electronic industry standards developed by the Consumer Technology Association (CTA). Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
