# Determine the customer value for each strategic priority

## Overview

Analyzing the value preposition; the value the customer gets from a product/services for each of your Identify strategic priorities [19975]. Customer value is the satisfaction a consumer feels after making a purchase for goods or services relative to what he/she must give up to receive them. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
