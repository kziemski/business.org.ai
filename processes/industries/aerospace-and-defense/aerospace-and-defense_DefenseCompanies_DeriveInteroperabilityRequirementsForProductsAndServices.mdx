# Derive interoperability requirements for products and services

## Overview

Determining the ability of products and services to work together, exchange and use information in a multi-vendor, multi-network, and multi-service environment. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
