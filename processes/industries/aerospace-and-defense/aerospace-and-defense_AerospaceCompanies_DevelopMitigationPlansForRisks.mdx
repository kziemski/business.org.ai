# Develop mitigation plans for risks

## Overview

Developing possibilities and arrangements to improve opportunities and reduce deviations to project objectives. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
