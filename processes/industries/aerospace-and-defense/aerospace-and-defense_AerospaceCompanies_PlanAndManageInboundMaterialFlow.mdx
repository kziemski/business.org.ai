# Plan and manage inbound material flow

## Overview

Working out the receipts of the inbound materials. Supervise the inbound delivery processes. Judge its efficiency. Take care of the deviations such as defective and incorrectly delivered products/materials. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
