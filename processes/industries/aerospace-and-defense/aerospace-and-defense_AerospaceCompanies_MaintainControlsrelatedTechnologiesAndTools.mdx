# Maintain controls-related technologies and tools

## Overview

Managing technologies and tools related to the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data in order to ensure the security of the organization's information. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
