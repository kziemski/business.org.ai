# Perform beyond economic repair (BER) assessment

## Overview

Performing BER assessment by the CRO provider when a damaged part arrives in a repair shop.  The purpose of this assessment is decide if the cost of repairing the part is less than the cost of scrapping it and buying a new one. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
