# Define customer service experience

## Overview

Communicating to the customer service resources what is expected when engaging the customer. Relate service level expectations to the workforce. Ensure positive customer experience. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
