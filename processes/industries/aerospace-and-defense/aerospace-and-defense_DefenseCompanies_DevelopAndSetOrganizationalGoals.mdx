# Develop and set organizational goals

## Overview

Developing overall goals for the organization that help in accomplishing its mission. Formulate organization-wide targets in the near to middle term, which will accumulate and propel the organization to realize its long-term objectives, as outlined in Develop an overall mission statement [10037]. Enlist business unit heads or equivalent personnel, in close collaboration with senior management executives. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
