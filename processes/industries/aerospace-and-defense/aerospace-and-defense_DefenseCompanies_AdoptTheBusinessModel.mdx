# Adopt the business model

## Overview

Consenting to a particular business model and formally accepting it to serve as the set of guiding principles in operating the company. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
