# Record outcome of sales process

## Overview

Completing all the paper-work associated with the sale of its products/services. Exchange any pertinent legal/financial information required for completing the sale, signing of a contract/work-order, and issuing copies of bills/invoices. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
