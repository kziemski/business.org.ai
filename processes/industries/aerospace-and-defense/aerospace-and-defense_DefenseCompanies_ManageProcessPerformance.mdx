# Manage process performance

## Overview

Evaluating and handling the performance of business processes. Provide training to process owners. Support the execution of business processes. Measure and report the performance of the business processes. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
