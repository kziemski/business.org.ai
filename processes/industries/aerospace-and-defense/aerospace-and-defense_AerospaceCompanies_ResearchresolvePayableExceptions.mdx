# Research/Resolve payable exceptions

## Overview

Resolving any atypical or inconsistent situation concerning payments to be made by the organization. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management.
