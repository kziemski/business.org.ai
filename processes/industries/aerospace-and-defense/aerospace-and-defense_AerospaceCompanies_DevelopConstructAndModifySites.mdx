# Develop, construct, and modify sites

## Overview

Creating, building, and renovating property sites. Construct the property. Make the necessary changes to the current property according to the requirements. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
