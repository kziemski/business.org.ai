# Develop, document, and maintain IT business continuity planning

## Overview

Develop, document, and maintain plans to ensure uninterrupted operations of critical IT services. Determine resources such as specialized personnel, equipment, support infrastructure, legal and financial aspects. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
