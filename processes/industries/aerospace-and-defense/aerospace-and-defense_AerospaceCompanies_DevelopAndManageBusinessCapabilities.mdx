# Develop and Manage Business Capabilities

## Overview

Performing activities by an organization that are fundamental to the successful operation of the organization, even across functions in a business. Capabilities defined in the PCF include business process management; portfolio, program, and project management; quality management; change management; benchmarking; environmental health and safety management; and knowledge management. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
