# Publish processes

## Overview

Disclosing the information available on business processes. Ensure the availability of the information regarding the business processes to all process team members, business stakeholders, and process owners. Use BPM software, as well as business process diagrams and documents that help depict the required information. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
