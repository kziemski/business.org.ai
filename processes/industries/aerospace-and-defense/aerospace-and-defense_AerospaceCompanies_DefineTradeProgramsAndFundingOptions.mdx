# Define trade programs and funding options

## Overview

Establishing business-to-business marketing campaigns and financial incentives for wholesalers, dealers, distributors and other intermediaries that the company uses to distribute its products or services. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
