# Confirm internal capabilities

## Overview

Verifying that the company has sufficient infrastructure and resources to deliver their offerings in a timely and cost-effective manner, and that it is able to scale up from the small-scale market samples, used for consumer testing, to the entire identified market segment. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
