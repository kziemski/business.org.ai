# Develop quality controls

## Overview

Developing controls for managing the quality of enterprise. Define the process steps for quality controls and the sampling plan. Identify the tools and methods to measure quality. Define the competencies required. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
