# Understand  IT customer environment

## Overview

Understanding the environment of staff dependent on information technology. Assess and evaluate services and solutions used by customers to conduct daily operations, and train new employees. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
