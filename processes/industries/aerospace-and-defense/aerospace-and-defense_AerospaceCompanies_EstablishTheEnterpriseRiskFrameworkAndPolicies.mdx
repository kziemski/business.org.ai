# Establish the enterprise risk framework and policies

## Overview

Creating an agenda for the rules and regulations of enterprise risk that deal with hazardous, financial, operational, and strategic risks. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
