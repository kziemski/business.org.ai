# Confirm internal prototype design / production capabilities

## Overview

Identifying capabilities required to launch new products.   Once capabilities are determined, procurement requirements can be established and procurement processes invoked.  Sourcing strategies are developed in Develop sourcing strategies [10277]. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
