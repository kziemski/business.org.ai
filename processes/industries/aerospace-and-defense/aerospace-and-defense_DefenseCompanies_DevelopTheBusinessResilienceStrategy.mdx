# Develop the business resilience strategy

## Overview

Creating a strategy for rapidly adapting to disturbances. Maintain continuous business processes and protecting employees, assets, and overall brand equity. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
