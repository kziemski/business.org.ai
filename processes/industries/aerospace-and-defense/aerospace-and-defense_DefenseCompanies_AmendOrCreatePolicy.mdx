# Amend or create policy

## Overview

Crafting a new framework of policies and procedures for deploying remediation efforts, or change existing policies and procedures. Adapt the policy structure to the context of the apposite national and international regulatory frameworks. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
