# Manage third party provider interactions

## Overview

Managing all interchanges with 3rd party providers of Service Partners. Ensure that all partners are aware of their commitments and that any modifications required can be quickly and easily completed. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
