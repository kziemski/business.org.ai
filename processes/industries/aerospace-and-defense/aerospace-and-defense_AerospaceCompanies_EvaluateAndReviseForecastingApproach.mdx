# Evaluate and revise forecasting approach

## Overview

Recognizing potential problems in the current forecast and making the necessary changes to align the forecast meet demand. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
