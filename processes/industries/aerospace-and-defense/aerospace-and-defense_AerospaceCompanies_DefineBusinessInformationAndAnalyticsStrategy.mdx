# Define business information and analytics strategy

## Overview

Create an organization-wide strategy for the IT function by combining skills, technologies, applications, and processes in order to attain organizations objectives. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
