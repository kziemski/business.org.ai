# Identify implications for key operating model business elements that require change

## Overview

Determine impacts of elements such as staffing, skills, training, new markets, technology, or policies within operating model which needs change. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
