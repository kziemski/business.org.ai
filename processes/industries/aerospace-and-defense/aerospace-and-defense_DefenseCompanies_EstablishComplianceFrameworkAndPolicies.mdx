# Establish compliance framework and policies

## Overview

Developing a set of procedures detailing an organization's progress in complying with established guidelines, provisions, and legislation. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
