# Assess and analyze impact of each option

## Overview

Scoping and probing to study the impact of strategic options for fulfilling the organization's objectives. Estimate a measure of the impact effectuated by each set of strategic decisions, which comprise Define strategic options [10047]. Closely examine the consequences of each option. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management.
