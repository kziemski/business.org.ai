# Establish project scope

## Overview

Establishing the horizons of business projects. Identify the objectives of the program, along with the resource requirements. Assess the readiness for the project management approach. Identify methodologies for project management. Obtain funding. Develop performance measures and indicators. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
