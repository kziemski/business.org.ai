# Identify design/development performance indicators

## Overview

Identifying performance parameters. Determine the parameters to measure performance of the design and development of the product/service concepts into prototypes. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
