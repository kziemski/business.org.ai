# Evaluate process performance

## Overview

Assessing process data, measures, and trends in an effort to evaluate process performance and identify possible improvements. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.

- **Quality Escape Rate**: Number of defects discovered after process completion per total units
