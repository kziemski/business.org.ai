# Understand IT customer strategy

## Overview

Understanding the strategy for staff dependent on information technology. Create a plan to create services and solutions, conduct daily operations, and train new employees. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
