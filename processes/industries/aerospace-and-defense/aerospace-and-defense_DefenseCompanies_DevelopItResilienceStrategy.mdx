# Develop IT resilience strategy

## Overview

Developing resilience strategies of IT across the organization so that prospective risks can be avoided. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
