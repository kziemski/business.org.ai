# Develop partner/alliance strategy

## Overview

Defining direction and plan objectives for partnering with other companies to deliver product/services. Focus on creating a vision and strategic objectives and culminate in creating measures for the strategic alliance or partnership. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
