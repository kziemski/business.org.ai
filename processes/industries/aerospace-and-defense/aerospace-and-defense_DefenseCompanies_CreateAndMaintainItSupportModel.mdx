# Create and maintain IT support model

## Overview

Design and maintaining an IT support model that defines the processes and procedures needed to support users of IT services and solutions. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
