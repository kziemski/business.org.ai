# Develop Lean/continuous improvement strategy

## Overview

Developing strategies for the optimization of processes and the improvement of functional areas in order to improve the bottom line. Create a road map of decision choices that would allow the organization to continuously enhance process efficiencies and advance performance standards. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
