# Identify core competency for each business unit

## Overview

Determining the resources and skills of each business unit based on knowledge and technical capacity. Enable business units to meet customer needs and grow in a competitive marketplace. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
