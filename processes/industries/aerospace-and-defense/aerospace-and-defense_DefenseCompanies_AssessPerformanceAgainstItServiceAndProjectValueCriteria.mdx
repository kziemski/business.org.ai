# Assess performance against IT service and project value criteria

## Overview

Process of evaluating performance to collect and analyze IT services and projects. Ensure expected IT service and project value based on established criteria. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
