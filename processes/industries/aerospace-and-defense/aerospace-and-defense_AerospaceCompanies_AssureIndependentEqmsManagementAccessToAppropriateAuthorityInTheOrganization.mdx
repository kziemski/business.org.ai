# Assure independent EQMS management access to appropriate authority in the organization

## Overview

Ensuring EQMS access to the person in charge of the quality management process. Establish who has the authority to manage the EQMS. Ensure access of EQMS to only the person(s) in authority. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
