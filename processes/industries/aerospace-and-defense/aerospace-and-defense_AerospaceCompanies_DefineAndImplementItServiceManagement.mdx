# Define and implement IT service management

## Overview

Defining and implementing activities involved in designing, creating, delivering, supporting, and managing the lifecycle of information technology services within an organization. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
