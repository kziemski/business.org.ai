# Develop shared services strategy

## Overview

Charting a plan to leverage internal services and support functions throughout the organization. Delineate a framework of parameters and criteria to selectively filter service areas for inclusion among the organizations common resources. Arrange the organizations functional areas to create efficiencies of scale in the delivery of internal services. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
