# Triage IT issues/requests

## Overview

Evaluate and assign IT issues/requests accordingly to allow for the correct routing of IT issues to the relevant support teams. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
