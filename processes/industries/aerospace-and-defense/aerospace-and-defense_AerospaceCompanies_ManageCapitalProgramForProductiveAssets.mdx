# Manage capital program for productive assets

## Overview

Producing and maintaining a planning schedule and a financial plan to purchase or manufacture productive assets. Determine the investment plan, monitor capital, and secure the necessary financing in order to realize completion of the program. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
