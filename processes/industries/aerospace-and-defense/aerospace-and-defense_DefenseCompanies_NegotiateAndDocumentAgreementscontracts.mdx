# Negotiate and document agreements/contracts

## Overview

Negotiating terms to reach a final draft of a contract that is acceptable to all parties. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
