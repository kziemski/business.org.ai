# Determine disposition of result(s)

## Overview

Deciding whether to take any additional actions based on the results of quality tests. Initiate a quarantine disposition and relocated inventory, scrap workflow item and scrap transaction, rework operation, net-able and nonnet-able items, and the activity list. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
