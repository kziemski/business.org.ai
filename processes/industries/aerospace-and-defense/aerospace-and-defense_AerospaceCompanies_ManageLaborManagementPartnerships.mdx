# Manage labor management partnerships

## Overview

Handling partnerships between labor and management. Develop a lasting two-way relationship that is beneficial for the labor, management, and the organization. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
