# Monitor maintenance performance against targets/contracts

## Overview

Following set performance targets, monitor and gage the success of the organization in meeting those targets. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
