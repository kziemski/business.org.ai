# Assess connection to other initiatives

## Overview

Correlating the change initiative with the other initiatives. Create an alignment between the goals and objectives of the change process and that of the other initiatives. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
