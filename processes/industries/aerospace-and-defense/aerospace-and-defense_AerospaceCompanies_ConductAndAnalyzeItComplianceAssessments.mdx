# Conduct and analyze IT compliance assessments

## Overview

Evaluate and analyze the IT environment for the compliance of industry regulations and government legislation. Ensure that IT capability and resources meet the set standards. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
