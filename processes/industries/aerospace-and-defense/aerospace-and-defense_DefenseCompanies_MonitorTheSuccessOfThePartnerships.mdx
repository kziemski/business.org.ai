# Monitor the success of the partnerships

## Overview

Analyzing current relationships with trade and industry groups. Ensure that the partnership in successful and make modifications where needed. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
