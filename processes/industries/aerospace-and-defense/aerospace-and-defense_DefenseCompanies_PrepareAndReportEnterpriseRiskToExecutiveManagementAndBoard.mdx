# Prepare and report enterprise risk to executive management and board

## Overview

Preparing and presenting reports about enterprise risk to the management of the organization. Create reports for management on hazard risks (e.g., property damage and liability torts), financial risks (e.g., currency and liquidity risks), and operational risks (e.g., product failure, customer satisfaction, social trends, and competition). Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
