# Relocate material and tools

## Overview

Relocating the tools and raw materials. Shift raw or finished material and machines of company from one place to another place according to changes in business requirements. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
