# Record calibration data and measurement device

## Overview

Tracking tool calibration.  Record and track when the tool was last calibrated and if it is still safe to use and still within the defined tolerances.  Apply for the production certification, which when granted, means that the Regulator has looked at production procedures and are satisfied. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
