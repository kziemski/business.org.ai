# Develop customer service segmentation/prioritization (e.g., tiers)

## Overview

Identifying and categorizing customer needs, and creating priority lists around them. Order and prioritize existing customers into segments. Adjust the customer service strategy accordingly. Analyze customer feedback. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
