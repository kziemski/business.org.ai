# Develop global support strategy

## Overview

Developing a plan to deploy support services and support functions throughout the organization globally. Arrange the organizations functional support areas to create efficiencies of scale in the delivery of support services, globally. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
