# Define and evaluate strategic options to achieve the objectives

## Overview

Assessing sets of strategic decisions designed to drive the organization's long-term objectives. Identify various strategies concerning core functional areas. Appraise strategic options in light of auxiliary decision frameworks that ensure smooth functioning, the advancement of functional efficiencies, and vitality. Involve senior management executives, especially strategy and/or business unit personnel, with need-based consultative assistance from professional services providers. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
