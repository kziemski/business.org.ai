# Evaluate divesture options

## Overview

Evaluating departments and/or subsidiaries within the organization to assess the appropriateness of a divestment, taking account of all market externalities. Examine any internal entities that have been identified to be suitable for dismemberment from the organization. Ensure the pertinence and soundness of such a move. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
