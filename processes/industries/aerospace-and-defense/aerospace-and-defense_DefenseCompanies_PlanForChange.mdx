# Plan for change

## Overview

Crafting a plan for implementing change to the organization's multiple frameworks, systems, and functional areas. Chart a schematic plan that gives the organization a foundation to shape its change efforts. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
