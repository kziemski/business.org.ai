# Monitor and report IT performance

## Overview

Supervising, analyzing, and reporting performance of information technology to ensure they are on-course and on-schedule in meeting the organizational objectives and performance targets. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
