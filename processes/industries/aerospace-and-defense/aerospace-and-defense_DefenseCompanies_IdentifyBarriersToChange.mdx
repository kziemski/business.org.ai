# Identify barriers to change

## Overview

Recognizing the circumstances or obstacles that keep the organization from progressing. Identify who and what are the resources resisting change. Identify integration failures, threats by competitive forces, and complexity failures. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
