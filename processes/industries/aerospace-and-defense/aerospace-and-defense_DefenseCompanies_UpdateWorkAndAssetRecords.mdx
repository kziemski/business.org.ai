# Update work and asset records

## Overview

Modifying existing maintenance records to include all new work that has been performed, what assets were serviced, and any issues that might have arisen. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
