# Create training and communication plans

## Overview

Designing a plan for equipping the project team with the necessary skills and abilities to fulfill their roles and responsibilities in the project effectively and efficiently. Offer formal training, mentoring, or coaching. Initiate informal conversations. Communicate messages during the project. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management.
