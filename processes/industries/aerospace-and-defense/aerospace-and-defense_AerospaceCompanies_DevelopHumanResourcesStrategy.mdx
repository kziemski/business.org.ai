# Develop human resources strategy

## Overview

Creating a long-term plan to associate human resource requirements with the strategic goals of the company to ensure that there is enough qualified staffing to achieve those goals, to maintain competitive advantage and to reduce employee turnover. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
