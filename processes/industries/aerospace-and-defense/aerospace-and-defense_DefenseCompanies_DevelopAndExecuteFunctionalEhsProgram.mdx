# Develop and execute functional EHS program

## Overview

Identify the requirements for regulation and shareholders. Assess future risks and opportunities. Develop a policy for the EHS program. Record and manage EHS program events. Within the Aerospace and Defense industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on precision manufacturing, safety-critical systems, and defense program management. Organizations must ensure alignment with FAA, ITAR, DFARS, AS9100 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
