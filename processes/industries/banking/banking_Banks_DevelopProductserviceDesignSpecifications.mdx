# Develop product/service design specifications

## Overview

This process enables Banks to develop product/service design specifications within the Banking industry. Given the industry's focus on risk management, compliance, and digital customer experience, this process must be executed with attention to FDIC, OCC, Basel III, Dodd-Frank, PCI-DSS compliance requirements. Effective implementation ensures operational excellence and supports the organization's strategic objectives in a competitive marketplace.
