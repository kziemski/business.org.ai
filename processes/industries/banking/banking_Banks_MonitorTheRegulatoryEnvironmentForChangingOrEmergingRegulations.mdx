# Monitor the regulatory environment for changing or emerging regulations

## Overview

This process enables Banks to monitor the regulatory environment for changing or emerging regulations within the Banking industry. Given the industry's focus on risk management, compliance, and digital customer experience, this process must be executed with attention to FDIC, OCC, Basel III, Dodd-Frank, PCI-DSS compliance requirements. Effective implementation ensures operational excellence and supports the organization's strategic objectives in a competitive marketplace.
