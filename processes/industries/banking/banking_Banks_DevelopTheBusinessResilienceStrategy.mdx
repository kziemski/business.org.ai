# Develop the business resilience strategy

## Overview

This process enables Banks to develop the business resilience strategy within the Banking industry. Given the industry's focus on risk management, compliance, and digital customer experience, this process must be executed with attention to FDIC, OCC, Basel III, Dodd-Frank, PCI-DSS compliance requirements. Effective implementation ensures operational excellence and supports the organization's strategic objectives in a competitive marketplace.
