# Develop salary/compensation structure and plan

> Creating the framework for the provision of salary/compensation to employees.

## Overview

Activity 7.5.1.1 is an activity within the Develop and Manage Human Capital framework. 

Creating the framework for the provision of salary/compensation to employees. Break down the salary structure into different components such as fixed pay, variable pay, bonus, and allowances such medical allowance, and rent allowance, etc. Develop, adjust, and maintain a pay structure.

## Industry Variations

This process applies universally across all industries, with the following common best practices:

### Universal Applicability

Compensation structure development is fundamental to attracting, retaining, and motivating talent across all sectors. Every organization must balance internal equity, external competitiveness, and budget constraints.

### Cross-Industry Best Practices

| Practice | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Market Benchmarking | Regularly compare pay levels to industry and geographic peers |
| Job Architecture | Establish clear job levels and pay grades for internal equity |
| Total Rewards View | Consider base pay, variable pay, benefits, and non-monetary rewards |
| Pay Transparency | Develop clear policies on pay communication and equity |
| Legal Compliance | Ensure structures meet wage, hour, and pay equity regulations |

### Common Metrics

- Compa-ratio (actual pay vs. market midpoint)
- Pay equity ratios across demographics
- Compensation cost as percentage of revenue
- Offer acceptance rate
- Turnover attributed to compensation
