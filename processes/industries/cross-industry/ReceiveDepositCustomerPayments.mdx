## Industry Variations

This process applies universally across all industries, with the following common best practices:

### Universal Applicability

Customer payment processing is fundamental to cash flow management in every organization. Efficient payment collection and deposit processes ensure liquidity and accurate financial reporting.

### Cross-Industry Best Practices

| Practice | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Multiple Payment Channels | Accept payments via ACH, credit card, check, and digital wallets |
| Same-Day Deposit | Expedite deposits to optimize cash availability |
| Automated Matching | Use technology to match payments to invoices automatically |
| Exception Handling | Establish clear processes for payment discrepancies |
| Security Controls | Implement fraud prevention for all payment channels |

### Common Metrics

- Days sales outstanding (DSO)
- Payment processing accuracy
- Time from receipt to deposit
- Exception rate requiring manual intervention
- Payment channel adoption rates
