Strategic planning in petroleum downstream must address the energy transition, evolving environmental regulations, and changing consumer preferences. Capital allocation decisions consider refinery configuration flexibility and terminal network optimization. Strategic partnerships may include biofuel producers, electric vehicle charging networks, and renewable energy developers.
