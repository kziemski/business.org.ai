## Industry Variations

Procurement in petroleum downstream manages crude oil acquisition, catalyst and chemical supplies, and maintenance materials for refinery and terminal operations. Crude procurement involves complex trading relationships, quality banking arrangements, and logistics coordination. Spare parts inventory must balance carrying costs with equipment criticality and lead times. Vendor management includes qualification requirements for safety-critical equipment and services. Strategic sourcing addresses long-term catalyst supply agreements and major equipment purchases for capital projects.
