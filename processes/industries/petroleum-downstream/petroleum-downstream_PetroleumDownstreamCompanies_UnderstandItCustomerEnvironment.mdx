HSE management in petroleum downstream is governed by extensive regulatory frameworks including OSHA Process Safety Management (PSM), EPA Risk Management Program (RMP), and state environmental regulations. API standards (API 750, 752, 753) guide facility siting, building occupancy, and portable building placement. Environmental compliance includes air emissions permits, wastewater treatment, and hazardous waste management under RCRA.
