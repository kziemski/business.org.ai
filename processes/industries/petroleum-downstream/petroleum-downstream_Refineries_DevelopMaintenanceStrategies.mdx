## Industry Variations

Maintenance in petroleum downstream follows reliability-centered maintenance (RCM) principles with emphasis on mechanical integrity for pressure vessels, piping, and rotating equipment. Predictive maintenance programs use vibration analysis, thermography, and corrosion monitoring to optimize equipment availability. Turnaround planning integrates inspection, maintenance, and capital projects during scheduled outages. API inspection codes (API 510, 570, 653) govern pressure vessel, piping, and tank inspection intervals. Maintenance execution follows work permit systems and lockout/tagout procedures for safe equipment isolation.
