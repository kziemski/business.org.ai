Refinery operations in petroleum downstream involve complex hydrocarbon processing including crude distillation, catalytic cracking, hydrotreating, and blending operations. Turnaround planning requires coordination of inspection, maintenance, and capital projects during scheduled shutdowns. Real-time optimization systems and advanced process control (APC) maximize throughput while maintaining safety margins and environmental compliance.
