# Develop succession plan

## Overview

Creating and implementing the plan for continuation of key positions within the organization. Identify internal people with the potential to fill key business leadership positions. Provide critical development experiences to employees who can move into important roles. Engage leaders to support the development of high-potential leaders.

## Industry Variations

In petroleum downstream operations, HR processes must address unique workforce challenges including 24/7 shift operations at refineries and terminals, specialized technical training requirements, and stringent safety certifications (OSHA, HAZWOPER). Organizations must maintain operator qualification programs for pipeline and terminal personnel, manage union relationships common in refinery settings, and ensure adequate staffing for turnaround and emergency response teams. Workforce planning accounts for the cyclical nature of maintenance turnarounds and seasonal fuel demand variations.
