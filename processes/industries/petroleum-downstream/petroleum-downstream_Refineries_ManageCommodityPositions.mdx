# Manage commodity positions

## Overview

Aggregating trading positions to determine market position and overall risk exposures. Monitor supply/purchase plans, trade carbon emission certificates, and manage market positions. Risk management activities are addressed in Monitor and execute risk and hedging transactions [11208].   Risk position and risk management is always separate from the group executing the trades to comply with separation of duties rules.  Compliance measures are set and executed to maintain overall positions within the risk limits determined in the trading strategy and is addressed Manage regulatory compliance [16463].
