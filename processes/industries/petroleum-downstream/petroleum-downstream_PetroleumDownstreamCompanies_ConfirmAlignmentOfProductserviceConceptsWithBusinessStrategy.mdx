# Confirm alignment of product/service concepts with business strategy

## Overview

Checking the alignment of product/service portfolio, and its individual offerings, with the organization's overall strategy and planning for the development of new or revised solutions. Conduct reality checks on new product/service concepts prior to committing significant investment to ensure that the revamped solutions' portfolio is in line with the overall business strategy, aligns organizational processes for their development, and creates a plan for assimilating these changes in the organization's offerings. Enlist senior management executives.

## Industry Variations

Refinery operations in petroleum downstream involve complex hydrocarbon processing including crude distillation, catalytic cracking, hydrotreating, and blending operations. Process control systems must maintain product quality specifications while optimizing yields and energy efficiency. Operations follow API and ASME standards for equipment design and maintenance. Turnaround planning requires coordination of inspection, maintenance, and capital projects during scheduled shutdowns. Real-time optimization systems and advanced process control (APC) maximize throughput while maintaining safety margins and environmental compliance.
