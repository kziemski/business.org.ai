# Evaluate performance to requirements

## Overview

Analyzing if the performance of the quality plan has achieved the estimated and desired requirements. Conduct tests against the quality plan. Assess the results of these tests. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
