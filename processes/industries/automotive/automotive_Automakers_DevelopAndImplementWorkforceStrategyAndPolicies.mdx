# Develop and implement workforce strategy and policies

## Overview

Creating and executing strategies and policies for smooth administration of work force. Determine and gather skill requirements. Plan the requirements for employee resourcing per unit. Create compensation, succession, HR program, and employee diversity plans. Develop and administer policies for HR. Develop benefits for employees. Create models for work force strategies. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
