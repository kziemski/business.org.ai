# Understand business requirements for IT capabilities

## Overview

Understanding business requirements for the existing IT environment as well as future IT needs. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
