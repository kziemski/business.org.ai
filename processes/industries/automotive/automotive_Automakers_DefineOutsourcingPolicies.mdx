# Define outsourcing policies

## Overview

Creating rules and regulations regarding contracting out of a business process to another party in order to reduce costs. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
