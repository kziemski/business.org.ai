## Overview

Managing all operational activities of the facility. Manage how each function/business unit works. Support the manufacturing facility to attain organizational goals. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
