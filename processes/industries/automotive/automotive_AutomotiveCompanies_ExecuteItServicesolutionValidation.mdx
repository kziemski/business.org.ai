# Execute IT service/solution validation

## Overview

Validating that the proposed IT service/solution is feasible and provides the needed services for the customer. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
