# Develop enterprise compliance policies and procedures

## Overview

Creating a standardized approach to ethics and compliance. Have a programmatic approach for compliance that focuses on the definite risks the organization faces. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
