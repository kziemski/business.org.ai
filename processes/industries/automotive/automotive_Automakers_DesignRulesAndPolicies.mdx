# Design rules and policies

## Overview

This process enables Automakers to design rules and policies within the Automotive industry. Given the industry's focus on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management, this process must be executed with attention to NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 compliance requirements. Effective implementation ensures operational excellence and supports the organization's strategic objectives in a competitive marketplace.
