# Determine and document warranty policies

## Overview

Establishing warranty policies to assure customers that the company will guarantee its warranties that it issues. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
