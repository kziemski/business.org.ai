# Provide value-add services

## Overview

For this process, value-added services can include such perks as in-house call centers for new and existing customer inquiries, on tap vehicle service and maintenance history, test drives, and hassle-free financing. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
