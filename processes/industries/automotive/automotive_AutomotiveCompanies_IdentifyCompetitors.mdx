# Identify competitors

## Overview

Identifying your competitors, their service and/or product. Evaluating competitors strategies to determine their strengths and weaknesses relative to those of your own product or service. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
