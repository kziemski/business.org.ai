# Assure independent EQMS management access to appropriate authority in the organization

## Overview

Ensuring EQMS access to the person in charge of the quality management process. Establish who has the authority to manage the EQMS. Ensure access of EQMS to only the person(s) in authority. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
