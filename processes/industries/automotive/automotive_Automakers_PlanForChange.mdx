# Plan for change

## Overview

Crafting a plan for implementing change to the organization's multiple frameworks, systems, and functional areas. Chart a schematic plan that gives the organization a foundation to shape its change efforts. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
