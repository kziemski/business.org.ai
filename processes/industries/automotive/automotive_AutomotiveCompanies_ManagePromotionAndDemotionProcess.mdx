# Manage promotion and demotion process

## Overview

Administering the process of promoting and demoting employees. Design a system for advancing or demoting an employee's rank or position. Leverage techniques such as horizontal promotion, vertical promotion, dry promotion, and involuntary/voluntary demotion. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
