# Establish  IT service management strategy and goals

## Overview

Implementing strategy for designing, delivering, managing, and improving the way information technology be used in the organization. The goal of IT Service Management is to ensure that the right processes, people, and technology are in place to meet business goals. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
