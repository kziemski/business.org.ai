# Production monitoring

## Overview

The final supply chain process group, “production monitoring”, assesses the Master Production Schedule (MPS) and Manufacturing Resource Planning (MRP) performance. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
