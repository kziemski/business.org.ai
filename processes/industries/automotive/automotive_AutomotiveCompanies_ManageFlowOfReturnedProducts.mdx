# Manage flow of returned products

## Overview

Tracking and taking care of the products that have been internally returned either because of their deficiency or in cases of incorrect delivery. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
