# Monitor and analyze IT value and benefits

## Overview

Examining and analyzing the value and benefits of IT service management to ensure benefits outweigh incurred costs. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
