# Identify and evaluate IT risk

## Overview

Developing a timely and continuous process to identify and evaluate activities that might hinder IT operations or an IT project's goals. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
