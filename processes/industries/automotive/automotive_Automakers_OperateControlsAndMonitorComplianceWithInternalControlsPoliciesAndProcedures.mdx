# Operate controls and monitor compliance with internal controls policies and procedures

## Overview

Incorporating planning, management, operations, and monitoring of internal control mechanism policies and procedures in order to manage internal controls. Design and implement control activities. Monitor control effectiveness. Remediate control deficiencies. Create compliance functions. Operate compliance functions. Implement and maintain technologies and tools to enable the internal controls-related activities. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
