# Train and manage customer service work force

## Overview

This process group involves developing training and certification standards for OEM wholesale service support staff and dealer service advisors and technicians. It also includes evaluating the quality of customer interaction with customer service representatives. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
