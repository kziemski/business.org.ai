# Develop construction strategy

## Overview

Developing a strategy to perform asset construction. Assure that timelines, regulations, and resources are on task and on budget. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
