# Assess the likelihood and consequences of occurrence of any hazards

## Overview

Performing risk analysis. Identify all dangers, evaluate how probable they are, and what kinds of negative results or or adverse side effect they carry. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
