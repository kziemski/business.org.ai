# Maintain relationships with regulators as appropriate

## Overview

Developing and preserving relationships with the regulators, without compromising the legal basis of the relationship. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
