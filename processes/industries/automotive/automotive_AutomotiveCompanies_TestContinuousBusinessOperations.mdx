# Test continuous business operations

## Overview

Assessing ongoing activities within the organization that are not intended to stop except for in an emergency. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
