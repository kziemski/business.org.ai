# Select partnerships and relationships to support the extended enterprise

## Overview

Supporting the design, manufacture and distribution of product and services through the extended enterprise model.  This is concerned with the strategic decisions on make vs buy, in house or out sourced.  Senior Executives map out how they want to run their business.  Make strategic choices as to whether to buy in components / sub-assemblies, run their own distribution fleet or contract out, own their dealerships or franchise out, etc.  Strategize with partnerships. Collaborate design consideration at strategy level for automotive and procurement act within the Target Operating Model set at strategy level. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
