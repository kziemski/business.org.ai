# Assess SLA compliance

## Overview

Gather data from each service target defined in an SLA for a time segment or review period to evaluate an overall performance percentage. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
