# Identify root cause(s)

## Overview

Recognizing the reasons that have triggered the nonconformance events or activities. Perform a root-cause analysis through documentary evidence and interviews. Involve the appropriate individuals. Leverage cause analysis and risk assessment. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
