# Share knowledge of specific risks across other parts of the organization

## Overview

Sharing information about risks and resilience strategies of business operations across the organization so that prospective risks can be avoided. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
