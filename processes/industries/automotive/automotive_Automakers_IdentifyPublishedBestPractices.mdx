# Identify published best practices

## Overview

Realizing those practices and procedures that are the most effective to the success of the business and making that information available. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
