# Manage suppliers

## Overview

Collecting and analyzing new information in order to track and rate suppliers through a supplier information management system. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
