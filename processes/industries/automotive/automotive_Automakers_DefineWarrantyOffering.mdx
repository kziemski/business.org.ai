# Define warranty offering

## Overview

Determining the exact terms and conditions under which specific warranties apply to certain goods or services. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
