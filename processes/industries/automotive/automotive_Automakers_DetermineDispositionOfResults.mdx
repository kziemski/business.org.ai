# Determine disposition of result(s)

## Overview

Deciding whether to take any additional actions based on the results of quality tests. Initiate a quarantine disposition and relocated inventory, scrap workflow item and scrap transaction, rework operation, net-able and nonnet-able items, and the activity list. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
