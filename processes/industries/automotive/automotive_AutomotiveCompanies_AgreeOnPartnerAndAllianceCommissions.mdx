# Agree on partner and alliance commissions

## Overview

Agreeing to a compensation structure for awarding commissions to the organization's partners/alliances. Select a methodology for awarding commissions, such as offering commissions for units sold per quarter or as a percentage of the margin over the cost of production. Set equitable figures for the direct compensation. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
