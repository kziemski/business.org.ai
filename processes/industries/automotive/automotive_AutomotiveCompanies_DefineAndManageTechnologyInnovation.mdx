# Define and manage technology innovation

## Overview

Outline and manage the innovation of technology within the organization. Research and understand emerging future technological concepts and capabilities. Plan for IT innovation investments. Plan and execute viable innovation projects. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
