# Design assets

## Overview

Designing assets to meet organizational needs as well as ensuring that the asset adheres to all national, regional, and local construction codes. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
