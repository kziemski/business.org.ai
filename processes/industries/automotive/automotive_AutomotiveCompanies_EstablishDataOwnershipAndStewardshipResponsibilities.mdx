# Establish  data ownership and stewardship responsibilities

## Overview

Establishing entities responsible for data accuracy, integrity, and timeliness that can authorize or deny access to certain data. Develop data utilizing governance processes to ensure fitness of data elements. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
