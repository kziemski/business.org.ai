# Define strategic options

## Overview

Defining the various options available to achieve the goals highlighted in the mission statement. Delineate (in accordance with a predefined criteria) the various permutations of strategic decisions that would help the organization achieve the objectives outlined in Develop overall mission statement [10037]. Involve senior management and key strategy personnel, with timely help from professional services providers. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
