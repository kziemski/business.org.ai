# Measure and report EHS performance

## Overview

Using performance techniques and indicators. Utilize number of audits or inspections performed, safety committee meetings, the number and types of findings and observations, timely preventive maintenance tasks performed, etc. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
