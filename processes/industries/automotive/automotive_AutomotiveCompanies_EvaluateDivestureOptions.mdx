# Evaluate divesture options

## Overview

Evaluating departments and/or subsidiaries within the organization to assess the appropriateness of a divestment, taking account of all market externalities. Examine any internal entities that have been identified to be suitable for dismemberment from the organization. Ensure the pertinence and soundness of such a move. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
