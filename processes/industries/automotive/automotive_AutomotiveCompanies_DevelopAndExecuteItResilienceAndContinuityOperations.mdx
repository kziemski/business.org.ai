# Develop and execute IT resilience and continuity operations

## Overview

Create and execute a process to rapidly adapt and respond to any internal or external opportunity, demand, disruption, or threat in IT. Maintain continuous IT operations to protect employees, assets, and overall brand equity. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
