# Develop IT service/solution "sunset" plans

## Overview

Developing plans to retire IT service/solution resources when the service/solution is no longer feasible. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
