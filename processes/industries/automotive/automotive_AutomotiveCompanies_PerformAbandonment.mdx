# Perform abandonment

## Overview

Abandoning assets. Manage the act of deliberately and permanently giving up, granting, leaving, or surrendering property or buildings and allowing others to use the intellectual property without protest. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management.
