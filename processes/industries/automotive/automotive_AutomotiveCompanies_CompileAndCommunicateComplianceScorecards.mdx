# Compile and communicate compliance scorecard(s)

## Overview

Creating a graphical representation of metrics in order to communicate the general health of the organization in relation to risk and compliancy. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
