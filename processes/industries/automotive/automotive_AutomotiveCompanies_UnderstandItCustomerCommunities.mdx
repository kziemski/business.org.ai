# Understand IT customer communities

## Overview

Interacting with IT customers to understand the IT needs through a collaborative community through involvement, connection, and informed communication. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
