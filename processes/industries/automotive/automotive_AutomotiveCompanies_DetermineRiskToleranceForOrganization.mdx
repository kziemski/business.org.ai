# Determine risk tolerance for organization

## Overview

Recognizing the organization's tolerance for risk, given risk-return trade-offs for one or more anticipated and predictable consequences. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
