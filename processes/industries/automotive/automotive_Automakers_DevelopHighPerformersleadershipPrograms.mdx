# Develop high performers/leadership programs

## Overview

Creating a program that incorporates incentives and compensation put forth by the organization to recognize high performing workers and excellence in leadership. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
