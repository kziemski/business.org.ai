# Evaluate IP acquisition options

## Overview

Establishing and defining measures and methods for valuation and acquisitions of IP. Defining the value of intellectual property for buying and licensing of IP rights. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
