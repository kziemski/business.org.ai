# Develop the business resilience strategy

## Overview

Creating a strategy for rapidly adapting to disturbances. Maintain continuous business processes and protecting employees, assets, and overall brand equity. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
