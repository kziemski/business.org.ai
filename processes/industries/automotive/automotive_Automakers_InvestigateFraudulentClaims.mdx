# Investigate fraudulent claims

## Overview

Reviewing and assessing claims that contain deliberately incorrect information or that have been submitted with the goal to deceive the system. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
