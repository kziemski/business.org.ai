# Evaluate process performance

## Overview

Assessing process data, measures, and trends in an effort to evaluate process performance and identify possible improvements. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
