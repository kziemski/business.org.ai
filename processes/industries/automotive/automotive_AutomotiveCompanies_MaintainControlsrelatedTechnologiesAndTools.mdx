# Maintain controls-related technologies and tools

## Overview

Managing technologies and tools related to the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data in order to ensure the security of the organization's information. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
