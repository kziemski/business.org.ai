# Control quality of returned parts

## Overview

Implement a checks and balances system to verify that returned parts meet acceptable quality standards to determine appropriate disposition activity. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
