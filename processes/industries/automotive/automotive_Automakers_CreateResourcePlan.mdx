# Create resource plan

## Overview

Creating a plan to ensure that all resources are available to carry out services required for the customer. This can include physical resources and personnel. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
