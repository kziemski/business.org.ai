# Create organizational design

## Overview

Formulating a design for the organization's resources that allow it to meet its objectives. Develop a new framework for molding the organization's various processes into a coherent and seamless whole. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
