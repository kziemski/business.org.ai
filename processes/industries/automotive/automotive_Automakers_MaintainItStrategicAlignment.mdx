# Maintain IT strategic alignment

## Overview

Maintaining alignment of the organization's business divisions and staff members with the organization's planned objectives for IT. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
