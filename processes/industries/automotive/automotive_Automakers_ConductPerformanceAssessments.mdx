# Conduct performance assessments

## Overview

Measuring, researching, and recording the performance of people, processes, mechanisms, or other areas of the business that the organization wants to benchmark or track. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
