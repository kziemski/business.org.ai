# Monitor and report IT performance

## Overview

Supervising, analyzing, and reporting performance of information technology to ensure they are on-course and on-schedule in meeting the organizational objectives and performance targets. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
