# Improve/validate vehicle design

## Overview

The first process executed in this group is to build and evaluate the vehicle assembly in digital mock-up (DMU). The DMU is a “virtual” digital model with mathematics and evaluates the assembly of the model - i.e. does everything in the model work together as a complete mock-up of the real product? The vehicle is then evaluated/improvised in virtual space (using computer aided engineering, or CAE). Next, the designs for the assembly and shipping /transportation of the vehicle are completed, and tools are designed and built to accomplish the production activities. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
