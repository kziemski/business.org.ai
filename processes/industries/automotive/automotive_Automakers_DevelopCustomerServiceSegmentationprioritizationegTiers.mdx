# Develop customer service segmentation/prioritization (e.g., tiers)

## Overview

Identifying and categorizing customer needs, and creating priority lists around them. Order and prioritize existing customers into segments. Adjust the customer service strategy accordingly. Analyze customer feedback. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
