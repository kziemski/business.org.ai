# Create IT marketing messages

## Overview

Developing concise statements that position the value proposition around the pressing concerns of the internal IT user base, thereby showing how the IT offerings are the right fit for a segment of IT customers. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
