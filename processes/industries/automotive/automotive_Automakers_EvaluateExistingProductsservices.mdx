# Evaluate existing products/services

## Overview

Examining the brands owned and products offered in the market. Determine the relative position of the existing products/brands in the marketplace. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
