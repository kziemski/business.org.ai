# Establish and maintain risk management roles

## Overview

Determine and maintain roles that are specialized in each risk areas and coordinating all risk management activities for IT function with due escalation structure. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
