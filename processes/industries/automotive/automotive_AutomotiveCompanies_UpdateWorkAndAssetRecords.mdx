# Update work and asset records

## Overview

Modifying existing maintenance records to include all new work that has been performed, what assets were serviced, and any issues that might have arisen. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
