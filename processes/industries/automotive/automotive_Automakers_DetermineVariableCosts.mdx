# Determine variable costs

## Overview

Calculating the variable costs of production. Approximate those costs that depend on the volume of products/services produced by the organization. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
