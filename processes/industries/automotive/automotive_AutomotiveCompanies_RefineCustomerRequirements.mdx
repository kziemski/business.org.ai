# Refine customer requirements

## Overview

Clarifying the details about procurement requests, such as the scope, timeline, data sources, type and characteristics and evaluation criteria for the goods or services to be delivered, and any additional terms, conditions or special requirements. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
