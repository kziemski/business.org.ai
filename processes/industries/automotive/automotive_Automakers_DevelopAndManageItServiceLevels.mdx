# Develop and manage IT service levels

## Overview

Establishing and maintaining service levels for the provision of IT services and solutions. Design and maintain the IT services and solution catalogue, as well as service level agreements. Evaluate the performance of IT service level agreements. Communicate the results to the management. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
