# Perform competitive analysis

## Overview

Comparing the proposals submitted by different bidders in terms of cost, efficiency and value. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
