# Measure and benchmark

## Overview

Creating and administering a strategy to manage organizational performance. Benchmark the performance of the internal processes, as well as the organization as a whole. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
