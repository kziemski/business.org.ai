# Design customer experience

## Overview

Creating a design of how customers interact with the business by analyzing data captured through various customer interaction and customer involvement. These will be captured through various channels such as customer satisfaction surveys, feedback forms, product reviews, targeted studies, observational studies, or voice of customer research. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
