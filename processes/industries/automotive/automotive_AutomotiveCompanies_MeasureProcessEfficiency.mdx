# Measure process efficiency

## Overview

Evaluating the efficiency of the organization's processes. Adapt evaluation to the activities and business components being measured. (Unlike process Measure staff productivity [11078], process Measures outcomes [11076] [e.g., how much of X is produced within X time period].) Measure a manufacturing process, for example, according to number of units produced per FTE or number of "high quality" units produced. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
