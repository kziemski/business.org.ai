# Define and evaluate strategic options to achieve the objectives

## Overview

Assessing sets of strategic decisions designed to drive the organization's long-term objectives. Identify various strategies concerning core functional areas. Appraise strategic options in light of auxiliary decision frameworks that ensure smooth functioning, the advancement of functional efficiencies, and vitality. Involve senior management executives, especially strategy and/or business unit personnel, with need-based consultative assistance from professional services providers. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
