# Define IT service catalog

## Overview

Create and design an organized and curated collection of all IT-related services that can be performed by, for, or within the organization. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
