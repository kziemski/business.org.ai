# Prepare activity-based performance measures

## Overview

Evaluating performance based on different sets of activities created by management to measure performance. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
