# Maintain intellectual property rights and restrictions

## Overview

Managing the upkeep of intellectual property rights by creating and managing a framework of rules, policies, procedures, and restrictions. Outline a clear policy for any possible scenario of their use by any external agent. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
