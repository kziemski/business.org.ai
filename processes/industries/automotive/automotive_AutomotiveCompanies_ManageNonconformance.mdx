# Manage non-conformance

## Overview

Handling any nonconformance activities or events. Assess the potential impact of the nonconformity. Decide the immediate actions to take. Identify the root causes. Take corrective or preventive action. Ensure future conformance. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
