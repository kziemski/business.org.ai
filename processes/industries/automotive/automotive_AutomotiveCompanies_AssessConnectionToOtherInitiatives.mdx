# Assess connection to other initiatives

## Overview

Correlating the change initiative with the other initiatives. Create an alignment between the goals and objectives of the change process and that of the other initiatives. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
