# Assess relationships

## Overview

Ascertaining how the business entity relates to all levels of government. Identify areas that needs further growth and resources to foster those relationships. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
