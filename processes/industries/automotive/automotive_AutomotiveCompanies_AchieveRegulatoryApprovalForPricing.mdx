# Achieve regulatory approval for pricing

## Overview

Obtaining internal price approvals and governmental approvals that are required for licensed products and for products that can be reimbursed by the government. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
