# Review and monitor physical and logical IT data security measures

## Overview

Identifying, examining, and reviewing physical and logical IT data security measures such as hardware security (smart cards), cryptographic protocols, and access control. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
