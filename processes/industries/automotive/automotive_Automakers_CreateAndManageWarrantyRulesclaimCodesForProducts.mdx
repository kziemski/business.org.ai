# Create and manage warranty rules/claim codes for products

## Overview

Establishing and maintaining claims processing and routing rules. Establish and maintain claims processing and routing rules for product warranties, coverage lists, repair, fault, trouble codes, repair time setup, and warranty policy registration. This also includes rolling out the codes and rules, and the improvements/updates to these rules and codes via systematic updates. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
