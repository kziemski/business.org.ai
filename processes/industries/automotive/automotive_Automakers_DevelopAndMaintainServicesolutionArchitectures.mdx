# Develop and maintain service/solution architectures

## Overview

Creating and maintaining a services and solutions architecture over a network that can be revised as needed or even eliminated in case of inefficiencies. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
