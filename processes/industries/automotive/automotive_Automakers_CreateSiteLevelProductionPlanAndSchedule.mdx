# Create site level production plan and schedule

## Overview

Developing a plan and schedule for managing site-level production. Develop a timeline of activities to perform at the production site. Decide the timing and duration of every on-site activity. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
