# Ensure that each business unit/function follows the enterprise risk reporting process

## Overview

Checking the reporting process of each business unit's/function's options and activities to improve opportunities and lessen threats. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
