# Prioritize IT projects

## Overview

Listing the IT projects in the order of most important to the least. Determining which of many IT projects are most important or critical to business operations. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
