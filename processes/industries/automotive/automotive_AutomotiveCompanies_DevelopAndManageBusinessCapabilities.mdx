# Develop and Manage Business Capabilities

## Overview

Performing activities by an organization that are fundamental to the successful operation of the organization, even across functions in a business. Capabilities defined in the PCF include business process management; portfolio, program, and project management; quality management; change management; benchmarking; environmental health and safety management; and knowledge management. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
