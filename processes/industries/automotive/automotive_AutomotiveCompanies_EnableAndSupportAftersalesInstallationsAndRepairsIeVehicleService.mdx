# Enable and support after-sales installations and repairs i.e. vehicle service

## Overview

This process group determines dealer installed accessory and installations, develops service technology parameters and service procedures, and distributes them to dealers. Dealers are then trained and the service attributes are defined to them. Also included in this group of processes is the resolution of technical and collision issues. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
