# Develop maintenance strategies

## Overview

Creating goals and agendas to better realize the success of the maintenance policies that have been put into place. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
