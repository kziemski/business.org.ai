# Manage work-in-progress inventory

## Overview

Handling the inventory attached with the production activities in-progress. Monitor every item of inventory being produced. Supervise the use of materials/raw materials in production to promote judicious and efficient use. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
