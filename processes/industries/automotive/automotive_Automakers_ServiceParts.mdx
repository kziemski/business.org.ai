# Service parts

## Overview

The “service parts” group includes the planning and execution of service parts. This includes a full set of supply chain activities from planning parts demand, inventory, supply and distribution to processing of parts purchase orders, performing warehousing and storage, managing physical inventory, processing sales orders and outbound parts, billing, monitoring and controlling the supply chain. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
