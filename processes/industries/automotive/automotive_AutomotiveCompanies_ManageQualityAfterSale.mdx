# Manage quality after sale

## Overview

In the “manage quality process group”, quality management is identified, early warning data is analyzed, feedback is provided to the enterprise based on the analysis and regulatory compliance is managed. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
