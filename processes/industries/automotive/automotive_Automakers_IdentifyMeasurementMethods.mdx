# Identify measurement methods

## Overview

Using tools to measure quality. Use tools such as quality improvement oversight system tool, performance measurement tools, consumer information models/guides, and validation tools to effectively determine the quality levels. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
