# Manage projects

## Overview

Establishing the scope of the projects. Create plans for implementing the projects. Initiate projects. Review and report project performance to management. Close projects. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
