# Translate customer service requirements into logistics requirements

## Overview

Determining the requirements for managing the flow of things between the point of origin and the point of consumption by assessing the service requirements of the customers. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
