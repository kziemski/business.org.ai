# Create and maintain IT compliance requirements

## Overview

Develop and maintain IT compliance standards. Maintaining requirements set forth by such directives as GRCP, PMI RMP, CGRC, CGEIT, CRMA. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
