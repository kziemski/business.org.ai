# Establish and maintain process tools and templates

## Overview

Instituting, organizing, and maintaining the upkeep of the techniques used for business process management (BPM). Create and maintain templates of BPM tools that can be readily implemented, including process engine, business analytics, content management, and collaboration tools. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
