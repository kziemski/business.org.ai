# Maintain IT asset records

## Overview

Maintaining the complete list of IT items or resources available with the organization with the details on date of purchase, licenses, deployment and SLAs. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
