# Develop partner/alliance strategy

## Overview

Defining direction and plan objectives for partnering with other companies to deliver product/services. Focus on creating a vision and strategic objectives and culminate in creating measures for the strategic alliance or partnership. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
