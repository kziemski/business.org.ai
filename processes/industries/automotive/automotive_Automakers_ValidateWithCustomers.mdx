# Validate with customers

## Overview

Creating a process to validate the sales process and the assumptions that underpin the business model. Understand if the products/services have a repeatable, scalable business model around that product. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
