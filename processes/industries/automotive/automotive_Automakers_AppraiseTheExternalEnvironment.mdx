# Appraise the external environment

## Overview

Evaluating the impact of the external environment. Evaluate the circumstances, objects, events, and aspects surrounding an organization that affects its actions and selections and that recognizes its opportunities and risks. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
