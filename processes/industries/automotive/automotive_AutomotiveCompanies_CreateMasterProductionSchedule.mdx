# Create master production schedule

## Overview

Creating  the plan for internal activities such as production, inventory, and staffing. Include forecasted quantity of items to produce based on inputs from sales planning, demand planning/forecasting, and supply chain partners. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
