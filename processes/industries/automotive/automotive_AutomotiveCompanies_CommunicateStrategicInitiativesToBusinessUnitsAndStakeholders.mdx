# Communicate strategic initiatives to business units and stakeholders

## Overview

Establishing procedures for communications within the organization which creates the road map for successful understanding of strategic initiatives for both business units and stakeholders (internal and external). Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
