# Define reverse logistics strategy

## Overview

Establish a strategy that includes rules and regulations for the physical handling, information processing, and disposition of product and packaging returned by the buyer to the seller or an intermediary. Include return approval, transportation coordination, advance communication, product tracking, receipt, disposition of the return, and processing warranty claims in the strategy. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
