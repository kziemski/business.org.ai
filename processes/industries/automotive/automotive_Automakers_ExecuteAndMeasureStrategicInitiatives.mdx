# Execute and measure strategic initiatives

## Overview

Managing strategic initiatives, from development through selection, execution, and evaluation. Conduct and oversee strategic projects supporting long-term objectives. Administer programs of strategic significance by developing such initiatives, select the most appropriate projects, and formulate measures to assess their impact. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
