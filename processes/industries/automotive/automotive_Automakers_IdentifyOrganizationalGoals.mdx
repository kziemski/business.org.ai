# Identify organizational goals

## Overview

Creating and developing strategic objectives that establishes a process to outline expected outcomes and guide employees' efforts. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
