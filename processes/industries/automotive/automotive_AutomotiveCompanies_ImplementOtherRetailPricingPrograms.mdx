# Implement other retail pricing programs

## Overview

Determining the optimum consumer pricing for each product or service at the point of sale, based on production and distribution costs and estimated sales volume. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
