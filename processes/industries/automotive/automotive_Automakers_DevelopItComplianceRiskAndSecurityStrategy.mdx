# Develop IT compliance, risk, and security strategy

## Overview

Ensuring that the organization effectively manages risk. Develop rules and standards for robust IT operations, manage risk, and adopt measures to protect integrity, confidentiality, and security of IT assets. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
