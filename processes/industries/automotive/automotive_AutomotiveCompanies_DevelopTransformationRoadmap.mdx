# Develop transformation roadmap

## Overview

Creating a blueprint for execution of IT transformation from the existing state to the planned organizational structure based on the value proposition and projected business growth. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
