# Establish the enterprise risk framework and policies

## Overview

Creating an agenda for the rules and regulations of enterprise risk that deal with hazardous, financial, operational, and strategic risks. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
