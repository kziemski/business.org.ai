# Define the strategic vision

## Overview

Developing goals to define organizations vision. Define and document ideas, direction, and activities which enable the organization to reach these goals. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
