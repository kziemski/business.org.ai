# Define manufacturing goals

## Overview

Creating quantifiable strategic objectives for each manufacturing segment in conjunction with sales projections. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
