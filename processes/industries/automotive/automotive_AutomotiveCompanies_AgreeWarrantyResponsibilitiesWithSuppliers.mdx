# Agree warranty responsibilities with suppliers

## Overview

Negotiating with manufacturers and dealers the obligations of each party in upholding warranties. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
