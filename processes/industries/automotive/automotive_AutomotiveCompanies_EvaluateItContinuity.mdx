# Evaluate IT continuity

## Overview

Evaluating IT business needs and IT's ability to recover from internal or external threat exposure. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
