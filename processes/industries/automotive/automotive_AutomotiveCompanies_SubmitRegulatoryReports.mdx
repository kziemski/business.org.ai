# Submit regulatory reports

## Overview

Creating and delivering reports to regulatory agencies to provide details about handling product recalls. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
