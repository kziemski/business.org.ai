# Schedule and perform maintenance

## Overview

This process addresses the activities where capital equipment and supply chain processes are maintained. For capital equipment, this means scheduling downtime, relocating production as needed to meet production demands during downtime, and coordinating all aspects of the downtime. For supply chain processes, this includes implementation of process improvement activities deﬁned during supply chain planning activities.
