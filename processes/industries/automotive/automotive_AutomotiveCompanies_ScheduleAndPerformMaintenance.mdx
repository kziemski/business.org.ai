# Schedule and perform maintenance

## Overview

This process addresses the activities where capital equipment and supply chain processes are maintained. For capital equipment, this means scheduling downtime, relocating production as needed to meet production demands during downtime, and coordinating all aspects of the downtime. For supply chain processes, this includes implementation of process improvement activities deﬁned during supply chain planning activities. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
