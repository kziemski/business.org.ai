# Confirm alignment of property requirements with business strategy

## Overview

Creating alignment between the requirement of properties and the overall business strategy. This process requires the organization to align the requirement of properties in accordance with its business strategies of the organization. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
