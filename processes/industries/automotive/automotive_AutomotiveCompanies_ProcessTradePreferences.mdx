# Process trade preferences

## Overview

Preparing global trade under preference, which allows the organization to import/export products at a lower or nil rate of customs duty and/or levy charge. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
