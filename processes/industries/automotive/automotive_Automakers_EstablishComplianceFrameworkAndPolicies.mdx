# Establish compliance framework and policies

## Overview

Developing a set of procedures detailing an organization's progress in complying with established guidelines, provisions, and legislation. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
