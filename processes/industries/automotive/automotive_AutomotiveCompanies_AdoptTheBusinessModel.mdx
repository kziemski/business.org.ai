# Adopt the business model

## Overview

Consenting to a particular business model and formally accepting it to serve as the set of guiding principles in operating the company. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
