# Plan and budget IT license usage volumes

## Overview

Creating a plan associated with usage volumes of IT licenses. Develop a framework to govern the licensing of an IT services along with identified usage volumes. Determine the amount of investment in IT license usage volumes and how would the license volumes be financed. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
