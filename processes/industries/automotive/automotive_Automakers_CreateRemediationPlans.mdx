# Create remediation plans

## Overview

Creating plans for remediation efforts. Make a plan to address a case of environmental adulteration. Identify and treat the adulteration so that the area will become operational again. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
