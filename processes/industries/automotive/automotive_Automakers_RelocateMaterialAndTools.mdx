# Relocate material and tools

## Overview

Relocating the tools and raw materials. Shift raw or finished material and machines of company from one place to another place according to changes in business requirements. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
