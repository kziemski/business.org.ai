# Analyze business unit strategies

## Overview

Assessing the performance of a business unit against set organizational goals which are based on pre-defined metrics collected through various business unit operations. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
