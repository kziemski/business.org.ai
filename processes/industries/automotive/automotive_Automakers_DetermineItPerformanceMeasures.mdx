# Determine IT performance measures

## Overview

Determining measures for evaluating the performance of IT services in the organization. Establish key performance indicators, including the IT services performance index. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
