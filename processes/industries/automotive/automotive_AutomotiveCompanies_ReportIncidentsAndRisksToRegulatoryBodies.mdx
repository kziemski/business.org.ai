# Report incidents and risks to regulatory bodies

## Overview

Notifying all stakeholders, legal, and industry regulatory bodies of the incidents and risks related to a return or recall, if needed. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
