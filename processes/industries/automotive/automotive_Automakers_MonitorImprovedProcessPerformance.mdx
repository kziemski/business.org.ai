## Overview

Monitoring the performance of improved business processes. Track the key performance indicators of the upgraded processes in order to gauge its contribution to the desired change. Expedite through the use of business process management software. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
