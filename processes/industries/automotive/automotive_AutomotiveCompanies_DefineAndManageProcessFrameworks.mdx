# Define and manage process frameworks

## Overview

Determining and organizing the structural composition of business processes. Design, establish, and administer the framework. Identify any cross-functional processes that are mandatory for achieving business excellence. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
