# Develop change management plans

## Overview

Creating a detailed structure summary for the purposes of managing the change. Demonstrate the reasons for the change. Define the type and scope of change. Form a change management team. Create a communication plan. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
