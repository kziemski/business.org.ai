# Define and execute business information lifecycle planning and control

## Overview

Develop and implement strategies to plan and manage the flow of an information system's data from creation and initial storage to the time when it becomes obsolete and deleted. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
