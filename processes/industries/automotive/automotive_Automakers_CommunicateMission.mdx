# Communicate mission

## Overview

Developing and executing a communication strategy to convey the mission statement. Create a universal communication strategy and appropriate delivery channels, with the objective of leveraging the latter to execute the former. Convey the inherent message of the mission to all stakeholders, including employees, customers, and the public. Ensure collaboration between senior strategy personnel and the communications/marketing team. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
