# Design facility

## Overview

Preparing and analyzing different designs for a facility in order to finalize which design will be the most suitable option. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
