# Perform lease management

## Overview

A lease is an agreement between a seller and a customer, in terms of which the customer is given use of a vehicle for an agreed lease payment amount over a fixed period of time. The processes associated with the OEM performing lease management in this process group include achieving sales; supporting dealer floor plans, retail financing and vehicle remarketing; and instilling ownership loyalty. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
