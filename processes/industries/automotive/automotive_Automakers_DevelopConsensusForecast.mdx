# Develop consensus forecast

## Overview

Arriving at a consensus over the forecasted levels of demand for services by analyzing baseline forecasts and customer input. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
