# Identify budget/roles

## Overview

Creating a plan of financial outlay for the newly defined roles. Determine the amount of capital the organization is willing to invest in effectuating these new roles, how would the roles be financed, and what would comprise the ROI from these flows. Coordinate personnel responsible for change management and the finance division. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
