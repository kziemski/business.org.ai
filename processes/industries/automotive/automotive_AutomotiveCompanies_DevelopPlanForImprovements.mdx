# Develop plan for improvements

## Overview

Devising a course of action to be taken to improve under-performing channels and to promote or expand channels that surpass expectations. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
