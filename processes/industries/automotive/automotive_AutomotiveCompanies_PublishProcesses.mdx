# Publish processes

## Overview

Disclosing the information available on business processes. Ensure the availability of the information regarding the business processes to all process team members, business stakeholders, and process owners. Use BPM software, as well as business process diagrams and documents that help depict the required information. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
