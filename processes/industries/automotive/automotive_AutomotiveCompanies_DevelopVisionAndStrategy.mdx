# Develop Vision and Strategy

## Overview

Establishing a direction and vision for an organization. This involves defining the business concept and long-term vision, as well as developing the business strategy and managing strategic initiatives. Processes in this category focus on creating a vision, a mission, and strategic objectives, and culminate in creating measures to ensure that the organization is moving in the desired direction. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
