# Assess new technology innovations

## Overview

Assessing developments in technologies presently being used by the business, new technologies that have a potential for the business, and any disruptive innovations. Conduct a survey of advancement in technologies that are already deployed with inputs from the personnel closely working with them, tracking utility and feasibility for deployment. Arrange for mid- to senior-level management personnel who explore contingent uses to assess new and disruptive technologies. Follow up with desk research, involving physical scoping and viability assessment. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
