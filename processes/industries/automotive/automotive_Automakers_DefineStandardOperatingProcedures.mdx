# Define standard operating procedures

## Overview

Establishing or prescribing methods to be followed routinely for the performance of designated operations or in designated situations. This may include step-by-step instructions to help workers carry out complex routine operations. The goal is to improve efficiency, quality, and uniformity of performance, while reducing miscommunication, failure, or rework. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
