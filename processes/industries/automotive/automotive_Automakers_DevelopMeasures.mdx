# Develop measures

## Overview

Creating metrics that can be used to systematically describe KM approaches and capabilities. Choose applicable scales, benchmarks and units of measure. Determine required precision and error rates. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.

## Key Metrics

- **Parts Per Million Defects (PPM)**: Quality metric measuring defect rate per million opportunities
- **Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE)**: Composite metric of availability, performance, and quality
- **Inventory Turns**: Rate at which inventory is cycled, indicating supply chain efficiency
