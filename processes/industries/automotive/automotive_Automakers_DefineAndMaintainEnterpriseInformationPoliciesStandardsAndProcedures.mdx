# Define and maintain enterprise information policies, standards, and procedures

## Overview

Outlining and establishing policies for information and setting information standards and procedures. Establish policies to regulate the creation, use, storage, access, communication, and dissemination of information. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
