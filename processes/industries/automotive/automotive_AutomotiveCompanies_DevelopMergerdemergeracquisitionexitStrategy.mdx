# Develop merger/demerger/acquisition/exit strategy

## Overview

Defining a strategy for corporate development. Include providing a framework for evaluating merger and acquisition candidates; and planning for a value creation through merging/demerging with a company, acquiring a company, or exiting from an already merged/acquired company. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
