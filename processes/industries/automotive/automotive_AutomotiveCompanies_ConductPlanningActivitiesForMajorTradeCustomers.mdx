# Conduct planning activities for major trade customers

## Overview

Arranging meetings with trade partners to coordinate logistics, manage critical resources, resolve bottlenecks, and schedule urgent or time-sensitive matters. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
