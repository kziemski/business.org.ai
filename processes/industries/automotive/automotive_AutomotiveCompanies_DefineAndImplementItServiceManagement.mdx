# Define and implement IT service management

## Overview

Defining and implementing activities involved in designing, creating, delivering, supporting, and managing the lifecycle of information technology services within an organization. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
