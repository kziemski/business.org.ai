# Monitor/Manage supplier information

## Overview

Examining procurement and vendor performance. Report delivery timing and the quality of the materials procured through different vendors. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
