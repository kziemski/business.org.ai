# Amend or create policy

## Overview

Crafting a new framework of policies and procedures for deploying remediation efforts, or change existing policies and procedures. Adapt the policy structure to the context of the apposite national and international regulatory frameworks. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
