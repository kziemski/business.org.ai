# Create and maintain IT security policies, standards, and procedures

## Overview

Develop and maintain an architecture for securing and ensuring the privacy of data flows throughout the organization. Create, test, evaluate, and implement IT security policies to ensure the safe use of IT services and solutions. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
