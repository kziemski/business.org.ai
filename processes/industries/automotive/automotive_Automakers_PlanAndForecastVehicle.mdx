# Plan and forecast vehicle

## Overview

This process group includes the processes to plan and simulate business, plan and forecast sales, perform collaborative planning, maintain incentive plans, and conduct planning based on options as well as on fully configured orders. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
