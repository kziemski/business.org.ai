# Confirm environmental readiness

## Overview

Confirming that the organization has the recourses necessary to meet the expectations for the solution for service delivery. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
