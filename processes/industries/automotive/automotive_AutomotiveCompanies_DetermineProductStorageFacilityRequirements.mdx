# Determine product storage facility requirements

## Overview

Evaluate constraints, needs, parameters, and conditions for physical storage and retrieval of components or products for future use or shipment in a storage facility within a certain timeframe. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
