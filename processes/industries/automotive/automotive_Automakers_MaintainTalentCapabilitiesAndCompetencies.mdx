# Maintain talent capabilities and competencies

## Overview

Maintaining a common denominator for the competency level within the organization's talent circle. Conduct training sessions, skill development activities, and quality excellence activities to ensure that the resources of the organization are competent enough and have the capabilities to achieve the required level of quality. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
