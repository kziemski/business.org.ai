# Determine change enablers

## Overview

Identifying the person(s) or thing(s)responsible for making the change possible. Consider factors such as interdependence of efforts, reward to the integrators, sharing of power and responsibility, and employee understanding of why change is essential. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
