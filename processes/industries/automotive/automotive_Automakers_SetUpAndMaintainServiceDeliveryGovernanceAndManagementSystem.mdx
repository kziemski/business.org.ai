# Set up and maintain service delivery governance and management system

## Overview

Providing a system for which to manage customer needs and a structure for which to facilitate service delivery to fulfill those needs. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
