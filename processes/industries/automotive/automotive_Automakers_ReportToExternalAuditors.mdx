# Report to external auditors

## Overview

Reporting to external auditors. This process requires the organization to report to external auditors about the regulations for any critical data that the organization is holding. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
