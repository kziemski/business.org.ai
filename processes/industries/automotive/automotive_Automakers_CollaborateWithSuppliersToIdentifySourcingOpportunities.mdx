# Collaborate with suppliers to identify sourcing opportunities

## Overview

Collaborating with the suppliers of materials and services in order to determine new opportunities for sourcing. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
