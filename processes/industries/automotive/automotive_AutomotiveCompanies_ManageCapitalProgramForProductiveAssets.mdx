# Manage capital program for productive assets

## Overview

Producing and maintaining a planning schedule and a financial plan to purchase or manufacture productive assets. Determine the investment plan, monitor capital, and secure the necessary financing in order to realize completion of the program. Within the Automotive industry, this process takes on particular importance due to the sector's emphasis on production efficiency, supply chain coordination, and quality management. Organizations must ensure alignment with NHTSA, EPA, IATF 16949 standards while optimizing for efficiency and effectiveness.
