## Industry Variations

Asset relationship management in broadcasting involves complex interconnections between content elements:

- **Series-episode hierarchies**: Linking episodes to seasons, series, franchises, and umbrella brands for navigation and rights management
- **Clip derivation tracking**: Maintaining parent-child relationships between source programs and derived clips, promos, and highlights
- **Multi-version associations**: Connecting different versions of content (broadcast, streaming, international, censored, extended) to master records
- **Talent and credit linkages**: Associating content with performers, creators, and crew for rights, search, and attribution
- **Rights bundle relationships**: Linking content to underlying rights deals, music licenses, and talent agreements
- **Related content connections**: Enabling content discovery through thematic, franchise, and editorial relationships
- **Cross-platform asset mapping**: Connecting the same content across different technical formats and delivery platforms
