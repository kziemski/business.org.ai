## Industry Variations

Accounting record adjustments in broadcasting require specialized media industry knowledge:

- **Revenue recognition timing**: Advertising revenue, retransmission fees, and content licensing have complex recognition rules under ASC 606
- **Program cost amortization**: Film and episodic content costs are amortized using individual-film-forecast or program-library methods per ASC 920-350
- **Make-good liability adjustments**: Adjusting for advertising underdelivery and associated make-good obligations
- **Participation and residual accruals**: Recording talent participation, profit sharing, and residual obligations as programs generate revenue
- **Rights impairment assessment**: Evaluating content library and programming rights for impairment under changing distribution economics
- **Barter revenue adjustments**: Proper fair value measurement and recording of barter advertising transactions
- **Affiliate and syndication settlements**: Reconciling complex revenue sharing arrangements with affiliate stations and syndication partners
