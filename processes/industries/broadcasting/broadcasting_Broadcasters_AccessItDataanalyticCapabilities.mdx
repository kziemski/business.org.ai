## Industry Variations

Data and analytics access in broadcasting presents unique considerations:

- **Ratings data integration**: Nielsen, Comscore, and first-party streaming data require specialized access protocols and data handling agreements
- **Real-time analytics**: Live broadcast monitoring demands sub-second data access for audience measurement, ad trafficking, and technical quality assurance
- **Cross-platform attribution**: Tracking viewer engagement across linear TV, streaming, social media, and websites requires complex data integration
- **Content performance metrics**: Analyzing program performance, episode retention, and binge-watching patterns drives programming decisions
- **Advertising effectiveness**: Providing advertisers with campaign analytics while maintaining viewer privacy requires careful data governance
- **Competitive intelligence**: Access to industry benchmarks and competitive ratings data involves licensed third-party relationships
- **Privacy compliance**: Viewer data, especially from connected TV and streaming platforms, falls under CCPA, GDPR, and COPPA regulations
