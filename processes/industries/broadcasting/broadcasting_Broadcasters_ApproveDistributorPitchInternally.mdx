## Industry Variations

Distributor pitch approval in broadcasting involves unique industry considerations:

- **Retransmission consent negotiations**: Pitches to cable, satellite, and streaming distributors require executive approval on carriage fees and terms
- **Must-carry vs. retrans elections**: Stations must choose between guaranteed carriage (must-carry) or negotiated fees (retransmission consent)
- **Virtual MVPD strategy**: Approving pitch strategies for YouTube TV, Hulu Live, and other streaming distributors requires different approaches
- **Bundling considerations**: Channel portfolio strategies determine how networks are packaged together in distribution negotiations
- **Digital rights allocation**: Distribution deals increasingly include streaming, on-demand, and authenticated TV Everywhere rights
- **International distribution**: Pitch approval for international distributors involves territory rights, local regulations, and currency considerations
- **Regulatory compliance**: Ensuring pitched terms comply with FCC program access rules and anti-discrimination requirements
