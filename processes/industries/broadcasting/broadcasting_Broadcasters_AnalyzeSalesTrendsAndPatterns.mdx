# Analyze sales trends and patterns

## Overview

Analyzing sales order data to identify patterns in order to capitalize on emerging trends in the industry or the economy. Closely examine the directory of sales orders. Discern any patterns from this index, which is representative of the demand for the organization's offerings. Identify trends among the various segments of the organization's customer base to create forecasts. Glean patterns from this analysis, including the triangulation of segments that are showing the most growth in demand or those that represent the highest decline revenue, industry-wide trends such as decline/boost in overall demand, and any unusual trends that lie outside of the organization's expectations.

## Industry Variations

Sales trend analysis in broadcasting involves unique market dynamics:

- **Upfront/Scatter market cycles**: Analyzing the annual upfront advertising buying season versus in-quarter scatter market purchasing patterns
- **Seasonality patterns**: Political advertising cycles, major sports seasons, sweeps periods, and holiday retail spending drive distinct seasonal trends
- **Programmatic vs. direct sales**: Understanding the shift from relationship-based direct sales to programmatic and automated ad buying
- **Inventory yield management**: Analyzing sellout rates, pricing tiers, and make-good liability across dayparts and programs
- **Cross-platform sales attribution**: Tracking bundled deals across linear TV, streaming, digital, and social platforms
- **Advertiser category health**: Monitoring spending trends by category (automotive, pharma, retail, tech) as leading indicators
- **Retransmission and affiliate revenue**: Analyzing carriage fee trends, renewal cycles, and subscriber trajectory impacts on distribution revenue
