## Industry Variations

IT service performance in broadcasting has critical real-time and reliability requirements:

- **Zero-tolerance transmission**: Broadcast transmission and streaming services require near-100% uptime with immediate failover capabilities
- **Live production support**: IT services must support live broadcasts where any service failure is immediately visible to audiences
- **Content delivery performance**: Streaming CDN performance, buffering rates, and video quality metrics directly impact viewer experience
- **MAM system availability**: Media Asset Management systems are critical 24/7 operations that cannot tolerate extended downtime
- **Broadcast automation SLAs**: Master control and playout automation systems require highest-tier service level agreements
- **Remote production infrastructure**: IT services supporting remote and cloud-based production need performance guarantees for contribution feeds
- **News and sports velocity**: Breaking news and live sports require IT systems capable of rapid content ingest, editing, and distribution
