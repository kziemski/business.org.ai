## Industry Variations

In broadcasting, makeup application is a critical pre-production activity that differs significantly from other industries:

- **Live broadcast requirements**: Makeup must be applied quickly and designed for high-definition cameras, which reveal imperfections more than standard video
- **On-air talent considerations**: News anchors and hosts require consistent looks across broadcasts to maintain brand identity
- **Studio lighting adaptation**: Makeup artists must account for specific studio lighting setups that can wash out or distort natural skin tones
- **Time pressure**: Unlike film production, live broadcasts operate on strict schedules with no opportunity for touch-ups during transmission
- **Multi-platform appearance**: Talent often appears across TV, streaming, and social media, requiring makeup that works across different formats and resolutions
