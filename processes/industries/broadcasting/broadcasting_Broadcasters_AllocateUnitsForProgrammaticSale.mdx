## Industry Variations

Programmatic advertising in broadcasting represents a fundamental shift in ad sales operations:

- **Linear vs. connected TV**: Programmatic allocation spans traditional broadcast inventory (limited automation) and streaming/CTV inventory (fully programmatic capable)
- **Private marketplace strategy**: Premium broadcast inventory typically sells through curated private marketplaces rather than open exchanges
- **Yield optimization**: Balancing programmatic allocation against direct-sold inventory to maximize revenue while honoring guaranteed deals
- **Data clean room integration**: Enabling advertiser data matching for audience targeting while protecting viewer privacy
- **Frequency capping**: Managing cross-platform frequency limits when inventory is allocated across programmatic and direct channels
- **Real-time decisioning**: Connected TV and streaming require sub-second ad decisioning infrastructure integrated with broadcast automation
- **Unified auction dynamics**: Managing simultaneous demand from DSPs, direct advertisers, and network house ads within single-auction frameworks
