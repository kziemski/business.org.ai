## Industry Variations

Metadata tagging in broadcasting requires industry-specific taxonomies and standards:

- **Content identification standards**: EIDR (Entertainment Identifier Registry), ISAN, and Ad-ID provide unique identifiers for content tracking across distribution channels
- **Rights metadata**: Territorial, platform, and temporal rights information must be captured for automated rights management and playout authorization
- **Technical specifications**: Frame rates, aspect ratios, audio configurations, and encoding parameters are critical for broadcast automation systems
- **Descriptive metadata**: Genre classifications, content ratings (TV-PG, TV-MA), closed caption availability, and SAP (secondary audio program) language tracks
- **Sports-specific tagging**: Play-by-play markers, highlight timestamps, and participant identification for sports content
- **News taxonomy**: Story slugs, beat classifications, reporter assignments, and wire service attributions for news content management
- **Advertising metadata**: Commercial break timing, sponsor information, and ad trafficking codes for revenue tracking
