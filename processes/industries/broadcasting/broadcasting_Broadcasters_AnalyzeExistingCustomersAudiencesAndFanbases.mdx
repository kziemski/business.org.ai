## Industry Variations

Audience analysis in broadcasting involves specialized methodologies distinct from general customer analytics:

- **Nielsen panel data**: Traditional TV ratings rely on sample-based measurement panels that require statistical interpretation and demographic projections
- **Streaming metrics**: First-party data from authenticated viewers provides granular engagement data including watch time, completion rates, and content discovery patterns
- **Social listening**: Fan engagement on Twitter/X, Reddit, TikTok, and fan communities provides real-time sentiment about programming and talent
- **Second-screen behavior**: Understanding how audiences interact with companion apps, live tweets, and fantasy sports during broadcasts
- **Fandom economics**: Analyzing merchandise purchases, convention attendance, and fan content creation to understand passionate audience segments
- **Cross-platform journey**: Tracking how audiences discover, sample, and become loyal viewers across linear TV, streaming, and social video
- **Advertiser-valued segments**: Identifying and quantifying audience segments that command premium CPMs from specific advertising categories
