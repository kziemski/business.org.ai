# Analyze and manage channel performance

## Overview

Monitoring marketing and distribution efforts of all channels individually and as a network. Re-distribute or add resources to channels that perform better than expected. Purge or consolidate under-performing channels, and find more promising replacements.

## Industry Variations

Channel performance management in broadcasting operates across a complex multi-platform ecosystem:

- **Linear network performance**: Ratings trends, demographic composition, and daypart performance across broadcast and cable networks
- **Streaming platform metrics**: Subscriber growth, churn rates, engagement depth, and content performance on owned streaming services
- **FAST channel management**: Performance of free ad-supported streaming TV channels including viewership, ad fill rates, and content rotation
- **Digital and social channels**: YouTube, TikTok, and social media channel performance including views, engagement, and subscriber growth
- **Affiliate performance**: Local station performance by market including ratings, news viewership, and syndicated programming delivery
- **Distribution channel optimization**: Evaluating carriage on cable, satellite, virtual MVPDs, and direct-to-consumer platforms
- **Cross-platform attribution**: Understanding how performance on one channel drives or cannibalizes viewership on others
