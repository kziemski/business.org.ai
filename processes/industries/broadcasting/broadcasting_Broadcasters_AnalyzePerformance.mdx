## Industry Variations

Performance analysis in broadcasting uses industry-specific metrics and benchmarks:

- **Ratings performance**: Analyzing Nielsen ratings delivery against projections for audience guarantees and internal targets
- **Time slot competitiveness**: Comparing performance against competing networks in the same daypart and demographic targets
- **Lead-in/lead-out flow**: Measuring how effectively programs retain or build audience from adjacent programming
- **Premiere vs. repeat performance**: Tracking ratings erosion between original airings and repeat broadcasts
- **DVR and streaming lift**: Understanding delayed viewing impact on total audience delivery
- **Social engagement correlation**: Analyzing relationships between social media buzz and viewing behavior
- **Content ROI analysis**: Evaluating program performance relative to acquisition, production, or licensing costs
