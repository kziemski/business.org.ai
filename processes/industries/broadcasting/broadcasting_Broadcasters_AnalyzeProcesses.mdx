## Industry Variations

Process analysis in broadcasting must account for industry-specific workflows:

- **Live production workflows**: Real-time content creation for news, sports, and live events requires process designs with minimal latency and maximum reliability
- **Multi-platform distribution**: Content must flow simultaneously to linear broadcast, streaming, social clips, and international feeds requiring parallel process streams
- **Content lifecycle management**: Processes span from concept development through production, post-production, distribution, archive, and rights expiration
- **24/7 operations requirements**: Master control, transmission, and streaming operations run continuously requiring process designs for shift handoffs and redundancy
- **Union work rules**: IATSE, IBEW, and NABET contracts specify work processes, crew sizes, and job function boundaries that constrain process optimization
- **Regulatory compliance integration**: Processes must embed FCC compliance, accessibility requirements, and advertising standards enforcement
- **Vendor and partner integration**: Complex supply chains involving production partners, post houses, and distribution platforms require inter-organizational process coordination
