## Industry Variations

Data analysis in education involves specialized analytical approaches:

- **Achievement Data Analysis**: Examining standardized test results, grades, and learning outcomes
- **Disaggregated Analysis**: Breaking down data by student subgroups to identify equity gaps
- **Growth Modeling**: Calculating student academic growth compared to similar peers
- **Predictive Analytics**: Using early warning indicators to identify at-risk students
- **Survey Data Analysis**: Analyzing climate, engagement, and satisfaction survey responses
- **Operational Metrics**: Examining attendance, discipline, graduation, and other operational data
- **Financial Analysis**: Analyzing budget performance, spending patterns, and resource allocation
- **Visualization and Reporting**: Creating dashboards and reports for various stakeholder audiences
