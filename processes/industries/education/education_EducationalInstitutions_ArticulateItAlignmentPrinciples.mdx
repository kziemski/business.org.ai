## Industry Variations

IT alignment principles in education emphasize instructional mission support:

- **Instructional Priority**: Technology investments prioritize direct support for teaching and learning outcomes
- **Equity First**: IT principles ensure equitable access to technology across all schools and student groups
- **Privacy by Design**: Student data protection is embedded in all technology selection and implementation decisions
- **Interoperability Standards**: Systems are expected to integrate using education data standards (Ed-Fi, SIF, LTI)
- **Sustainability**: Technology planning considers total cost of ownership including training and support
- **Accessibility**: All technology must meet accessibility requirements for students and staff with disabilities
- **Security Balance**: Security measures balance protection with educational access and usability
- **Stakeholder Voice**: Teachers and principals participate in technology decision-making processes
