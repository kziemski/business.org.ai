## Industry Variations

Student achievement assessment in education uses comprehensive multi-measure approaches:

- **State Standardized Testing**: Annual state assessments measure proficiency against grade-level academic standards
- **Growth Measurement**: Student growth percentiles compare learning progress to academic peers
- **Classroom Assessment**: Ongoing teacher-created assessments measure mastery of specific learning objectives
- **Benchmark Assessments**: Periodic interim assessments predict state test performance and guide instruction
- **Portfolio Assessment**: Some programs use performance portfolios demonstrating student work quality
- **College Readiness Indicators**: High school assessments include SAT, ACT, and AP exam performance
- **Graduation Requirements**: Tracking progress toward graduation credits and competency requirements
- **Subgroup Analysis**: Achievement data is analyzed by demographic subgroups to identify equity gaps
