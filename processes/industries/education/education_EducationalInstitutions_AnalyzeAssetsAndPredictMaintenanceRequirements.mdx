## Industry Variations

Asset and maintenance analysis in educational settings involves specific considerations:

- **Aging Infrastructure**: Many school buildings are decades old, requiring comprehensive condition assessments and lifecycle planning
- **HVAC Critical Systems**: Climate control directly impacts learning environments and requires priority maintenance attention
- **Safety Systems**: Fire suppression, security systems, and emergency equipment require regular inspection and maintenance schedules
- **Technology Infrastructure**: Network equipment, interactive displays, and device refresh cycles require coordinated planning
- **Playground and Athletic Facilities**: Outdoor equipment and fields have specific safety inspection and maintenance requirements
- **Vehicle Fleet**: School buses and district vehicles require DOT-compliant inspection and maintenance tracking
- **Deferred Maintenance Backlogs**: Many districts carry significant deferred maintenance requiring long-term capital planning
- **Bond Planning**: Asset condition data supports facilities bond measure development and voter communication
