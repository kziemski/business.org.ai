## Industry Variations

Engagement and satisfaction analysis in education spans multiple stakeholder groups:

- **Multi-Stakeholder Surveys**: Schools measure satisfaction of students, parents, staff, and community members separately
- **School Climate Surveys**: Many states require annual climate surveys measuring safety, engagement, and environment perceptions
- **Employee Engagement**: Staff surveys address job satisfaction, professional development, and working conditions
- **Student Engagement Metrics**: Beyond surveys, schools track attendance, course completion, and extracurricular participation
- **Parent Engagement Tracking**: Schools measure family involvement in conferences, volunteering, and communication response rates
- **NSSE/CCSSE (Higher Ed)**: Colleges use standardized engagement surveys to benchmark against peer institutions
- **Exit Surveys**: Understanding reasons for student transfers, dropouts, and employee departures
- **Accreditation Requirements**: Satisfaction data often supports institutional effectiveness reporting for accreditors
