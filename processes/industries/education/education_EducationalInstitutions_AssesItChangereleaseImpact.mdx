## Industry Variations

Change and release impact assessment in education considers instructional continuity:

- **Instructional Disruption Risk**: Evaluating potential impact on classroom instruction and student learning activities
- **Testing System Stability**: Heightened concern for changes affecting assessment and grading systems
- **Parent Portal Impact**: Assessing how changes affect parent access to student information
- **Training Requirements**: Estimating professional development needs for staff before changes take effect
- **Student Data Migration**: Carefully assessing risks to student records during system transitions
- **Integration Dependencies**: Mapping impacts across interconnected educational systems (SIS, LMS, assessment)
- **Timing Constraints**: Evaluating feasibility within academic calendar windows for implementation
- **Communication Requirements**: Identifying stakeholders requiring advance notification of changes
