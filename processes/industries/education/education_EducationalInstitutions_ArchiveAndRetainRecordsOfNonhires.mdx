## Industry Variations

Non-hire record retention in education follows specific regulatory requirements:

- **EEOC Retention Periods**: Federal requirements mandate retention of unsuccessful applicant records for specified periods
- **State Public Records Laws**: Application materials may be subject to public records requests
- **Discrimination Defense**: Records support defense against potential employment discrimination claims
- **Interview Documentation**: Notes and scoring rubrics from interview panels are retained with application files
- **Background Check Records**: Criminal history information requires secure storage and eventual destruction
- **Applicant Pool Analysis**: Aggregate data from non-hires supports diversity hiring analysis
- **Reapplication Tracking**: Records help identify repeat applicants for consistent treatment
- **Destruction Protocols**: Secure destruction methods are required when retention periods expire
