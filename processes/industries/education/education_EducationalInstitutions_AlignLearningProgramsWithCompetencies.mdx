## Industry Variations

Aligning learning programs with competencies in education spans both student and staff development:

- **Standards-Based Alignment**: Student learning programs align with state academic standards and competency frameworks
- **Educator Competency Frameworks**: Professional development aligns with teaching standards such as InTASC, Danielson, or state-specific frameworks
- **Career Technical Education**: CTE programs align with industry competency standards and certification requirements
- **Competency-Based Education Models**: Some schools use mastery-based progression rather than traditional seat-time requirements
- **Social-Emotional Learning**: Programs increasingly address SEL competencies alongside academic skills
- **Administrator Preparation**: Leadership development aligns with principal and superintendent licensure competencies
- **Micro-Credentials**: Educators may earn competency badges in specific skill areas
- **Work-Based Learning**: Student internships and apprenticeships connect classroom learning to workplace competencies
