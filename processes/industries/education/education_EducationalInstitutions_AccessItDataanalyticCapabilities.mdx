## Industry Variations

Educational institutions approach IT data and analytics capabilities differently from other sectors:

- **Student Information Systems**: Education relies heavily on specialized SIS platforms (PowerSchool, Infinite Campus, Banner) that integrate with learning management systems
- **FERPA Compliance**: Access to student data is governed by strict federal privacy regulations requiring role-based access controls and audit trails
- **Learning Analytics**: Focus on predictive analytics for student success, early warning systems, and intervention tracking rather than customer behavior analysis
- **Assessment Data Integration**: Analytics must incorporate standardized testing data, formative assessments, and competency-based metrics
- **Disaggregated Reporting**: Data must be analyzable by demographic subgroups to meet equity reporting requirements and identify achievement gaps
- **Research Data Management**: Higher education institutions must support faculty research data needs alongside administrative analytics
