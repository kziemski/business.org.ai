## Industry Variations

Hiring in education involves specialized requirements and processes:

- **Credential Verification**: Confirming valid teaching licenses, endorsements, and degree requirements before hire
- **Background Check Requirements**: Enhanced fingerprinting and criminal history checks for positions working with minors
- **Board Approval**: Many contracts require formal board of education approval for employment
- **Salary Schedule Placement**: Determining step and lane placement based on experience and education credentials
- **Collective Bargaining Constraints**: Union contracts may govern hiring processes, seniority rights, and transfer procedures
- **Certification Assistance**: Supporting candidates in obtaining or transferring licenses to meet state requirements
- **Alternative Certification**: Working with career changers completing alternative pathways to certification
- **Hard-to-Staff Incentives**: Offering signing bonuses or loan forgiveness for high-need subject areas
