## Industry Variations

Student residence file management supports enrollment and attendance zone administration:

- **Enrollment Verification**: Residence documentation verifies student eligibility for neighborhood school attendance
- **Proof of Residency**: Maintaining required documentation (utility bills, leases, affidavits) for each student
- **Homeless Student Identification**: McKinney-Vento requires identifying and serving students in transitional housing
- **Non-Resident Transfers**: Tracking students attending outside their home attendance zone
- **Boundary Updates**: Residence data supports attendance zone planning and boundary adjustments
- **Transportation Routing**: Accurate addresses are essential for school bus routing and scheduling
- **State Reporting**: Student residence data supports state enrollment and funding reports
- **Address Verification Services**: Some districts use third-party verification to confirm residence claims
