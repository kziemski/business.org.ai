## Industry Variations

Cash remittance processing in education handles diverse payment sources:

- **Tuition and Fee Payments**: Processing student tuition, activity fees, and registration payments
- **Cafeteria Payments**: Managing prepaid meal account deposits and daily cash collections
- **Activity Fund Receipts**: Handling cash from school events, fundraisers, and extracurricular activities
- **Donation Processing**: Receipting charitable contributions with proper donor acknowledgment
- **State and Federal Payments**: Receiving and applying grant reimbursements and state aid distributions
- **Property Tax Receipts**: For districts with taxing authority, processing property tax collections
- **Online Payment Integration**: Reconciling payments from parent payment portals and mobile apps
- **Point of Sale Systems**: Managing cash handling across multiple campus locations
