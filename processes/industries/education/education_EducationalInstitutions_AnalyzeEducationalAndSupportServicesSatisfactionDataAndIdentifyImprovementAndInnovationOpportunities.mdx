## Industry Variations

Satisfaction analysis in education spans instructional and support services:

- **Instructional Quality Feedback**: Student and parent satisfaction with teaching quality and curriculum relevance
- **Support Services Assessment**: Satisfaction with counseling, tutoring, health services, and food service
- **Transportation Surveys**: Parent and student feedback on bus routes, safety, and timeliness
- **Technology Support Satisfaction**: User satisfaction with help desk, device programs, and digital tools
- **Special Education Services**: Parent satisfaction with IEP process and related services delivery
- **Facilities Feedback**: Satisfaction with building conditions, cleanliness, and learning environment
- **Communication Effectiveness**: Stakeholder satisfaction with school-to-home communication channels
- **Continuous Improvement Cycles**: Satisfaction data feeds improvement planning with documented action steps
