## Industry Variations

Parent-teacher organization collaboration supports school-community partnerships:

- **PTA/PTO Coordination**: Working with parent volunteer organizations on school improvement and events
- **Volunteer Management**: Coordinating parent volunteers for classroom support, field trips, and activities
- **Fundraising Partnerships**: Collaborating on fundraising efforts while ensuring appropriate financial controls
- **Event Planning**: Joint planning for open houses, family nights, and community celebrations
- **Communication Channels**: Sharing school information through parent organization networks
- **Advisory Input**: Incorporating parent organization perspectives in school decision-making
- **Resource Donations**: Managing donated supplies, equipment, and enrichment program funding
- **Advocacy Partnerships**: Coordinating parent advocacy on educational issues and policy matters
