## Industry Variations

Aligning development needs in education involves specialized considerations:

- **Licensure Requirements**: Professional development must address state-mandated continuing education for license renewal
- **Content Area Alignment**: Teacher development should connect individual growth to curriculum implementation and student achievement needs
- **School Improvement Focus**: Development priorities are driven by campus improvement plans and identified areas of student underperformance
- **Instructional Framework Alignment**: Training must support adopted instructional models, curricula, and assessment practices
- **New Teacher Support**: Early-career educators require intensive induction and mentoring aligned with district expectations
- **Leadership Pipeline**: Districts must develop aspiring administrators through targeted leadership development programs
- **Technology Integration**: Ongoing training supports effective use of learning management systems, assessment tools, and instructional technology
- **Evaluation System Connection**: Professional growth plans should connect to educator evaluation frameworks and performance feedback
