## Industry Variations

Assessment program evaluation in education examines a comprehensive testing system:

- **State Assessment Alignment**: Evaluating how well local assessments prepare students for state-mandated tests
- **Assessment Balance**: Reviewing the mix of formative, interim, and summative assessments for coherence
- **Testing Burden Analysis**: Assessing whether total testing time is appropriate and not excessive
- **Data Utility Review**: Evaluating whether assessment data is actionable for improving instruction
- **Accommodation Adequacy**: Reviewing accessibility of assessments for students with disabilities and English learners
- **Technology Readiness**: Assessing infrastructure capacity for computer-based testing requirements
- **Psychometric Quality**: Reviewing reliability and validity of locally-developed assessments
- **Cost-Effectiveness**: Analyzing assessment program costs relative to instructional value provided
