## Industry Variations

Change and release approval in educational IT requires academic calendar alignment:

- **Academic Calendar Timing**: Major deployments are scheduled during summer, winter break, or spring break to minimize instructional disruption
- **Testing Window Avoidance**: Changes are frozen during state testing windows to ensure assessment platform stability
- **Pilot Campus Approach**: New systems are often piloted at selected schools before district-wide deployment
- **Teacher Input**: Instructional technology changes include teacher feedback and training readiness assessment
- **Student Data Impact Assessment**: Changes affecting student information systems require additional privacy review
- **Parent Communication**: Major system changes affecting parent portals require advance notification
- **Rollback Planning**: Change approval includes contingency plans for reverting changes if issues arise
- **Support Capacity**: Deployment timing considers help desk capacity for increased support needs
