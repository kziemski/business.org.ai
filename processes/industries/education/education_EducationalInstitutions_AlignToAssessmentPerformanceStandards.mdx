## Industry Variations

Aligning to assessment performance standards in education is uniquely regulated:

- **State Standards Alignment**: Curricula and assessments must align with state-adopted academic content standards
- **Cut Score Setting**: Performance levels (basic, proficient, advanced) are established through psychometric standard-setting processes
- **College Readiness Benchmarks**: Standards increasingly incorporate readiness indicators for postsecondary success
- **Growth Measures**: Beyond proficiency, standards include student growth expectations compared to academic peers
- **Subgroup Performance**: ESSA requires meeting standards for all student subgroups, not just overall performance
- **Alternate Assessment Standards**: Students with significant cognitive disabilities have modified achievement standards
- **Career Technical Standards**: CTE programs align with industry certification and employability skill standards
- **International Benchmarks**: Some districts compare performance to international standards (PISA, TIMSS)
