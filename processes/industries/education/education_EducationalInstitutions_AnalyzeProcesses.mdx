## Industry Variations

Process analysis in education examines both administrative and instructional operations:

- **Enrollment Processes**: Analyzing student registration, transfers, and records management workflows
- **Instructional Planning**: Examining curriculum development, lesson planning, and assessment cycles
- **Student Support Referrals**: Mapping intervention and special education referral processes
- **Hiring Workflows**: Analyzing teacher recruitment, screening, and onboarding processes
- **Procurement Cycles**: Examining requisition to payment processes for efficiency and compliance
- **Budget Development**: Analyzing budget planning, approval, and monitoring processes
- **Parent Communication**: Examining communication workflows and response time metrics
- **Continuous Improvement**: Applying process improvement methodologies (Lean, Plan-Do-Study-Act) to educational operations
