## Industry Variations

Systems and technology analysis in education addresses instructional and operational needs:

- **Student Information System Evaluation**: Analyzing SIS capabilities for enrollment, grades, attendance, and reporting
- **Learning Management Systems**: Assessing LMS features, adoption rates, and integration with other platforms
- **Assessment Technology**: Evaluating testing platforms for state assessments and classroom formative assessment
- **Data Interoperability**: Analyzing ability of systems to share data using Ed-Fi and other standards
- **Accessibility Compliance**: Ensuring technology meets Section 508 and WCAG requirements for students with disabilities
- **Network Infrastructure**: Analyzing bandwidth, WiFi coverage, and device connectivity across campuses
- **Security Posture**: Evaluating cybersecurity controls protecting student and employee data
- **Technology Refresh Cycles**: Planning hardware and software replacement based on lifecycle analysis
