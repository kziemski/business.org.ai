## Industry Variations

Capturing stakeholder needs in education addresses diverse community constituencies:

- **Student Voice Mechanisms**: Surveys, focus groups, and student advisory councils capture student perspectives
- **Parent Input Channels**: Parent surveys, conferences, and advisory committees gather family feedback
- **Community Needs Assessment**: Understanding broader community expectations for schools
- **Employer Input (CTE)**: Business advisory committees inform career program relevance
- **Title I Parent Consultation**: Federal requirements mandate parent input in program planning
- **Special Education Input**: IEP meetings and parent advocacy groups provide special needs perspectives
- **Staff Surveys**: Employee climate surveys capture staff satisfaction and suggestions
- **Alumni Feedback**: Graduate follow-up provides perspective on preparation quality
