## Industry Variations

Meal eligibility certification follows federal school nutrition program requirements:

- **Income Verification**: Processing free and reduced-price meal applications based on federal income guidelines
- **Direct Certification**: Automated matching with SNAP, TANF, and other program data to certify eligibility
- **Community Eligibility Provision**: Schools with high poverty rates may serve universal free meals
- **Verification Sampling**: Annual verification of a sample of approved applications for accuracy
- **Confidentiality Protection**: Student meal status is protected information requiring secure handling
- **Point-of-Sale Systems**: Maintaining systems that protect student dignity while tracking eligibility
- **USDA Compliance**: Following detailed USDA regulations for meal program administration
- **Carryover Eligibility**: Managing eligibility carryover at the start of each school year
