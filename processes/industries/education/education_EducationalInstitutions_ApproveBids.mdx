## Industry Variations

Bid approval in education follows public procurement requirements:

- **Board Approval Thresholds**: Purchases above specified dollar thresholds require formal board of education approval
- **Competitive Bidding Requirements**: State laws mandate competitive bidding for purchases exceeding threshold amounts
- **Lowest Responsible Bidder**: Public schools typically must accept the lowest responsible and responsive bid
- **Cooperative Purchasing**: Bids may reference pricing from state or regional purchasing cooperatives
- **Local Preference Policies**: Some jurisdictions allow or require preferences for local vendors
- **Curriculum Material Exceptions**: Textbook and curriculum adoptions may follow different approval processes
- **Emergency Procurement**: Documented emergency procedures allow expedited approval for urgent needs
- **Conflict of Interest Review**: Bid approval includes verification of no conflicts with board members or staff
