## Industry Variations

Risk management in education addresses unique institutional vulnerabilities:

- **Student Safety Risks**: Active shooter preparedness, bullying prevention, and student supervision protocols
- **Liability Exposures**: Field trip safety, athletic injuries, and playground supervision risks
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Title IX, IDEA, FERPA, and civil rights compliance risks
- **Reputation Management**: Media relations, social media incidents, and community perception risks
- **Financial Risks**: Enrollment decline, funding formula changes, and pension liability exposures
- **Property Risks**: Building condition, natural disaster exposure, and fleet safety
- **Employment Practices**: Discrimination claims, wrongful termination, and collective bargaining disputes
- **Cybersecurity**: Student data breach risks and ransomware vulnerability
