## Industry Variations

Analyzing contribution to district goals involves education-specific frameworks:

- **Strategic Plan Alignment**: Campus and department activities are mapped to district strategic plan priorities and key performance indicators
- **State Accountability Contribution**: Individual school performance contributes to overall district accountability ratings
- **Continuous Improvement Planning**: Campus improvement plans document how school-level efforts support district goals
- **Multi-Measure Dashboards**: Progress tracking uses balanced scorecards including academics, climate, finance, and operations
- **Theory of Action**: Educational improvement often uses explicit logic models connecting inputs, activities, and outcomes
- **Equity Analysis**: Goal contribution is examined through equity lenses ensuring all student groups benefit
- **Resource Alignment**: Budget allocations are justified through explicit connection to strategic priorities
- **Board Reporting**: Progress toward goals is regularly communicated to governing boards and the public
