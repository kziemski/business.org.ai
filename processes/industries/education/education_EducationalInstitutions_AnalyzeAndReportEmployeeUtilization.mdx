## Industry Variations

Employee utilization analysis in education involves distinct metrics and considerations:

- **Teaching Load Analysis**: Measuring instructional minutes, class sizes, and preparation periods per teacher
- **Student-Staff Ratios**: Tracking staffing efficiency across schools while maintaining quality instructional delivery
- **Specialization Coverage**: Ensuring art, music, PE, library, and other specialist positions are efficiently deployed across campuses
- **Substitute Utilization**: Tracking substitute teacher usage patterns and associated costs
- **Support Staff Allocation**: Analyzing paraprofessional, counselor, and administrator deployment relative to student needs
- **Contracted vs Direct Services**: Comparing utilization and costs for contracted versus employed service providers
- **Faculty Workload (Higher Ed)**: Tracking teaching, research, and service components of faculty assignments
- **Adjunct Reliance**: Monitoring the proportion of courses taught by part-time versus full-time faculty
