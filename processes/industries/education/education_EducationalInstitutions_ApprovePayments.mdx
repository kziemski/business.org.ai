## Industry Variations

Payment approval in education follows public sector accountability requirements:

- **Multi-Level Approval**: Invoices typically require department head approval before finance processing
- **Three-Way Matching**: Payments are verified against purchase orders and receiving documentation
- **Board Ratification**: Payment registers are presented to the board for ratification at public meetings
- **Grant Fund Segregation**: Payments from federal and state grants require additional compliance verification
- **Prompt Payment Requirements**: Some states mandate specific payment timing for public agencies
- **Vendor Certification**: Payments over thresholds may require W-9 verification and debarment checks
- **Split Funding**: Single invoices may require allocation across multiple fund sources and cost centers
- **Activity Fund Controls**: Student activity fund payments follow separate approval procedures
