## Industry Variations

Emerging technology analysis in education focuses on learning and operational applications:

- **AI in Education**: Evaluating adaptive learning platforms, automated feedback systems, and AI tutoring applications
- **Immersive Technologies**: Assessing VR/AR applications for science labs, historical simulations, and career exploration
- **Learning Analytics**: Emerging platforms that provide real-time student progress monitoring and intervention alerts
- **Accessibility Technology**: New assistive technologies supporting students with disabilities
- **Security Innovations**: Emerging safety and security technologies for campus protection
- **Interoperability Standards**: Evaluating technologies for compliance with Ed-Fi and LTI integration standards
- **Student Data Privacy**: Analyzing emerging technologies for FERPA, COPPA, and state privacy compliance
- **Pilot Program Design**: Structuring controlled trials of new technologies with clear success metrics
