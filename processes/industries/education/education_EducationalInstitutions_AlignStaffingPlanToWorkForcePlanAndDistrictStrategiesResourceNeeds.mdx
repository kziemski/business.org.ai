## Industry Variations

Staffing plan alignment in education involves unique considerations:

- **Enrollment Projections**: Staffing levels are driven by student enrollment forecasts and resulting class size requirements
- **Student-Teacher Ratios**: State regulations and contract provisions often mandate maximum class sizes that determine staffing needs
- **Special Education Requirements**: IEP caseload limits and service delivery models significantly impact staffing calculations
- **Certification Alignment**: Staff assignments must match teacher certification areas with course assignments
- **Recruiting Calendar**: Teacher hiring cycles follow academic calendars with peak activity in spring for fall positions
- **Hard-to-Staff Positions**: Districts must plan for persistent shortages in STEM, special education, and bilingual education
- **Reduction in Force Protocols**: Declining enrollment may trigger RIF procedures governed by contract seniority provisions
- **Grant-Funded Positions**: Some positions depend on continuing grant funding requiring contingency planning
