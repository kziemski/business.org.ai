## Industry Variations

Organizational analysis in education examines unique structural characteristics:

- **Governance Structure**: Analyzing effectiveness of board governance, superintendent leadership, and administrative organization
- **School Configurations**: Examining grade-level configurations (K-5, K-8, 6-8, 9-12) and their operational implications
- **Central vs Site-Based**: Assessing balance between central office control and school-level autonomy
- **Organizational Culture**: Understanding teaching and learning culture, professional community, and collaborative practices
- **Leadership Pipeline**: Analyzing succession planning and administrator development capacity
- **Collective Bargaining Impact**: Understanding how union relationships shape organizational flexibility
- **Community Partnerships**: Analyzing network of relationships with community organizations, businesses, and higher education
- **Size and Scale**: Examining implications of district size for administrative efficiency and service delivery
