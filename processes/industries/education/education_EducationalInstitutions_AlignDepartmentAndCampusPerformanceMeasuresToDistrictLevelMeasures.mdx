## Industry Variations

Performance measure alignment in education has unique requirements:

- **State Accountability Systems**: Campus and district measures must align with state accountability frameworks and ratings systems
- **Federal Reporting Requirements**: Performance metrics must support ESSA, Title I, and other federal program reporting
- **Achievement Gap Focus**: Measures must enable disaggregated analysis by student subgroups to identify and address equity gaps
- **Multiple Domains**: Educational performance includes academic achievement, growth, graduation rates, college readiness, and school climate indicators
- **Leading and Lagging Indicators**: Schools balance summative outcome measures with formative progress indicators for continuous improvement
- **Comparison School Identification**: Measures should enable comparison with demographically similar schools for meaningful benchmarking
- **Campus Improvement Plans**: Individual school performance measures must connect to campus improvement planning processes
- **Board Reporting**: District governance requires clear performance dashboards showing progress toward strategic plan goals
