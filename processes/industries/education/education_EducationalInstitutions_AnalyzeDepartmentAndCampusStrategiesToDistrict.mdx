## Industry Variations

Analyzing campus and department strategies in education ensures system coherence:

- **Campus Improvement Plans**: Each school develops improvement plans that must align with district strategic priorities
- **Theory of Action Coherence**: Campus strategies should connect logically to district-level theories of change
- **Resource Request Alignment**: Budget requests from campuses are evaluated for strategic plan alignment
- **Performance Target Cascading**: District goals cascade to campus and classroom-level targets
- **Department Coordination**: Central office departments (curriculum, HR, technology) coordinate around common strategic themes
- **Cross-Campus Collaboration**: Analysis identifies opportunities for schools to share successful strategies
- **Vertical Alignment**: K-12 strategies ensure coherent student progression across elementary, middle, and high school levels
- **Feedback Loops**: Campus implementation experiences inform refinement of district-level strategies
