## Industry Variations

Requisition approval in education involves budget and compliance verification:

- **Budget Availability Check**: Requisitions are verified against available budget before approval
- **Multi-Level Workflow**: Approval routes through department head, principal, and central office based on amount
- **Fund Source Verification**: Requisitions identify specific fund, function, and object codes for proper charging
- **Curriculum Review**: Instructional material requisitions may require curriculum department approval
- **Technology Standards**: Technology purchases require IT review for compatibility and support standards
- **Contract Approval Requirements**: Requisitions involving service contracts may require additional legal review
- **Site-Based Budget Authority**: Schools have delegated approval authority within their allocated budgets
- **Year-End Cutoffs**: Requisitions are subject to fiscal year-end deadlines for encumbrance
