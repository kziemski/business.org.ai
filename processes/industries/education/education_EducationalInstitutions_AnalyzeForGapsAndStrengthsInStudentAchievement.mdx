## Industry Variations

Student achievement gap analysis is central to educational improvement:

- **Disaggregated Data Analysis**: Examining performance differences among racial, economic, disability, and language learner subgroups
- **Growth vs Proficiency**: Analyzing both absolute achievement levels and student learning growth over time
- **Standards Alignment**: Identifying specific learning standards where students demonstrate strength or struggle
- **Cohort Tracking**: Following student cohorts across years to understand learning trajectories
- **Predictive Analytics**: Using early warning indicators to identify students at risk before assessment results arrive
- **Root Cause Analysis**: Investigating systemic factors contributing to achievement gaps
- **Benchmark Comparisons**: Comparing achievement to state averages, similar schools, and national norms
- **Intervention Effectiveness**: Evaluating which instructional strategies successfully close identified gaps
