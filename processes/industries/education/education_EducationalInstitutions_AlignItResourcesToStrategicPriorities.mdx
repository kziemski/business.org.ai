## Industry Variations

Aligning IT resources to strategic priorities in education involves distinct considerations:

- **Instructional Technology Priority**: Resources must support classroom technology, digital curriculum, and online learning platforms
- **Infrastructure for All Campuses**: Districts must ensure equitable technology access across schools regardless of location or demographics
- **1:1 Device Programs**: Significant resources support student device deployment, management, and refresh cycles
- **Cybersecurity for Student Data**: FERPA compliance and protecting minor student data requires specialized security investments
- **Community Connectivity**: Some districts extend IT resources to support student home internet access
- **Staff Technology Proficiency**: Resources include ongoing technology training for educators at varying skill levels
- **E-Rate Funding Alignment**: Federal E-Rate programs provide telecommunications discounts that influence resource allocation decisions
- **Emergency Preparedness**: IT resources must support crisis communication, safety systems, and remote learning continuity
