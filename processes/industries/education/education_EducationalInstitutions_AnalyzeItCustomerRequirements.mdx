## Industry Variations

IT customer requirements in education span instructional and administrative users:

- **Teacher Technology Needs**: Understanding requirements for classroom instruction, grading, communication, and assessment
- **Student Device Requirements**: Age-appropriate specifications for student devices across grade levels
- **Administrative Systems**: Requirements for student information, HR, finance, and facilities management systems
- **Learning Management**: LMS requirements for course delivery, assignment submission, and parent portals
- **Assessment Platform Needs**: Requirements for standardized testing, formative assessment, and progress monitoring
- **Accessibility Requirements**: Ensuring technology meets needs of students and staff with disabilities
- **Home Access Considerations**: Requirements considering varying home technology environments and connectivity
- **Security and Privacy**: FERPA and COPPA requirements shape technology specifications
