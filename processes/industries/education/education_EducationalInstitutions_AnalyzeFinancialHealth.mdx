## Industry Variations

Financial health analysis in education uses public sector metrics and considerations:

- **Fund Balance Ratios**: Analyzing reserve levels against state-mandated minimums and board policy targets
- **Revenue Diversification**: Assessing reliance on state funding versus local property taxes, federal grants, and other sources
- **Structural Balance**: Determining whether ongoing revenues support ongoing expenditures without one-time resources
- **Cash Flow Projections**: Managing cash position given irregular timing of state aid payments and tax collections
- **Debt Service Coverage**: Analyzing ability to meet bond payment obligations from available resources
- **Per-Pupil Spending Trends**: Tracking expenditure trends relative to enrollment changes and peer districts
- **Audit Findings**: Monitoring and addressing findings from annual financial audits and compliance reviews
- **Credit Ratings**: Maintaining financial practices that support favorable bond ratings for capital financing
