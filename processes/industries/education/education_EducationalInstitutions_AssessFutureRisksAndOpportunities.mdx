## Industry Variations

Future risk and opportunity assessment in education addresses sector-specific concerns:

- **Enrollment Decline Risk**: Demographic shifts, school choice expansion, and declining birth rates threaten enrollment-based funding
- **Funding Volatility**: State budget fluctuations and changes to funding formulas create financial uncertainty
- **Teacher Shortage Risk**: Persistent shortages in key subject areas and declining interest in teaching careers
- **Technology Disruption**: Online learning, AI in education, and personalized learning platforms may transform delivery models
- **Regulatory Changes**: Shifts in federal and state education policy create compliance risks and opportunities
- **Community Development**: New residential construction or economic decline affects enrollment and tax base
- **Public Perception**: School choice debates and accountability ratings affect community support
- **Partnership Opportunities**: Higher education, business, and community collaborations offer innovation potential
