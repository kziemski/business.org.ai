## Industry Variations

Extended learning curriculum alignment ensures program coherence with regular instruction:

- **Standards Alignment**: After-school and summer programs reinforce the same learning standards taught during the school day
- **Academic Intervention Focus**: Extended learning often targets students needing additional support in core subjects
- **Enrichment Balance**: Programs balance academic reinforcement with enrichment activities (arts, STEM, sports)
- **21st CCLC Requirements**: Many programs operate under 21st Century Community Learning Centers grants with specific requirements
- **Summer Slide Prevention**: Summer programs specifically address learning loss during extended breaks
- **Staff Coordination**: Regular classroom teachers and extended learning staff share curriculum and student progress information
- **Credit Recovery**: High school summer programs may offer credit recovery aligned with course standards
- **Community Partner Integration**: External providers deliver aligned programming within district frameworks
