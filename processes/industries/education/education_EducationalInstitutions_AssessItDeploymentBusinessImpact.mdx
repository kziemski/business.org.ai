## Industry Variations

IT deployment business impact assessment in education focuses on educational outcomes:

- **Instructional Continuity**: Assessing potential disruption to teaching and learning during deployment
- **Assessment Window Protection**: Ensuring deployments do not interfere with scheduled testing periods
- **Training Readiness**: Evaluating staff preparedness for new systems before go-live
- **Parent Access Impact**: Considering effects on parent portal functionality and family communication
- **Data Migration Risks**: Assessing potential for student record errors during system transitions
- **Integration Effects**: Evaluating downstream impacts on connected systems (SIS, LMS, assessment platforms)
- **Support Capacity**: Ensuring help desk can handle increased call volume during transition
- **Rollback Contingency**: Planning for reverting to previous systems if critical issues arise
