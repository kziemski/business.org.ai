## Industry Variations

Reimbursement and advance approval in education involves specific policies and constraints:

- **Travel Reimbursement**: Conference and professional development travel follow per diem rates and pre-approval requirements
- **Mileage Rates**: Employee use of personal vehicles follows IRS or board-adopted mileage reimbursement rates
- **Classroom Supply Reimbursement**: Teachers may be reimbursed for out-of-pocket classroom supply purchases within limits
- **Athletic Travel**: Student activity travel advances and reimbursements follow specific approval chains
- **Grant-Funded Travel**: Travel charged to federal grants requires additional compliance documentation
- **Receipt Requirements**: Original receipts are required with specific documentation standards
- **Cash Advance Controls**: Travel advances require timely reconciliation with return of unused funds
- **Credit Card Programs**: Some districts use purchasing cards with post-travel reconciliation requirements
