## Industry Variations

Discipline data analysis in education is subject to significant regulatory and equity considerations:

- **Disproportionality Analysis**: Federal requirements mandate examining discipline rates by race, gender, disability, and other subgroups
- **IDEA Compliance**: Special education discipline must track manifestation determinations and alternative placement decisions
- **Restorative Practices Metrics**: Schools adopting restorative approaches track alternatives to suspension and expulsion
- **Incident Pattern Recognition**: Data analysis identifies systemic patterns by time, location, and incident type
- **Civil Rights Reporting**: OCR data collection requires accurate discipline record keeping and reporting
- **Exclusionary Discipline Reduction**: Many districts have goals to reduce out-of-school suspensions and expulsions
- **PBIS Implementation**: Positive behavior intervention systems require ongoing data analysis for fidelity
- **Mental Health Correlations**: Discipline data may inform student support service needs and interventions
