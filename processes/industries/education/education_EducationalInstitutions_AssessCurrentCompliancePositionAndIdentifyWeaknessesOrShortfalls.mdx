## Industry Variations

Compliance assessment in education spans extensive regulatory requirements:

- **IDEA Compliance**: Ensuring special education programs meet Individuals with Disabilities Education Act requirements
- **Title I Program Review**: Assessing compliance with federal Title I funding requirements and parent involvement mandates
- **Title IX Requirements**: Evaluating gender equity in athletics and sexual harassment prevention programs
- **FERPA Compliance**: Reviewing student privacy protections and data handling procedures
- **Civil Rights Obligations**: Assessing compliance with non-discrimination requirements and OCR reporting
- **State Accreditation**: Meeting state accountability and accreditation requirements for schools and districts
- **Health and Safety Codes**: Ensuring facilities meet building codes, fire safety, and health regulations
- **Employment Law Compliance**: Reviewing HR practices for labor law, ADA, and equal employment compliance
