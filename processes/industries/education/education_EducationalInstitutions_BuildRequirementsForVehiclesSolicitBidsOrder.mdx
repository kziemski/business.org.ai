## Industry Variations

School vehicle procurement follows safety and regulatory requirements:

- **Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards**: School buses must meet specific FMVSS safety requirements
- **State Specifications**: States may mandate additional equipment, color, and construction standards
- **Accessibility Requirements**: Fleet must include wheelchair-accessible vehicles for students with disabilities
- **Alternative Fuel Consideration**: Growing emphasis on electric and propane buses for environmental and cost reasons
- **Procurement Cooperatives**: Many districts purchase through state contracts or purchasing cooperatives
- **Fleet Age Management**: Replacement cycles balance safety, maintenance costs, and budget constraints
- **Route-Based Specifications**: Vehicle size and capacity needs depend on route characteristics
- **Financing Options**: Large fleet purchases may use lease-purchase or bond financing arrangements
