## Industry Variations

IT security threat analysis in education involves protecting sensitive student data:

- **Student Data Protection**: FERPA-protected student records are prime targets for identity theft and require robust protection
- **Ransomware Vulnerability**: Schools are increasingly targeted by ransomware attacks disrupting operations
- **Remote Learning Security**: Hybrid and remote learning expanded attack surfaces and security considerations
- **Phishing Awareness**: Staff and student email systems are frequent targets requiring ongoing security awareness
- **Third-Party Application Risks**: Educational technology vendors require security vetting for student data protection
- **Social Engineering**: Schools may be targets for social engineering due to accessible culture and limited security resources
- **Physical Access**: Computer labs, devices, and network infrastructure require physical security considerations
- **Incident Response Planning**: Security breach notification requirements for student data under FERPA and state laws
