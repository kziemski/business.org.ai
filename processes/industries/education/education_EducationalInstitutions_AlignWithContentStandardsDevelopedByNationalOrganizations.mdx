## Industry Variations

Aligning with national content standards is central to educational practice:

- **National Council Standards**: Subject-area organizations (NCTM for math, NCTE for English, NGSS for science) publish voluntary standards that inform curriculum
- **Common Core Adoption**: Many states adopted Common Core State Standards for ELA and math, though implementation varies
- **College Board Alignment**: AP courses follow College Board standards and curriculum frameworks
- **ISTE Standards**: Technology integration follows International Society for Technology in Education standards
- **SEL Competencies**: CASEL frameworks guide social-emotional learning program alignment
- **Professional Standards**: Teacher preparation aligns with InTASC and subject-specific professional teaching standards
- **Career Readiness**: Employability skills align with frameworks from organizations like the Partnership for 21st Century Learning
- **Early Childhood Standards**: Pre-K programs align with NAEYC developmentally appropriate practice guidelines
