## Industry Variations

Benefit enrollment in educational institutions has distinct characteristics:

- **Multiple Bargaining Units**: Schools often have separate benefit packages negotiated with teachers unions, classified staff unions, and administrative associations
- **Academic Year Alignment**: Open enrollment periods are typically aligned with the academic calendar rather than calendar year
- **Sabbatical Benefits**: Higher education institutions offer sabbatical leave programs not found in other industries
- **Tuition Remission**: Educational benefits including tuition waivers for employees and dependents are a significant benefit component
- **Summer Coverage**: Addressing benefits continuity for 9-10 month employees during summer months requires special plan designs
- **Retirement Systems**: Many educators participate in state teacher retirement systems (TRS, STRS) rather than private pension plans or 401(k)s
- **Adjunct Considerations**: Part-time faculty benefit eligibility and ACA compliance require careful hours tracking and plan design
