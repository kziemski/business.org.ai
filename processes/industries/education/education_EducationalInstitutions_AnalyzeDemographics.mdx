## Industry Variations

Demographic analysis in education serves multiple critical planning functions:

- **Enrollment Projections**: Demographic data drives student enrollment forecasting for staffing and facility planning
- **Boundary Planning**: School attendance zone decisions consider demographic distribution for equity and capacity balance
- **Subgroup Tracking**: ESSA requires monitoring achievement for racial, economic, disability, and English learner subgroups
- **Teacher Diversity**: Districts analyze workforce demographics seeking to increase teacher diversity to match student populations
- **Community Changes**: Residential development, migration patterns, and economic shifts require ongoing demographic monitoring
- **Equity Analysis**: Resource allocation is examined through demographic lenses to ensure equitable distribution
- **Title I Eligibility**: Poverty demographics determine school eligibility for federal Title I funding
- **Birth Rate Trends**: Long-term planning incorporates birth data to project future kindergarten enrollment
