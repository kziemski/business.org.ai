## Industry Variations

Bank fee management for educational institutions involves public sector considerations:

- **Public Deposit Requirements**: State laws govern which financial institutions can hold public funds and collateralization requirements
- **Earnings Credit Analysis**: Evaluating compensating balance arrangements against explicit fee structures
- **Lockbox Services**: Managing donation and tuition payment processing through bank lockbox arrangements
- **ACH Payment Fees**: Negotiating fees for electronic payroll and vendor payments
- **Investment Policy Compliance**: Bank services must align with board-adopted investment policies for public funds
- **Bid Requirements**: Banking services may be subject to competitive procurement requirements
- **Regional Bank Considerations**: Some districts prioritize local banking relationships for community partnership reasons
- **Grant Fund Segregation**: Federal grants may require separate bank accounts with specific fee tracking
