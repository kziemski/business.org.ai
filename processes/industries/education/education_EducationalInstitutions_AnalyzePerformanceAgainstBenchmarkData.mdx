## Industry Variations

Benchmarking in education uses specialized comparison frameworks:

- **Peer District Comparisons**: Comparing performance with demographically similar districts or aspirational peers
- **State and National Norms**: Measuring achievement against state averages and national percentile rankings
- **Value-Added Measures**: Benchmarking student growth compared to expected growth for similar students
- **Financial Benchmarking**: Comparing spending per pupil, administrative costs, and resource allocation patterns
- **NAEP Comparisons**: Using National Assessment of Educational Progress for national benchmarking
- **International Assessments**: Higher education may benchmark against OECD PISA or TIMSS international data
- **Best Practice Identification**: Identifying high-performing schools or districts for practice benchmarking
- **Consortium Data Sharing**: Participating in data collaboratives for confidential benchmarking analysis
