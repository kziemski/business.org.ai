# Establish and operate enterprise architecture governance

## Overview

Establishing and operating a structure by which an enterprise defines appropriate strategies and ensures development alignment with those strategies. Create and establish the rules, regulations, policies, and standards that will govern the individual components of the IT architecture, as well as the architecture in its entirety.

## Industry Variations

IT in life sciences must validate systems per 21 CFR Part 11 for electronic records and signatures. Data integrity (ALCOA+ principles) is paramount for regulatory compliance. Systems supporting clinical trials, manufacturing, and pharmacovigilance require extensive validation and audit trails. Cybersecurity protects valuable intellectual property and patient data.
