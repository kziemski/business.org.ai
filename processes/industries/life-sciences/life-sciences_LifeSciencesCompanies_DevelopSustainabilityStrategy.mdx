# Develop sustainability strategy

## Overview

Formulating strategic options that create opportunities for the sustenance and prosperity of the business in the long run. Go beyond business longevity to consider alternate strategies that allow the organizations preservation of vitality over time. Earmark resources and target processes, the former of which are dedicated to the absorption of sustainable practices in the latter.

## Industry Variations

In the life sciences industry, strategy development must account for lengthy drug development timelines (10-15 years), high R&D costs, and complex regulatory pathways. Strategic planning integrates pipeline management, patent lifecycles, and competitive therapeutic landscape analysis. Life sciences companies must balance innovation investment with commercial viability while navigating FDA, EMA, and other global regulatory requirements.
