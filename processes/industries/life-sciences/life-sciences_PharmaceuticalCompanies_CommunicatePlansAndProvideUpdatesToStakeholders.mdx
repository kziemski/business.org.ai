# Communicate plans and provide updates to stakeholders

## Overview

Conveying the plans for HR function to stakeholders. Ensure that the HR plans and strategy are effectively communicated to the people who can affect or be affected by the organization's actions, objectives, and policies such as the creditors, shareholders, employees, and suppliers. Provide regular updates to these stakeholders to ensure effective communication.

## Industry Variations

Human capital management in life sciences addresses specialized talent needs including PhD scientists, regulatory affairs professionals, and medical directors. Compliance training on GxP, HIPAA, and industry ethics is mandatory. The industry competes for talent with academic institutions and requires ongoing scientific education. Performance management must align with both business and scientific objectives.
