# Develop product/service design specifications

## Overview

Creating design specifications. Create specifications for the design of new or revised product/service concepts as a measure to meet during development. Have the senior functional-level solutioning or design staff create a framework of compliance standards for these products/services.

## Industry Variations

Product development in life sciences follows rigorous scientific and regulatory protocols including preclinical studies, clinical trials (Phase I-III), and regulatory submissions. Quality by Design (QbD) principles guide formulation development, and Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) compliance is mandatory. Life sciences R&D requires extensive documentation for regulatory filings and intellectual property protection.
