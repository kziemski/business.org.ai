# Communicate mission

## Overview

Developing and executing a communication strategy to convey the mission statement. Create a universal communication strategy and appropriate delivery channels, with the objective of leveraging the latter to execute the former. Convey the inherent message of the mission to all stakeholders, including employees, customers, and the public. Ensure collaboration between senior strategy personnel and the communications/marketing team.

## Industry Variations

In the life sciences industry, strategy development must account for lengthy drug development timelines (10-15 years), high R&D costs, and complex regulatory pathways. Strategic planning integrates pipeline management, patent lifecycles, and competitive therapeutic landscape analysis. Life sciences companies must balance innovation investment with commercial viability while navigating FDA, EMA, and other global regulatory requirements.
