# Define employee development guidelines

## Overview

Outlining the guidelines for development of employees. Design development policies and procedures to identify areas of growth for employees, either in their current position or in preparation for future roles. Include topics related to knowledge and skill development.

## Industry Variations

Human capital management in life sciences addresses specialized talent needs including PhD scientists, regulatory affairs professionals, and medical directors. Compliance training on GxP, HIPAA, and industry ethics is mandatory. The industry competes for talent with academic institutions and requires ongoing scientific education. Performance management must align with both business and scientific objectives.
