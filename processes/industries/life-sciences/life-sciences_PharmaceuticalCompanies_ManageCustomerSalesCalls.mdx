# Manage customer sales calls

## Overview

Managing the entire sales process, from using leads to open sales to closing sales and creating records. Govern all sales activities. Make sales calls based on leads and preparatory work (drafting terms of the sale, creating proposals, suggesting prices, etc.). Close the sale, along with any administrative activities related to data entry and the processing of the sale.

## Industry Variations

Life sciences sales and marketing operates under strict FDA promotional guidelines and industry codes (PhRMA, AdvaMed). Medical affairs teams support scientific exchange with healthcare professionals. Market access strategies must address payer negotiations, formulary placement, and patient assistance programs. Digital engagement with HCPs has become increasingly important alongside traditional detailing.
