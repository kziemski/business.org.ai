# Receive customer problems, requests, and inquiries

## Overview

Receiving requests for information from customers over multiple channels. Receive various requests and inquiries from customers regarding products/services. Accept these inquiries through channels such as email, telephone, online forms, text messages, social media, and in person. Supply dedicated equipment, systems, and personnel.

## Industry Variations

Customer service in life sciences encompasses healthcare provider inquiries, patient assistance programs, and adverse event intake. All customer interactions must comply with pharmacovigilance requirements for safety reporting. Medical information requests require scientific accuracy and regulatory compliance. Patient support teams help navigate insurance, copay assistance, and therapy management.
