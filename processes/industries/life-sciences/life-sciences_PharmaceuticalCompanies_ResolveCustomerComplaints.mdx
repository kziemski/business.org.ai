# Resolve customer complaints

## Overview

Resolving any customer complaints that are deemed to be sound and reasonable. Redress any objections, grievances, and complaints received from customers regarding the offerings provided by the organization. Identify the legitimate complaints, where the situation needs to be appropriately corrected. Deploy personnel who can rectify the issue within a stipulated time frame.

## Industry Variations

Customer service in life sciences encompasses healthcare provider inquiries, patient assistance programs, and adverse event intake. All customer interactions must comply with pharmacovigilance requirements for safety reporting. Medical information requests require scientific accuracy and regulatory compliance. Patient support teams help navigate insurance, copay assistance, and therapy management.
