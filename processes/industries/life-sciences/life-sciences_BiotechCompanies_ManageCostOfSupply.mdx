# Manage cost of supply

## Overview

Managing all expenses to provide products/services in the market. Estimate the overall cost of supplying of products/inventory, including the cost distributing it through various partners and channels. Consider the cost of all the logistical processes that occur from the moment a product is ready to be dispatched to the time it reaches the destination.

## Industry Variations

Life sciences product delivery requires cold chain logistics, serialization for track-and-trace compliance, and adherence to Good Distribution Practice (GDP). Supply chain must maintain product integrity for temperature-sensitive biologics and controlled substances. Batch traceability and recall readiness are critical requirements for pharmaceutical distribution.
