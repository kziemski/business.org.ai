# Develop procurement plan

## Overview

Creating a plan for procuring materials and services. Plan what to buy, when, and from what sources. Include project requirements, the procurement team, the justification for the procurement, a timeline of events, and an explanation of the supplier selection process. Outline specific actions to start and complete purchases in order to adhere to best practices.

## Industry Variations

Life sciences product delivery requires cold chain logistics, serialization for track-and-trace compliance, and adherence to Good Distribution Practice (GDP). Supply chain must maintain product integrity for temperature-sensitive biologics and controlled substances. Batch traceability and recall readiness are critical requirements for pharmaceutical distribution.
