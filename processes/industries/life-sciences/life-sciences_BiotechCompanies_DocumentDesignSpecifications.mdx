# Document design specifications

## Overview

Documenting requirements to meet in the design of new or revised products/services. Specify technical, quality, and costing requirements, as well as ergonomic, safety, and servicing requirements for such products/services. Ensure the information presented can be understood by the personnel executing the design and includes examples, anecdotal references, and illustrations.

## Industry Variations

Product development in life sciences follows rigorous scientific and regulatory protocols including preclinical studies, clinical trials (Phase I-III), and regulatory submissions. Quality by Design (QbD) principles guide formulation development, and Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) compliance is mandatory. Life sciences R&D requires extensive documentation for regulatory filings and intellectual property protection.
