# Respond to IT information security and network breaches

## Overview

Address any form of unauthorized network breach such as unauthorized access or usage of data, applications, services, networks, and/or devices. Identify the root cause and take corrective measures to resolve the breach.

## Industry Variations

IT in life sciences must validate systems per 21 CFR Part 11 for electronic records and signatures. Data integrity (ALCOA+ principles) is paramount for regulatory compliance. Systems supporting clinical trials, manufacturing, and pharmacovigilance require extensive validation and audit trails. Cybersecurity protects valuable intellectual property and patient data.
