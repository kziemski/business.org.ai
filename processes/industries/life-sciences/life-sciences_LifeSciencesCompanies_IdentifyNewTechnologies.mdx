# Identify new technologies

## Overview

Determining new technologies to revise the portfolio of solution offerings. Identify recently developed technological advances that can be leveraged in the development or advancement of the organization's product/service portfolio. Enlist senior management in conjunction with personnel responsible for the design, processing, and delivery of products/services. Have the organization's research division(s) carry out the process.

## Industry Variations

Product development in life sciences follows rigorous scientific and regulatory protocols including preclinical studies, clinical trials (Phase I-III), and regulatory submissions. Quality by Design (QbD) principles guide formulation development, and Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) compliance is mandatory. Life sciences R&D requires extensive documentation for regulatory filings and intellectual property protection.
