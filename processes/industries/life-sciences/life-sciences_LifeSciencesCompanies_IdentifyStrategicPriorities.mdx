# Identify strategic priorities

## Overview

Creating a statement of the organization's direction to guide decision making around the allocation of resources. Provide a focus on the organization's overarching goals to ensure coherent and considered action. Strategic objectives are ranked by their importance in achieving the strategic goals. All subsequent operational or tactical planning and resource allocation is based on strategic priorities

## Industry Variations

In the life sciences industry, strategy development must account for lengthy drug development timelines (10-15 years), high R&D costs, and complex regulatory pathways. Strategic planning integrates pipeline management, patent lifecycles, and competitive therapeutic landscape analysis. Life sciences companies must balance innovation investment with commercial viability while navigating FDA, EMA, and other global regulatory requirements.
