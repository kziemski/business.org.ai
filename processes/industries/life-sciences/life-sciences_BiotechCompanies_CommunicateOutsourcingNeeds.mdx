# Communicate outsourcing needs

## Overview

Conveying outsourcing needs within the organization, with the objective of sourcing the assistance required. Define the portion of logistical activities that can be transferred to outside suppliers. Assess third-party agencies to carefully select the most appropriate agencies for outsourcing. Convey these needs to management or the appropriate authority.

## Industry Variations

Life sciences product delivery requires cold chain logistics, serialization for track-and-trace compliance, and adherence to Good Distribution Practice (GDP). Supply chain must maintain product integrity for temperature-sensitive biologics and controlled substances. Batch traceability and recall readiness are critical requirements for pharmaceutical distribution.
