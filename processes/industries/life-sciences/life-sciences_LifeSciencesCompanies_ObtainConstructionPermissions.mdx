# Obtain construction permissions

## Overview

Gathering the required permits for construction from the proper jurisdiction. This may include inspections during and after construction to verify that the new asset meets all national, regional, and local codes.

## Industry Variations

Asset management in life sciences covers GMP manufacturing facilities, laboratory equipment, and cleanroom environments. Facility qualification (IQ/OQ/PQ) and equipment validation are regulatory requirements. Preventive maintenance schedules must maintain validated states. Capital investments in biologics manufacturing and advanced therapies require long-term planning.
