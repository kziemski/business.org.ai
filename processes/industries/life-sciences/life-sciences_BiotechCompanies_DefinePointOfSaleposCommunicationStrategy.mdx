# Define point of sale (POS) communication strategy

## Overview

Establishing a framework for coordinated marketing to increase the profitability and increase brand awareness at the point of sale. This may include promotional posters on product shelves or island displays, advertisements in shopping carts, stickers on the floor that lead consumers to the promoted product, multi-buy promotions, coupons on sales receipts, etc.

## Industry Variations

Life sciences sales and marketing operates under strict FDA promotional guidelines and industry codes (PhRMA, AdvaMed). Medical affairs teams support scientific exchange with healthcare professionals. Market access strategies must address payer negotiations, formulary placement, and patient assistance programs. Digital engagement with HCPs has become increasingly important alongside traditional detailing.
