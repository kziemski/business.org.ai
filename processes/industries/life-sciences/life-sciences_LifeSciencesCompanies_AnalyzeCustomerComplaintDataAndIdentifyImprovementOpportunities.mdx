# Analyze customer complaint data and identify improvement opportunities

## Overview

Examining the information obtained through handling and resolving complaints for development/improvement opportunities. Categorize the customer complaints data on the basis of speed, accuracy, courtesy, price, product choice, availability, hours, location, etc. Determine complaint patterns in order to diagnose areas needing enhancement.

Customer service in life sciences encompasses healthcare provider inquiries, patient assistance programs, and adverse event intake. All customer interactions must comply with pharmacovigilance requirements for safety reporting. Medical information requests require scientific accuracy and regulatory compliance. Patient support teams help navigate insurance, copay assistance, and therapy management.
