# Gather and solicit post-sale customer feedback on products and services

## Overview

Obtaining customer feedback/review on the quality and utility derived from the products/services after the sale is complete. Use techniques such as surveys, feedback boxes, and user activity and usability tests.

## Industry Variations

Customer service in life sciences encompasses healthcare provider inquiries, patient assistance programs, and adverse event intake. All customer interactions must comply with pharmacovigilance requirements for safety reporting. Medical information requests require scientific accuracy and regulatory compliance. Patient support teams help navigate insurance, copay assistance, and therapy management.
