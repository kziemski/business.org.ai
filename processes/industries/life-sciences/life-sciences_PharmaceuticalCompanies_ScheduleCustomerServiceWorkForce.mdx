# Schedule customer service work force

## Overview

Deploying the work force to manage customer service contracts. Create a systematic summary of the operations and service required, as well as the specific amount of work force that is to be deployed to the customer service operations. Ensure work force is directly proportional to the estimated forecast of customer service contracts.

## Industry Variations

Customer service in life sciences encompasses healthcare provider inquiries, patient assistance programs, and adverse event intake. All customer interactions must comply with pharmacovigilance requirements for safety reporting. Medical information requests require scientific accuracy and regulatory compliance. Patient support teams help navigate insurance, copay assistance, and therapy management.
