# Define current business

## Overview

Defining the status quo relating to the de facto core of what the business is. Reflect over the fundamental essence of what the business accomplishes and the manner in which it operates. Look beyond the obvious solution capabilities to delineate capacities that form the basis of the business engine. Involve senior executives and management personnel and possibly professional services providers.

## Industry Variations

In the life sciences industry, strategy development must account for lengthy drug development timelines (10-15 years), high R&D costs, and complex regulatory pathways. Strategic planning integrates pipeline management, patent lifecycles, and competitive therapeutic landscape analysis. Life sciences companies must balance innovation investment with commercial viability while navigating FDA, EMA, and other global regulatory requirements.
