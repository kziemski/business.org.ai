# Develop and test prototype production and/or service delivery process

## Overview

Creating the new manufacturing/delivery processes for the new products/services, and testing them to ensure proper functioning. Create the production/delivery process for the prototypes that have been built for the new products/services. Conduct trial-runs to test these processes and their integration with the organization's other processes.

## Industry Variations

Product development in life sciences follows rigorous scientific and regulatory protocols including preclinical studies, clinical trials (Phase I-III), and regulatory submissions. Quality by Design (QbD) principles guide formulation development, and Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) compliance is mandatory. Life sciences R&D requires extensive documentation for regulatory filings and intellectual property protection.
