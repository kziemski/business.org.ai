# Analyze deal options

## Overview

Examining various options shortlisted for assimilating new entities into the organization or dissociating from it. Undertake a piecemeal and comprehensive consideration of each option identified for acquisition, merger, de-merger, and divestment. Consider intangible and non-material aspects of the entities involved and synergic aspects. Consider the assistance of specialist professional services.

## Industry Variations

In the life sciences industry, strategy development must account for lengthy drug development timelines (10-15 years), high R&D costs, and complex regulatory pathways. Strategic planning integrates pipeline management, patent lifecycles, and competitive therapeutic landscape analysis. Life sciences companies must balance innovation investment with commercial viability while navigating FDA, EMA, and other global regulatory requirements.
