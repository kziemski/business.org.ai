# Conduct in-house product/service testing and evaluate feasibility

## Overview

Carrying out an in-house appraisal of the prototypes in order to validate design and feasibility. Test the product/service prototypes to confirm their compliance with design and usability standards. Corroborate the viability of the design, and validate the feasibility of their production. Identify any areas for improvement.

## Industry Variations

Product development in life sciences follows rigorous scientific and regulatory protocols including preclinical studies, clinical trials (Phase I-III), and regulatory submissions. Quality by Design (QbD) principles guide formulation development, and Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) compliance is mandatory. Life sciences R&D requires extensive documentation for regulatory filings and intellectual property protection.
