# Process and audit carrier invoices and documents

## Overview

Organizing and inspecting all account statements and any other documentation for the carriers used in delivery. Create, manage, and inspect all documents related to the financial, regulatory, and administrative accounts of all the carriers/freights. Generate receipts for all freight transactions.

## Industry Variations

Life sciences product delivery requires cold chain logistics, serialization for track-and-trace compliance, and adherence to Good Distribution Practice (GDP). Supply chain must maintain product integrity for temperature-sensitive biologics and controlled substances. Batch traceability and recall readiness are critical requirements for pharmaceutical distribution.
