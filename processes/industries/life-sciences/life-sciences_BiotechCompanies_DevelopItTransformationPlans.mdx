# Develop IT transformation plans

## Overview

Developing a robust plan to replace or upgrade an organization's information technology systems. Understanding the business need of IT transformation from current to an expected state for the business. Developing a strategic plan for IT operating model, governance, service delivery, and workforce transformation.

## Industry Variations

IT in life sciences must validate systems per 21 CFR Part 11 for electronic records and signatures. Data integrity (ALCOA+ principles) is paramount for regulatory compliance. Systems supporting clinical trials, manufacturing, and pharmacovigilance require extensive validation and audit trails. Cybersecurity protects valuable intellectual property and patient data.
