# Establish training needs by analysis of required and available skills

## Overview

Determining the training necessitated by business processes, using an examination of skill sets that are needed by the organization and those already possessed. Examine the various skills required by individual employees. Design training in light of the availability of resources to provide specific segments of training.

## Industry Variations

Human capital management in life sciences addresses specialized talent needs including PhD scientists, regulatory affairs professionals, and medical directors. Compliance training on GxP, HIPAA, and industry ethics is mandatory. The industry competes for talent with academic institutions and requires ongoing scientific education. Performance management must align with both business and scientific objectives.
