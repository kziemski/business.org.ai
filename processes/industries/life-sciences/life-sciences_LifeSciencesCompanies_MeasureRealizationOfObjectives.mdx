# Measure realization of objectives

## Overview

Determining the accomplishment of HR goals and objectives. Evaluate the effectiveness of the HR function by estimating the present rate of achievement of the established objectives. Use metrics to determine if the objectives are being realized. Leverage measures such as turnover, training, return on human capital, costs of labor, and expenses per employee.

## Industry Variations

Human capital management in life sciences addresses specialized talent needs including PhD scientists, regulatory affairs professionals, and medical directors. Compliance training on GxP, HIPAA, and industry ethics is mandatory. The industry competes for talent with academic institutions and requires ongoing scientific education. Performance management must align with both business and scientific objectives.
