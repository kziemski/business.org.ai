# Collaborate demand with customers

## Overview

Working closely with the organization's customers to understand their drives and behavior, with the objective of estimating future demand. Reach out to customers through various means to understand their behavior patterns, usage elasticity, and degree of variability--and ultimately determine demand for each offering.

## Industry Variations

Life sciences product delivery requires cold chain logistics, serialization for track-and-trace compliance, and adherence to Good Distribution Practice (GDP). Supply chain must maintain product integrity for temperature-sensitive biologics and controlled substances. Batch traceability and recall readiness are critical requirements for pharmaceutical distribution.
