## Industry Variations

### Consumer Electronics Context

Physical product delivery in consumer electronics involves global supply chains, typically centered in Asia-Pacific manufacturing hubs. Just-in-time inventory management, reverse logistics for returns/recycling, and handling of fragile electronics require specialized packaging and shipping protocols.

### Key Differentiators

- **Rapid Innovation Cycles**: Product lifecycles of 12-24 months require agile processes
- **Global Supply Chain**: Manufacturing concentrated in Asia-Pacific with worldwide distribution
- **Regulatory Complexity**: Multiple certifications required (FCC, CE, UL, RoHS, WEEE)
- **Consumer Focus**: Direct relationship with end consumers drives customer-centric processes
