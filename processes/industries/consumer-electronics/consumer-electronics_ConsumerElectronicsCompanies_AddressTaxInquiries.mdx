### Consumer Electronics Context

Financial management navigates currency fluctuations, component cost volatility, and capital-intensive manufacturing investments. Product margins are typically thin (5-15%), requiring precise cost control and volume forecasting.

- **Regulatory Complexity**: Multiple certifications required (FCC, CE, UL, RoHS, WEEE)
- **Consumer Focus**: Direct relationship with end consumers drives customer-centric processes
