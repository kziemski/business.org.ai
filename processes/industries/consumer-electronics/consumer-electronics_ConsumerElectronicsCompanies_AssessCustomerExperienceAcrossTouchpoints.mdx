## Industry Variations

### Consumer Electronics Context

In the consumer electronics industry, strategy development emphasizes rapid innovation cycles, technology roadmapping, and competitive positioning against global players. Companies must anticipate consumer trends, plan for short product lifecycles (typically 12-24 months), and align with emerging technology standards (5G, Wi-Fi 7, USB-C).

### Key Differentiators

- **Rapid Innovation Cycles**: Product lifecycles of 12-24 months require agile processes
- **Global Supply Chain**: Manufacturing concentrated in Asia-Pacific with worldwide distribution
- **Regulatory Complexity**: Multiple certifications required (FCC, CE, UL, RoHS, WEEE)
- **Consumer Focus**: Direct relationship with end consumers drives customer-centric processes
