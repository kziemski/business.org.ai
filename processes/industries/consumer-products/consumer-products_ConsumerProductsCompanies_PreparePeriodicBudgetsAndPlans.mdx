# Prepare periodic budgets and plans

## Overview

Creating reports on a quarterly or annual basis for fund allocation. Create a financial statement that estimates revenues and expenses over a specific period of time. (Leverage budget methods such as cost-based and zero-based budgeting techniques, in light of the periodic targets outlined during Develop and maintain budget policies and procedures [10771].)

## Industry Variations

Financial management in consumer products addresses:
- **Trade spend management** - Complex accounting for promotions, deductions, and chargebacks
- **SKU profitability** - Cost allocation across thousands of product variants
- **Working capital optimization** - Managing inventory investment and payment terms
- **Currency hedging** - Managing commodity and FX exposure in global operations
- **Revenue growth management** - Price/pack architecture and promotion ROI analysis
