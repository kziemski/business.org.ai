# Perform consolidated reporting/review of cost management reports

## Overview

Making reports for all units to help higher management in decision making. Prepare combined financial statements of a parent company and its all subsidiaries (separate legal entities controlled by a parent company) showing assets, liabilities, equity, income, expenses and cash flows, and also going through periodic reports which shows the actual and estimated costs and their variances.

## Industry Variations

Financial management in consumer products addresses:
- **Trade spend management** - Complex accounting for promotions, deductions, and chargebacks
- **SKU profitability** - Cost allocation across thousands of product variants
- **Working capital optimization** - Managing inventory investment and payment terms
- **Currency hedging** - Managing commodity and FX exposure in global operations
- **Revenue growth management** - Price/pack architecture and promotion ROI analysis
