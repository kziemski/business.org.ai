# Prepare high-level business case and technical assessment

## Overview

Preparing a business-level business case and a technical feasibility assessment in order to move the product/service projects forward. Weigh the costs and benefits of designing, developing, and evaluating the shortlisted product/service concepts. Prepare a business case to justify the product/service projects. Conduct a technical appraisal to ensure that the organization has the technical know-how and resources to further develop these concepts.

## Industry Variations

Consumer products companies approach product development with distinct considerations:
- **Consumer-centric design** - Extensive use of focus groups, taste tests, and consumer panels
- **SKU proliferation management** - Balancing variety with operational complexity
- **Packaging innovation** - Significant focus on shelf appeal, sustainability, and convenience
- **Regulatory compliance** - Navigating FDA, FTC, and international food/safety regulations
- **Private label competition** - Differentiating against retailer own-brands
