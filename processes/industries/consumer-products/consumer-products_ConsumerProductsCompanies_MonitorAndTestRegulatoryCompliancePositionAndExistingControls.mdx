# Monitor and test regulatory compliance position and existing controls

## Overview

Monitoring, appraising, and evaluating the compliance position of the organization in order to fine-tune for effective remediation. Track efforts for handling regulatory and compliance requirements necessitated by law. Test the robustness of internal frameworks, procedures, and approaches for dealing with these requirements, in order to clearly identify any necessary changes.

## Industry Variations

Risk and compliance in consumer products covers:
- **Food safety regulations** - FDA, USDA, FSMA, and international food safety standards
- **Labeling compliance** - Nutritional facts, allergen warnings, and marketing claims
- **Product liability** - Managing recall risks and consumer safety claims
- **Supply chain traceability** - Track-and-trace requirements for ingredients and finished goods
- **Environmental compliance** - Packaging waste regulations and sustainability reporting
