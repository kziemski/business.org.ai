# Enter orders into system

## Overview

Analyzing all data relating to sales orders by entering it into a centralized repository, and using the insights generated to create opportunities for cross-/up-selling. Maintain up-to-date records of sales, along with current status. Include all sales activity with relevant information about the associated customers, and identify patterns in the data. Glean insights from these patterns to persuade customers to purchase additional offerings or upgrade purchased products/services.

## Industry Variations

Marketing and sales in consumer products has unique characteristics:
- **Trade marketing emphasis** - Heavy investment in retailer promotions, slotting fees, and co-op advertising
- **Category management** - Acting as category captain to optimize retailer shelf space
- **Shopper marketing** - Point-of-purchase displays, in-store promotions, and sampling programs
- **Omnichannel presence** - Balancing traditional retail with DTC and e-commerce channels
- **Brand building** - Long-term investment in brand equity and consumer loyalty programs
