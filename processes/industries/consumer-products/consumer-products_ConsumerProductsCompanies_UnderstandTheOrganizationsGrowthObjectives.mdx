Financial management in consumer products addresses:

- **SKU profitability** - Cost allocation across thousands of product variants
- **Working capital optimization** - Managing inventory investment and payment terms
- **Currency hedging** - Managing commodity and FX exposure in global operations
- **Revenue growth management** - Price/pack architecture and promotion ROI analysis
