# Refine business unit strategies in support of company strategy

## Overview

Evaluating existing business unit strategy based on the company's strategy and eliminate unwanted/unnecessary resources/elements and re-consider necessary resources to meet the overall company's strategy.

## Industry Variations

In the consumer products industry, strategy development places heavy emphasis on:
- **Brand portfolio management** - Balancing multiple product lines and brand hierarchies
- **Consumer insights integration** - Leveraging shopper data and market research for strategic decisions
- **Retail relationship strategy** - Coordinating with major retailers and managing trade spend
- **Speed to market** - Faster innovation cycles to respond to changing consumer preferences
- **Sustainability commitments** - Increasing focus on ESG goals and sustainable sourcing
