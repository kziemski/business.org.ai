# Refine promotional performance metrics

## Overview

Fine-tuning promotional activities by employing the insights gleaned from the quantitative, as well as any qualitative, performance evaluations. Change certain attributes of the schemes, campaigns, and programs deployed in order to increase the impact generated, in terms of measures already agreed upon such as customer uptake, market penetration, sustenance of impact created, and revenue growth through offerings marketed.

## Industry Variations

Marketing and sales in consumer products has unique characteristics:
- **Trade marketing emphasis** - Heavy investment in retailer promotions, slotting fees, and co-op advertising
- **Category management** - Acting as category captain to optimize retailer shelf space
- **Shopper marketing** - Point-of-purchase displays, in-store promotions, and sampling programs
- **Omnichannel presence** - Balancing traditional retail with DTC and e-commerce channels
- **Brand building** - Long-term investment in brand equity and consumer loyalty programs
