# Identify requirements for changes to manufacturing/delivery processes

## Overview

Identifying any changes that need to be effectuated in the organization's internal processes for manufacturing, and delivering the newly developed products/services. Determine if any changes need to be made to the production and distribution processes, in light of the new products/services. Begin production process planning. Prepare for factory layout planning. Generate shop-floor instructions and changes to the supply chain.

## Industry Variations

Consumer products companies approach product development with distinct considerations:
- **Consumer-centric design** - Extensive use of focus groups, taste tests, and consumer panels
- **SKU proliferation management** - Balancing variety with operational complexity
- **Packaging innovation** - Significant focus on shelf appeal, sustainability, and convenience
- **Regulatory compliance** - Navigating FDA, FTC, and international food/safety regulations
- **Private label competition** - Differentiating against retailer own-brands
