External relationship management in consumer products includes:
- **Retailer partnerships** - Joint business planning and collaborative forecasting
- **Supplier relationships** - Commodity sourcing, contract manufacturing, and co-packers
- **Industry associations** - Participation in GS1, FMI, and category trade groups
- **Government relations** - Engagement on food policy, labeling regulations, and trade issues
- **Community engagement** - Cause marketing, sustainability initiatives, and local partnerships
