# Communicate plans and provide updates to stakeholders

## Overview

Conveying the plans for HR function to stakeholders. Ensure that the HR plans and strategy are effectively communicated to the people who can affect or be affected by the organization's actions, objectives, and policies such as the creditors, shareholders, employees, and suppliers. Provide regular updates to these stakeholders to ensure effective communication.

## Industry Variations

Human capital management in consumer products emphasizes:
- **Frontline workforce** - Managing large manufacturing, warehouse, and field sales teams
- **Seasonal staffing** - Ramping capacity for promotional periods and holidays
- **Food safety training** - Mandatory certifications for production workers
- **Union relations** - Managing organized labor in manufacturing facilities
- **Field force effectiveness** - Training and incentivizing retail merchandisers and sales reps
