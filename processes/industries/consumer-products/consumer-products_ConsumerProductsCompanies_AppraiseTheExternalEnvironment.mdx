## Overview

Asset management in consumer products focuses on:

- **Distribution networks** - Warehouses, distribution centers, and cold storage facilities
- **Fleet management** - Delivery trucks and route optimization for DSD
- **Retail fixtures** - Coolers, displays, and point-of-sale equipment in retail locations
- **R&D laboratories** - Product development and quality testing facilities
