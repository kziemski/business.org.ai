# Establish training needs by analysis of required and available skills

## Overview

Determining the training necessitated by business processes, using an examination of skill sets that are needed by the organization and those already possessed. Examine the various skills required by individual employees. Design training in light of the availability of resources to provide specific segments of training.

## Industry Variations

Human capital management in consumer products emphasizes:
- **Frontline workforce** - Managing large manufacturing, warehouse, and field sales teams
- **Seasonal staffing** - Ramping capacity for promotional periods and holidays
- **Food safety training** - Mandatory certifications for production workers
- **Union relations** - Managing organized labor in manufacturing facilities
- **Field force effectiveness** - Training and incentivizing retail merchandisers and sales reps
