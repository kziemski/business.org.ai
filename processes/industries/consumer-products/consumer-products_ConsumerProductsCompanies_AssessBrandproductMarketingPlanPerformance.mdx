# Assess brand/product marketing plan performance

## Overview

Examining the performance of all marketing efforts, across multiple parameters in order create an optimal marketing mix. Examine these programs on various aspects, such as return on investment, brand penetration, brand sentiment, and brand impact. Leverage focus groups, client feedback, customer engagement tracking, analytics over customer decisions, activity over the marketing channels employed, etc. Survey the performance of all marketing programs.

## Industry Variations

Marketing and sales in consumer products has unique characteristics:
- **Trade marketing emphasis** - Heavy investment in retailer promotions, slotting fees, and co-op advertising
- **Category management** - Acting as category captain to optimize retailer shelf space
- **Shopper marketing** - Point-of-purchase displays, in-store promotions, and sampling programs
- **Omnichannel presence** - Balancing traditional retail with DTC and e-commerce channels
- **Brand building** - Long-term investment in brand equity and consumer loyalty programs
