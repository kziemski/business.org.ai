# Report on profitability

## Overview

Making a report about revenues generated by the organization or business unit concerned. This process requires the organization to create a report which shows how business is generating profits. Profits are the part which is left after paying all expenses directly related to the generation of the revenue, such as producing a product, and other expenses related to conducting business activities.

## Industry Variations

Financial management in consumer products addresses:
- **Trade spend management** - Complex accounting for promotions, deductions, and chargebacks
- **SKU profitability** - Cost allocation across thousands of product variants
- **Working capital optimization** - Managing inventory investment and payment terms
- **Currency hedging** - Managing commodity and FX exposure in global operations
- **Revenue growth management** - Price/pack architecture and promotion ROI analysis
