# Validate value proposition with target segments

## Overview

Validating the desirability of the perceived value delivered by the organization's offerings, to the targeted customer segment. Substantiate the value of the benefits accrued to the customers through the organization's offerings. Justify the value proposition in light of the targeted segments by gathering feedback (using teaser demonstrations, surveys, interviews, primary research studies, and customer case studies). Corroborate the benefits of the organization's offerings.

## Industry Variations

Marketing and sales in consumer products has unique characteristics:
- **Trade marketing emphasis** - Heavy investment in retailer promotions, slotting fees, and co-op advertising
- **Category management** - Acting as category captain to optimize retailer shelf space
- **Shopper marketing** - Point-of-purchase displays, in-store promotions, and sampling programs
- **Omnichannel presence** - Balancing traditional retail with DTC and e-commerce channels
- **Brand building** - Long-term investment in brand equity and consumer loyalty programs
