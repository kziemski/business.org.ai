# Analyze customer website activity

## Overview

Examining user activity on company, vendor or reseller websites to improve traffic on and to the website, improve user experience on the website to simplify purchasing process and encourage repeat purchases, and to increase the site's visibility in search engine results. Various metrics can be used to measure user activity, such as number of users who are new, returning or unique, time spent on page, session duration, bounce rate, click through rate, conversion rate, and others.

## Industry Variations

Marketing and sales in consumer products has unique characteristics:
- **Trade marketing emphasis** - Heavy investment in retailer promotions, slotting fees, and co-op advertising
- **Category management** - Acting as category captain to optimize retailer shelf space
- **Shopper marketing** - Point-of-purchase displays, in-store promotions, and sampling programs
- **Omnichannel presence** - Balancing traditional retail with DTC and e-commerce channels
- **Brand building** - Long-term investment in brand equity and consumer loyalty programs
