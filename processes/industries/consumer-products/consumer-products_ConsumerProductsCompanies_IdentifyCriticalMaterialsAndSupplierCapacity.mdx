# Identify critical materials and supplier capacity

## Overview

Identifying principal materials needed for the manufacturing process and the levels of supply that may be ensured for them. Determine the essential and crucial inventory items required for the smooth functioning of all manufacturing processes. Estimate the average, peak, and baseline capacities of various vendors and suppliers. Establish the capability of individual suppliers from the market and the vendors.

## Industry Variations

Physical product delivery in consumer products involves:
- **High-volume distribution** - Managing millions of units across multiple retail channels
- **Cold chain management** - Temperature-controlled logistics for perishables
- **Seasonal demand spikes** - Managing inventory for holidays and promotional periods
- **Retailer compliance** - Meeting strict delivery windows, labeling, and EDI requirements
- **Direct store delivery** - Route-to-market strategies for fresh and beverage products
