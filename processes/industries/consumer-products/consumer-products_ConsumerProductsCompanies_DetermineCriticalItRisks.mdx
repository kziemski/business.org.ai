IT management in consumer products involves:

- **Trade promotion management** - Complex systems for managing retailer promotions and deductions
- **Consumer data platforms** - CDPs and analytics for personalization and targeting
- **Manufacturing execution systems** - Real-time production monitoring and quality control
- **Retailer integration** - EDI, GDSN, and data synchronization with retail partners
