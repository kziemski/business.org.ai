Service delivery in consumer products focuses on:
- **Consumer support services** - Product inquiries, nutritional information, and usage guidance
- **Retailer services** - Category management support, planogram development, and merchandising
- **B2B customer service** - Supporting distributor and retail partner operations
- **Product recall management** - Rapid response protocols for safety issues
