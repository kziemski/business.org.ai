# Monitor the regulatory environment for changing or emerging regulations

## Overview

Analyzing and overseeing the regulatory environment in order to spot any changing or emerging regulations. This process element calls upon the organization to monitor the regulatory environment for any new statutes, policies, and enactments issued by the respective government authorities or those which have been updated.

## Industry Variations

Risk and compliance in consumer products covers:
- **Food safety regulations** - FDA, USDA, FSMA, and international food safety standards
- **Labeling compliance** - Nutritional facts, allergen warnings, and marketing claims
- **Product liability** - Managing recall risks and consumer safety claims
- **Supply chain traceability** - Track-and-trace requirements for ingredients and finished goods
- **Environmental compliance** - Packaging waste regulations and sustainability reporting
