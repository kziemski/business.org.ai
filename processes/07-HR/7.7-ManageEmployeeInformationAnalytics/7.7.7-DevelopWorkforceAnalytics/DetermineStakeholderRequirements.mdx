# Determine stakeholder requirements

> Collect and manage requirements from various enterprise stakeholders about workforce analytics.

## Overview

Activity 7.7.7.1 is an activity within the Develop and Manage Human Capital framework. 

Collect and manage requirements from various enterprise stakeholders about workforce analytics.

This process involves systematic analysis and determination of stakeholder requirements. It requires data gathering, stakeholder input, comparative analysis, and structured decision-making to arrive at well-informed conclusions that support organizational objectives.
