# Identify research questions

## Overview

Activity 7.7.7.2 is an activity within the Develop and Manage Human Capital framework.

This process focuses on systematically identifying and analyzing research questions to support informed decision-making. It involves gathering relevant data, assessing current conditions, benchmarking against industry standards, and producing actionable insights that guide organizational strategy and operational improvements.
