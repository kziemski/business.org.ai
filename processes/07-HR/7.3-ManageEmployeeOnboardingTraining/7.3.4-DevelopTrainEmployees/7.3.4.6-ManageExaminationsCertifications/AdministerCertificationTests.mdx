# Administer certification tests

## Overview

Sub-Activity 7.3.4.6.2 is an activity within the Develop and Manage Human Capital framework. 

Providing tests to the workforce that will satisfy completion of certifications.

This process handles the administration of certification tests across the organization. It includes processing transactions, maintaining records, ensuring policy compliance, managing exceptions, and providing support to stakeholders throughout the administrative lifecycle.

## Industry Variations

### Healthcare

Requires mandatory continuing education (CME/CEU), clinical competency assessments, and compliance training for patient safety protocols.

### Financial Services

Emphasizes regulatory compliance training (SOX, AML, KYC), licensing requirements (Series 7, CFA), and ethics certification programs.

### Manufacturing

Focuses on safety certification (OSHA), equipment-specific training, lean/Six Sigma methodology, and apprenticeship programs.
