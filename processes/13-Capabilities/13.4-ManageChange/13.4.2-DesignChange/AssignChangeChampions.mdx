# Assign change champion(s)

> Utilizing champions that have been trained to carry out needed changes.

## Overview

The assign change champion(s) process is a critical component of the Business Capabilities function within an organization. It encompasses the systematic approach to assign change champion(s), ensuring that all activities are performed consistently, efficiently, and in alignment with organizational objectives. This process establishes the framework through which assign change champion(s) is executed, monitored, and continuously improved to deliver value across the enterprise.

Organizations that mature this process typically see improved operational performance, reduced risk exposure, and stronger alignment between tactical activities and strategic goals.
