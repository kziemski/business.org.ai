# Develop core KM methodologies

> Creating core knowledge management procedures and methodologies.

## Overview

The develop core km methodologies process is a critical component of the Business Capabilities function within an organization. It encompasses the systematic approach to develop core km methodologies, ensuring that all activities are performed consistently, efficiently, and in alignment with organizational objectives. This process establishes the framework through which develop core km methodologies is executed, monitored, and continuously improved to deliver value across the enterprise.

Within the APQC Process Classification Framework (hierarchy 13.5.1.5), this activity supports the broader "Develop and Manage Business Capabilities" category. Organizations that mature this process typically see improved operational performance, reduced risk exposure, and stronger alignment between tactical activities and strategic goals.
