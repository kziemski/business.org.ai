# Assess IT needs and engage IT function

> Determining the IT needs for developing the knowledge management strategy, and collaborating with the IT function to implement the strategy.

## Overview

The assess it needs and engage it function process is a critical component of the Business Capabilities function within an organization. It encompasses the systematic approach to assess it needs and engage it function, ensuring that all activities are performed consistently, efficiently, and in alignment with organizational objectives. This process establishes the framework through which assess it needs and engage it function is executed, monitored, and continuously improved to deliver value across the enterprise.

Within the APQC Process Classification Framework (hierarchy 13.5.1.6), this activity supports the broader "Develop and Manage Business Capabilities" category. Effective execution requires cross-functional collaboration, clear accountability, and robust governance mechanisms. Organizations that mature this process typically see improved operational performance, reduced risk exposure, and stronger alignment between tactical activities and strategic goals.
