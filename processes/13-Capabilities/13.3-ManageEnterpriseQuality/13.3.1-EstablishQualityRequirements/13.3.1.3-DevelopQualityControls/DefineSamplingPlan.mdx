# Define sampling plan

> Establishing a detailed summary including measures, on which material, in what manner, and by whom.

## Overview

The define sampling plan process is a critical component of the Business Capabilities function within an organization. It encompasses the systematic approach to define sampling plan, ensuring that all activities are performed consistently, efficiently, and in alignment with organizational objectives. This process establishes the framework through which define sampling plan is executed, monitored, and continuously improved to deliver value across the enterprise.

Within the APQC Process Classification Framework (hierarchy 13.3.1.3.2), this subactivity supports the broader "Develop and Manage Business Capabilities" category. Effective execution requires cross-functional collaboration, clear accountability, and robust governance mechanisms. Organizations that mature this process typically see improved operational performance, reduced risk exposure, and stronger alignment between tactical activities and strategic goals.
