# Conduct, monitor, and manage engineering efforts

## Overview

The conduct, monitor, and manage engineering efforts process is a critical component of the Business Capabilities function within an organization. It encompasses the systematic approach to conduct, monitor, and manage engineering efforts, ensuring that all activities are performed consistently, efficiently, and in alignment with organizational objectives. This process establishes the framework through which conduct, monitor, and manage engineering efforts is executed, monitored, and continuously improved to deliver value across the enterprise.

Within the APQC Process Classification Framework (hierarchy 13.9.2.3.34), this subactivity supports the broader "Develop and Manage Business Capabilities" category. Effective execution requires cross-functional collaboration, clear accountability, and robust governance mechanisms. Organizations that mature this process typically see improved operational performance, reduced risk exposure, and stronger alignment between tactical activities and strategic goals.
