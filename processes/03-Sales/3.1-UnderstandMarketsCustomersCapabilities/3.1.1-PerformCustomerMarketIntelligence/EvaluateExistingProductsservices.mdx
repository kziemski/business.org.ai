# Evaluate existing products/services

> Examining the brands owned and products offered in the market.

## Overview

Activity 3.1.1.5 is an activity within the Market and Sell Products and Services framework.

Examining the brands owned and products offered in the market. Determine the relative position of the existing products/brands in the marketplace.

This process is critical to effective sales and marketing execution. It ensures that activities are systematically planned, executed, and measured against organizational objectives. When performed effectively, this process drives revenue growth, enhances customer engagement, and strengthens competitive positioning in target markets.
