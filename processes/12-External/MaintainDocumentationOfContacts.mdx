# Maintain documentation of contacts

> Keeping a repository of documents that contain information about the network of partners, prospects and customers.

## Overview

The maintain documentation of contacts process is a critical component of the External Relationships function within an organization. It encompasses the systematic approach to maintain documentation of contacts, ensuring that all activities are performed consistently, efficiently, and in alignment with organizational objectives. This process establishes the framework through which maintain documentation of contacts is executed, monitored, and continuously improved to deliver value across the enterprise.

Within the APQC Process Classification Framework (hierarchy 12.2.2.3), this activity supports the broader "Manage External Relationships" category. Effective execution requires cross-functional collaboration, clear accountability, and robust governance mechanisms. Organizations that mature this process typically see improved operational performance, reduced risk exposure, and stronger alignment between tactical activities and strategic goals.
