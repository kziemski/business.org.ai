# Manage community relations

> Developing and administering community relations.

## Overview

The manage community relations process is a critical component of the External Relationships function within an organization. It encompasses the systematic approach to manage community relations, ensuring that all activities are performed consistently, efficiently, and in alignment with organizational objectives. This process establishes the framework through which manage community relations is executed, monitored, and continuously improved to deliver value across the enterprise.

Within the APQC Process Classification Framework (hierarchy 12.5.1), this process supports the broader "Manage External Relationships" category. Effective execution requires cross-functional collaboration, clear accountability, and robust governance mechanisms. Organizations that mature this process typically see improved operational performance, reduced risk exposure, and stronger alignment between tactical activities and strategic goals.
