# Issue press releases

> Issuing press releases to carefully selected media in distribution channels such as the web, newspapers, and social media.

## Overview

The issue press releases process is a critical component of the External Relationships function within an organization. It encompasses the systematic approach to issue press releases, ensuring that all activities are performed consistently, efficiently, and in alignment with organizational objectives. This process establishes the framework through which issue press releases is executed, monitored, and continuously improved to deliver value across the enterprise.

Within the APQC Process Classification Framework (hierarchy 12.5.5), this process supports the broader "Manage External Relationships" category. Effective execution requires cross-functional collaboration, clear accountability, and robust governance mechanisms. Organizations that mature this process typically see improved operational performance, reduced risk exposure, and stronger alignment between tactical activities and strategic goals.
