# Track legal activity/performance

> Keeping track of the legal activities and performance of the organization.

## Overview

The track legal activity/performance process is a critical component of the External Relationships function within an organization. It encompasses the systematic approach to track legal activity/performance, ensuring that all activities are performed consistently, efficiently, and in alignment with organizational objectives. This process establishes the framework through which track legal activity/performance is executed, monitored, and continuously improved to deliver value across the enterprise.

Within the APQC Process Classification Framework (hierarchy 12.4.5.6), this activity supports the broader "Manage External Relationships" category. Effective execution requires cross-functional collaboration, clear accountability, and robust governance mechanisms. Organizations that mature this process typically see improved operational performance, reduced risk exposure, and stronger alignment between tactical activities and strategic goals.
