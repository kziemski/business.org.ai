# Assess problem and determine work requirements

> Examining the problems and deciding the action requirements for engaging outside counsel.

## Overview

The assess problem and determine work requirements process is a critical component of the External Relationships function within an organization. It encompasses the systematic approach to assess problem and determine work requirements, ensuring that all activities are performed consistently, efficiently, and in alignment with organizational objectives. This process establishes the framework through which assess problem and determine work requirements is executed, monitored, and continuously improved to deliver value across the enterprise.

Within the APQC Process Classification Framework (hierarchy 12.4.5.1), this activity supports the broader "Manage External Relationships" category. Effective execution requires cross-functional collaboration, clear accountability, and robust governance mechanisms. Organizations that mature this process typically see improved operational performance, reduced risk exposure, and stronger alignment between tactical activities and strategic goals.
