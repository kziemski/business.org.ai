# Recycle parts

> Recycling parts in accordance with legal, regulatory, and social/environmental responsibilities.

## Overview

The recycle parts process is a critical component of the Asset Management function within an organization. It encompasses the systematic approach to recycle parts, ensuring that all activities are performed consistently, efficiently, and in alignment with organizational objectives. This process establishes the framework through which recycle parts is executed, monitored, and continuously improved to deliver value across the enterprise.

Within the APQC Process Classification Framework (hierarchy 10.4.7), this process supports the broader "Acquire, Construct, and Manage Assets" category. Effective execution requires cross-functional collaboration, clear accountability, and robust governance mechanisms. Organizations that mature this process typically see improved operational performance, reduced risk exposure, and stronger alignment between tactical activities and strategic goals.
