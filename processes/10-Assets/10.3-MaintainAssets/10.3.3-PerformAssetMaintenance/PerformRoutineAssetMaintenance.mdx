# Perform routine asset maintenance

> Carrying out required maintenance to continue upkeep of equipment or assets.

## Overview

The perform routine asset maintenance process is a critical component of the Asset Management function within an organization. It encompasses the systematic approach to perform routine asset maintenance, ensuring that all activities are performed consistently, efficiently, and in alignment with organizational objectives. This process establishes the framework through which perform routine asset maintenance is executed, monitored, and continuously improved to deliver value across the enterprise.

Within the APQC Process Classification Framework (hierarchy 10.3.3.2), this activity supports the broader "Acquire, Construct, and Manage Assets" category. Effective execution requires cross-functional collaboration, clear accountability, and robust governance mechanisms. Organizations that mature this process typically see improved operational performance, reduced risk exposure, and stronger alignment between tactical activities and strategic goals.
