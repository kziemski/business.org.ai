# Schedule maintenance work

> Defining a timetable for which to execute the maintenance of the asset.

## Overview

The schedule maintenance work process is a critical component of the Asset Management function within an organization. It encompasses the systematic approach to schedule maintenance work, ensuring that all activities are performed consistently, efficiently, and in alignment with organizational objectives. This process establishes the framework through which schedule maintenance work is executed, monitored, and continuously improved to deliver value across the enterprise.

Within the APQC Process Classification Framework (hierarchy 10.3.2.1), this activity supports the broader "Acquire, Construct, and Manage Assets" category. Effective execution requires cross-functional collaboration, clear accountability, and robust governance mechanisms. Organizations that mature this process typically see improved operational performance, reduced risk exposure, and stronger alignment between tactical activities and strategic goals.
