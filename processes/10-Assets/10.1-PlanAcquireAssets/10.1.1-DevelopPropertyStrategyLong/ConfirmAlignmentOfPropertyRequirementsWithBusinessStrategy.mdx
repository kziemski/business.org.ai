# Confirm alignment of property requirements with business strategy

> Creating alignment between the requirement of properties and the overall business strategy.

## Overview

The confirm alignment of property requirements with business strategy process is a critical component of the Asset Management function within an organization. It encompasses the systematic approach to confirm alignment of property requirements with business strategy, ensuring that all activities are performed consistently, efficiently, and in alignment with organizational objectives. This process establishes the framework through which confirm alignment of property requirements with business strategy is executed, monitored, and continuously improved to deliver value across the enterprise.

Within the APQC Process Classification Framework (hierarchy 10.1.1.1), this activity supports the broader "Acquire, Construct, and Manage Assets" category. Effective execution requires cross-functional collaboration, clear accountability, and robust governance mechanisms. Organizations that mature this process typically see improved operational performance, reduced risk exposure, and stronger alignment between tactical activities and strategic goals.
