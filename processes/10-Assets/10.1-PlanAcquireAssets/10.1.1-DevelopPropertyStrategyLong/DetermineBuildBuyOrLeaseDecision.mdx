# Determine build, buy, or lease decision

> Deciding whether to buy or build properties.

## Overview

The determine build, buy, or lease decision process is a critical component of the Asset Management function within an organization. It encompasses the systematic approach to determine build, buy, or lease decision, ensuring that all activities are performed consistently, efficiently, and in alignment with organizational objectives. This process establishes the framework through which determine build, buy, or lease decision is executed, monitored, and continuously improved to deliver value across the enterprise.

Within the APQC Process Classification Framework (hierarchy 10.1.1.3), this activity supports the broader "Acquire, Construct, and Manage Assets" category. Effective execution requires cross-functional collaboration, clear accountability, and robust governance mechanisms. Organizations that mature this process typically see improved operational performance, reduced risk exposure, and stronger alignment between tactical activities and strategic goals.
