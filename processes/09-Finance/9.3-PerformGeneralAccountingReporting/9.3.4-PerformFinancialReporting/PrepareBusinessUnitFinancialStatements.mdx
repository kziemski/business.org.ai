# Prepare business unit financial statements

> Making reports of subsidiaries units to show profits generated from them.

## Overview

Activity 9.3.4.1 is an activity within the General Accounting & Reporting domain of the Manage Financial Resources framework.

Making reports of subsidiaries units to show profits generated from them. This activity plays a critical role in ensuring that the organization maintains sound financial governance, operational efficiency, and regulatory compliance. It supports upstream planning and downstream execution by providing structured outputs that inform decision-making across finance and business operations. Effective execution of this activity requires coordination among finance professionals, process owners, and leadership stakeholders to ensure accuracy, timeliness, and alignment with organizational objectives.
