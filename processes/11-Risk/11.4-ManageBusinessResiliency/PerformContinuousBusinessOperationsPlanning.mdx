# Perform continuous business operations planning

> Developing plans to ensure continuous business operations.

## Overview

The perform continuous business operations planning process is a critical component of the Enterprise Risk & Compliance function within an organization. It encompasses the systematic approach to perform continuous business operations planning, ensuring that all activities are performed consistently, efficiently, and in alignment with organizational objectives. This process establishes the framework through which perform continuous business operations planning is executed, monitored, and continuously improved to deliver value across the enterprise.

Within the APQC Process Classification Framework (hierarchy 11.4.2), this process supports the broader "Manage Enterprise Risk, Compliance, Remediation, Resiliency" category. Effective execution requires cross-functional collaboration, clear accountability, and robust governance mechanisms. Organizations that mature this process typically see improved operational performance, reduced risk exposure, and stronger alignment between tactical activities and strategic goals.
