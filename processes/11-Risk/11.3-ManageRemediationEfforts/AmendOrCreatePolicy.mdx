# Amend or create policy

> Crafting a new framework of policies and procedures for deploying remediation efforts, or change existing policies and procedures.

## Overview

The amend or create policy process is a critical component of the Enterprise Risk & Compliance function within an organization. It encompasses the systematic approach to amend or create policy, ensuring that all activities are performed consistently, efficiently, and in alignment with organizational objectives. This process establishes the framework through which amend or create policy is executed, monitored, and continuously improved to deliver value across the enterprise.

Within the APQC Process Classification Framework (hierarchy 11.3.6), this process supports the broader "Manage Enterprise Risk, Compliance, Remediation, Resiliency" category. Effective execution requires cross-functional collaboration, clear accountability, and robust governance mechanisms. Organizations that mature this process typically see improved operational performance, reduced risk exposure, and stronger alignment between tactical activities and strategic goals.
