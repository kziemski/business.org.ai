# Ensure risks and risk mitigation actions are monitored

> Ensuring risk monitoring and mitigation activities.

## Overview

The ensure risks and risk mitigation actions are monitored process is a critical component of the Enterprise Risk & Compliance function within an organization. It encompasses the systematic approach to ensure risks and risk mitigation actions are monitored, ensuring that all activities are performed consistently, efficiently, and in alignment with organizational objectives. This process establishes the framework through which ensure risks and risk mitigation actions are monitored is executed, monitored, and continuously improved to deliver value across the enterprise.

Within the APQC Process Classification Framework (hierarchy 11.1.2.5), this activity supports the broader "Manage Enterprise Risk, Compliance, Remediation, Resiliency" category. Effective execution requires cross-functional collaboration, clear accountability, and robust governance mechanisms. Organizations that mature this process typically see improved operational performance, reduced risk exposure, and stronger alignment between tactical activities and strategic goals.
