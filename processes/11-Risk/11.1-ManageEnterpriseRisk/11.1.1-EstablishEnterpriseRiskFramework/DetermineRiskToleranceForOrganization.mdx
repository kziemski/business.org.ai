# Determine risk tolerance for organization

> Recognizing the organization's tolerance for risk, given risk-return trade-offs for one or more anticipated and predictable consequences.

## Overview

The determine risk tolerance for organization process is a critical component of the Enterprise Risk & Compliance function within an organization. It encompasses the systematic approach to determine risk tolerance for organization, ensuring that all activities are performed consistently, efficiently, and in alignment with organizational objectives. This process establishes the framework through which determine risk tolerance for organization is executed, monitored, and continuously improved to deliver value across the enterprise.

Within the APQC Process Classification Framework (hierarchy 11.1.1.1), this activity supports the broader "Manage Enterprise Risk, Compliance, Remediation, Resiliency" category. Effective execution requires cross-functional collaboration, clear accountability, and robust governance mechanisms. Organizations that mature this process typically see improved operational performance, reduced risk exposure, and stronger alignment between tactical activities and strategic goals.
