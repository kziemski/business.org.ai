# Develop enterprise compliance policies and procedures

> Creating a standardized approach to ethics and compliance.

## Overview

The develop enterprise compliance policies and procedures process is a critical component of the Enterprise Risk & Compliance function within an organization. It encompasses the systematic approach to develop enterprise compliance policies and procedures, ensuring that all activities are performed consistently, efficiently, and in alignment with organizational objectives. This process establishes the framework through which develop enterprise compliance policies and procedures is executed, monitored, and continuously improved to deliver value across the enterprise.

Within the APQC Process Classification Framework (hierarchy 11.2.1.1), this activity supports the broader "Manage Enterprise Risk, Compliance, Remediation, Resiliency" category. Effective execution requires cross-functional collaboration, clear accountability, and robust governance mechanisms. Organizations that mature this process typically see improved operational performance, reduced risk exposure, and stronger alignment between tactical activities and strategic goals.
