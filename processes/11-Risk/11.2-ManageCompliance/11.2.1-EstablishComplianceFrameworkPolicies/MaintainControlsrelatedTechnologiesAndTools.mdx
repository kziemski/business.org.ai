# Maintain controls-related technologies and tools

> Managing technologies and tools related to the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data in order to ensure the security of the organization's information.

## Overview

The maintain controls-related technologies and tools process is a critical component of the Enterprise Risk & Compliance function within an organization. It encompasses the systematic approach to maintain controls-related technologies and tools, ensuring that all activities are performed consistently, efficiently, and in alignment with organizational objectives. This process establishes the framework through which maintain controls-related technologies and tools is executed, monitored, and continuously improved to deliver value across the enterprise.

Within the APQC Process Classification Framework (hierarchy 11.2.1.4), this activity supports the broader "Manage Enterprise Risk, Compliance, Remediation, Resiliency" category. Effective execution requires cross-functional collaboration, clear accountability, and robust governance mechanisms. Organizations that mature this process typically see improved operational performance, reduced risk exposure, and stronger alignment between tactical activities and strategic goals.
