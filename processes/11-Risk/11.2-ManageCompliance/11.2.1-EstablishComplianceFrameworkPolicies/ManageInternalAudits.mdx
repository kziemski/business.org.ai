# Manage internal audits

> Managing accounts and prepare regular reports on financial performance.

## Overview

The manage internal audits process is a critical component of the Enterprise Risk & Compliance function within an organization. It encompasses the systematic approach to manage internal audits, ensuring that all activities are performed consistently, efficiently, and in alignment with organizational objectives. This process establishes the framework through which manage internal audits is executed, monitored, and continuously improved to deliver value across the enterprise.

Within the APQC Process Classification Framework (hierarchy 11.2.1.3), this activity supports the broader "Manage Enterprise Risk, Compliance, Remediation, Resiliency" category. Effective execution requires cross-functional collaboration, clear accountability, and robust governance mechanisms. Organizations that mature this process typically see improved operational performance, reduced risk exposure, and stronger alignment between tactical activities and strategic goals.
