## Overview

The compile and communicate compliance scorecard(s) process is a critical component of the Enterprise Risk & Compliance function within an organization. It encompasses the systematic approach to compile and communicate compliance scorecard(s), ensuring that all activities are performed consistently, efficiently, and in alignment with organizational objectives. This process establishes the framework through which compile and communicate compliance scorecard(s) is executed, monitored, and continuously improved to deliver value across the enterprise.

Within the APQC Process Classification Framework (hierarchy 11.2.2.7), this activity supports the broader "Manage Enterprise Risk, Compliance, Remediation, Resiliency" category. Effective execution requires cross-functional collaboration, clear accountability, and robust governance mechanisms. Organizations that mature this process typically see improved operational performance, reduced risk exposure, and stronger alignment between tactical activities and strategic goals.
