# Develop partner/alliance strategy

> Defining direction and plan objectives for partnering with other companies to deliver product/services.

## Overview

Activity 1.2.3.1 is an activity within the Develop Vision and Strategy framework. 

Defining direction and plan objectives for partnering with other companies to deliver product/services. Focus on creating a vision and strategic objectives and culminate in creating measures for the strategic alliance or partnership.

This process plays a critical role within the broader "Develop Vision and Strategy" capability area (APQC Category 1.0). By systematically executing this activity, organizations ensure that strategic decisions are grounded in thorough analysis and aligned with overall business objectives. The outputs of this process feed into downstream strategy development and execution activities, creating a foundation for informed decision-making across the enterprise.
