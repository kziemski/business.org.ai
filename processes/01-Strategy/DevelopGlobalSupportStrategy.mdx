# Develop global support strategy

> Developing a plan to deploy support services and support functions throughout the organization globally.

## Overview

Activity 1.2.3.5 is an activity within the Develop Vision and Strategy framework. 

Developing a plan to deploy support services and support functions throughout the organization globally. Arrange the organizations functional support areas to create efficiencies of scale in the delivery of support services, globally.

This process plays a critical role within the broader "Develop Vision and Strategy" capability area (APQC Category 1.0). By systematically executing this activity, organizations ensure that strategic decisions are grounded in thorough analysis and aligned with overall business objectives. The outputs of this process feed into downstream strategy development and execution activities, creating a foundation for informed decision-making across the enterprise.
