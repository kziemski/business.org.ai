# Buffets

> This U.

## Overview

Buffets represents a specialized segment within the Accommodation and Food Services sector (NAICS 72).

This U.S. industry comprises establishments, known as cafeterias, grill buffets, or buffets, primarily engaged in preparing and serving meals for immediate consumption using cafeteria-style or buffet serving equipment, such as steam tables, refrigerated areas, display grills, and self-service nonalcoholic beverage dispensing equipment.
