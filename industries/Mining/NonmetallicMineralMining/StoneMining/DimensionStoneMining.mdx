# Dimension Stone Mining

> This U.

## Overview

Dimension Stone Mining represents a specialized segment within the Mining, Quarrying, and Oil and Gas Extraction sector (NAICS 21).

This U.S. industry comprises establishments primarily engaged in developing the mine site and/or mining or quarrying dimension stone (i.e., rough blocks and/or slabs of stone). Cross-References.
