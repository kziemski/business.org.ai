# Garden Equipment Repair

> This U.S.

## Overview

Garden Equipment Repair represents a specialized segment within the Other Services (except Public Administration) sector (NAICS 81). This national industry encompasses establishments primarily engaged in garden equipment repair.

This U.S. industry comprises establishments primarily engaged in repairing and servicing home and garden equipment without retailing new home and garden equipment, such as lawnmowers, handheld power tools, edgers, snowblowers, leaf blowers, and trimmers. Cross-References.
