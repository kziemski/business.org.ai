# Unlaminated Plastics Profile Shape Manufacturing

> This U.S.

## Overview

Unlaminated Plastics Profile Shape Manufacturing represents a specialized segment within the U.S. Manufacturing sector (NAICS 31-33). This national industry encompasses establishments primarily engaged in unlaminated plastics profile shape manufacturing.

This U.S. industry comprises establishments primarily engaged in converting plastics resins into nonrigid plastics profile shapes (except film, sheet, bags, and hoses), such as rod, tube, and sausage casings. Cross-References. Establishments primarily engaged in--
