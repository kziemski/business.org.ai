# Soft Drink

> This industry comprises establishments primarily engaged in one or more of the following: (1) manufacturing soft drinks; (2) manufacturing ice; and (3) purifying and bottling water.

## Overview

Soft Drink represents an important category within the U.S. Manufacturing sector (NAICS 31-33). This industry encompasses establishments primarily engaged in soft drink.
