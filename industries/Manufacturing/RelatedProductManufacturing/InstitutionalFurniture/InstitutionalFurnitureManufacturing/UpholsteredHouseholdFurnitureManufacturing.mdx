> This U.S. industry comprises establishments primarily engaged in manufacturing upholstered household-type furniture.

Upholstered Household Furniture Manufacturing represents a specialized segment within the U.S. Manufacturing sector (NAICS 31-33). This national industry encompasses establishments primarily engaged in upholstered household furniture manufacturing.
