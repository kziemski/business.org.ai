# Pharmacy Benefit Management

> This U.

## Overview

Pharmacy Benefit Management represents a specialized segment within the Finance and Insurance sector (NAICS 52).

This U.S. industry comprises establishments primarily engaged in providing pharmacy benefit management (PBM) services and other third party administration services of insurance and pension funds, such as claims processing and other administrative services to insurance carriers, employee benefit plans, and self-insurance funds.
