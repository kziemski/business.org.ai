# Consumer Lending

> This U.

## Overview

Consumer Lending represents a specialized segment within the Finance and Insurance sector (NAICS 52).

This U.S. industry comprises establishments primarily engaged in making unsecured cash loans to consumers. Illustrative Examples: Finance companies (i.e., unsecured cash loans) Personal credit institutions (i.e., unsecured cash loans) Loan companies (i.e., consumer, personal, student, small) Student loan companies Cross-References.
