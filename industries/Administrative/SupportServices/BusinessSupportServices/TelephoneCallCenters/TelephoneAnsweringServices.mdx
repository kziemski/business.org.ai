# Telephone Answering Services

> This U.S.

## Overview

Telephone Answering Services represents a specialized segment within the Administrative and Support and Waste Management and Remediation Services sector (NAICS 56). This national industry encompasses establishments primarily engaged in telephone answering services.

This U.S. industry comprises establishments primarily engaged in answering telephone calls and relaying messages on behalf of clients or for other establishments of the same enterprise. Cross-References.
