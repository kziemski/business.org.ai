# Stenotype Services

> This U.

## Overview

Stenotype Services represents a specialized segment within the Administrative and Support and Waste Management and Remediation Services sector (NAICS 56).

This U.S. industry comprises establishments primarily engaged in providing verbatim reporting and stenotype recording of live legal proceedings and transcribing subsequent recorded materials. Illustrative Examples: Real-time (i.e., simultaneous) closed captioning of live television performances, meetings, conferences Court reporting or stenotype recording services Public stenography services Cross-References.
