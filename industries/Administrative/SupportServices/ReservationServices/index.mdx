# Reservation Services

> This industry group comprises establishments primarily engaged in one of the following: (1) travel agency services; (2) arranging and assembling tours; or (3) other travel arrangement and reservation services.

## Overview

Reservation Services represents an important category within the Administrative and Support and Waste Management and Remediation Services sector (NAICS 56). This industry group encompasses establishments primarily engaged in reservation services.
