# Solid Waste Landfill

> This U.

## Overview

Solid Waste Landfill represents a specialized segment within the Administrative and Support and Waste Management and Remediation Services sector (NAICS 56).

This U.S. industry comprises establishments primarily engaged in (1) operating landfills for the disposal of nonhazardous solid waste or (2) the combined activity of collecting and/or hauling nonhazardous waste materials within a local area and operating landfills for the disposal of nonhazardous solid waste.
