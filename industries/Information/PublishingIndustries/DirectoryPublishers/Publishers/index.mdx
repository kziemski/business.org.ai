# Publishers

> This industry comprises establishments known as publishers (except newspaper, magazine, book, directory, mailing list, music, and software publishers).

## Overview

Publishers represents an important category within the Information sector (NAICS 51). This industry encompasses establishments primarily engaged in publishers.

This industry comprises establishments known as publishers (except newspaper, magazine, book, directory, mailing list, music, and software publishers). These establishments may publish works in print or electronic form, including exclusively on the Internet. Illustrative Examples: Art publishers Greeting card publishers Calendar publishers Cross-References.
