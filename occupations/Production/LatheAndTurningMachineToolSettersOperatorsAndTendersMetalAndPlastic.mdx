# Lathe and Turning Machine Tool Setters, Operators, and Tenders, Metal and Plastic

> Set up, operate, or tend lathe and turning machines to turn, bore, thread, form, or face metal or plastic materials, such as wire, rod, or bar stock.

## Overview

Lathe and Turning Machine Tool Setters, Operators, and Tenders, Metal and Plastic professionals set up, operate, or tend lathe and turning machines to turn, bore, thread, form, or face metal or plastic materials, such as wire, rod, or bar stock..
