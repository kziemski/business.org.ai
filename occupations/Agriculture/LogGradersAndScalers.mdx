# Log Graders and Scalers

> Grade logs or estimate the marketable content or value of logs or pulpwood in sorting yards, millpond, log deck, or similar locations. Inspect logs for defects or measure logs to determine volume.

## Overview

Log Graders and Scalers professionals grade logs or estimate the marketable content or value of logs or pulpwood in sorting yards, millpond, log deck, or similar locations. This occupation falls within the Farming, Fishing, and Forestry category and requires a combination of specialized knowledge, technical skills, and practical experience.
